## Description

When a commuter rail bridge sheds a critical bolt, materials auditor Sera Kade is handed a ledger proving the structure was repaired years ago. The bridge itself disagrees.

The installed bolts are old, brittle, and magnetically wrong. The invoices point to ferrite steel that never reached the piers, while a contractor, a transit treasurer, and a frightened inspector all have reasons to keep the repair history vague.
