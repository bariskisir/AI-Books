# The Ferrite Ledger - Outline

## Premise

After a commuter rail bridge is nearly condemned, materials auditor Sera Kade finds a ledger claiming the line received new ferrite steel bolts ten years earlier. The bolts in the bridge are old, brittle, and magnetic in the wrong way. To prevent a quiet infrastructure failure from becoming a public disaster, Sera follows metallurgical tests, rail-yard invoices, inspection photographs, and one missing maintenance account through a city agency that has learned to hide corruption inside routine repair.

## Core Cast

- Sera Kade - materials auditor specializing in rail infrastructure.
- Malik Orin - bridge engineer who first doubts the repair records.
- Dana Thorne - transit authority treasurer guarding the ledger.
- Ivo Pike - scrap dealer whose yard holds the missing bolts.
- Celeste Rusk - metallurgist who reads fracture surfaces.
- Reuben Shaw - union inspector blamed for the false sign-off.
- Clara Voss - city council investigator.
- Bren Vale - contractor who profited from phantom steel.
