# The Weight of Silence

**Author:** Barış Kısır
**Genre:** Psychological Thriller
**Model:** sonnet-4.6
**Chapters:** 50

## Synopsis

Dr. Iris Vane is brought in to evaluate Jamie Dowd, a sixteen-year-old boy who has not spoken since witnessing his mother's murder six weeks ago. The police need his testimony, the court has ordered an assessment, and Jamie — calm, attentive, profoundly strategic — sits across from Iris in the evaluation room and says nothing.

Over weeks of sessions, using drawings, sand trays, and close observation, Iris begins to understand that Jamie's silence is not the absence of communication but its most precise form: he knows exactly who killed his mother, and he has decided that naming the killer is more dangerous than silence.

A slow-burn psychological thriller about the uses of silence, the ethics of trust, and the intelligence required to survive when the system meant to protect you is compromised.
