# The Riverboat Codex — 50-Chapter Outline

**Genre:** Mystery
**Setting:** Contemporary Baton Rouge and New Orleans, Louisiana — riverboat casinos, French Quarter antique shops, Louisiana State Archive, Mississippi River wharf. Flashbacks to Prohibition-era 1920s New Orleans gambling underworld.

## Core Cast
- **Dr. Lila Vance** — Cryptanalyst, disgraced mathematics professor, works part-time for a Baton Rouge PI firm
- **Roland "Rollie" Devereaux** — Octogenarian former riverboat dealer, discovered poker chips in mentor's estate
- **Detective Marcus Thibodeau** — NOPD cold case investigator, 20 years on the force
- **Simone Bouchard** — Casino surveillance director at Belle Fleur, antagonist with layered motives
- **Felix Beaumont** — Deceased riverboat dealer (mentor to Rollie), held secrets of the Codex

## Chapter Breakdown

**Part I: The Discovery (Chapters 1-10)**
- Ch1: The Safety Deposit Box — Rollie discovers seven poker chips with strange markings in Felix Beaumont's safety deposit box
- Ch2: The Invitation — Rollie contacts Vance & Associates Investigations; Lila takes the case reluctantly
- Ch3: A Deck of Lies — Lila meets Rollie and examines the chips; they discuss the legend of the Riverboat Codex
- Ch4: The Seventh Chip — Lila identifies a cipher pattern on the chips; realizes one chip is different from the others
- Ch5: The 1993 File — Marcus Thibodeau investigates an old murder at Belle Fleur casino that may connect to the chips
- Ch6: The French Quarter Dealer — Lila visits an antique gaming dealer who knew Felix; learns about the 1920s gambling scene
- Ch7: The Break-In — Lila's apartment is burglarized; only her notes on the chips are taken
- Ch8: The Plaster Impressions — Rollie reveals he made plaster casts of the chip surfaces years ago
- Ch9: The Archivist — Lila meets Elise Delacroix at the Louisiana State Archive; research into prohibition-era records
- Ch10: Felix's Notebook — Rollie provides Felix's personal notebook filled with cryptic references

**Part II: The Players (Chapters 11-20)**
- Ch11: The House Line — Lila receives an anonymous threat; "The House" enters the game
- Ch12: The Riverboat Invitation — Simone Bouchard invites Lila to the Belle Fleur for a private consultation
- Ch13: The Green Felt — Lila tours the Belle Fleur, observes the operations, meets Simone
- Ch14: The First Translation — Lila cracks the first layer of the poker chip cipher using combinatorial mathematics
- Ch15: The Painted Pillar — A clue from Felix's notebook leads Lila to a hidden message in the French Quarter
- Ch16: Simone's Proposal — Simone offers Lila resources and access in exchange for information about the chips
- Ch17: The Collector — A shadowy antique collector named Tommy Vitale surfaces, wanting to buy the chips
- Ch18: Tommy's Story — Tommy reveals he was at the Belle Fleur in 1993 the night of the murder
- Ch19: The Cold Case Connects — Marcus establishes a direct link between the 1993 murder and the chips
- Ch20: Felix's Last Deal — Rollie recounts the last time he saw Felix alive, and the secret Felix confessed

**Part III: The Code (Chapters 21-30)**
- Ch21: The Second Layer — Lila discovers a second cipher embedded in the chip material itself
- Ch22: The Warehouse — Tommy leads Lila and Marcus to a warehouse Felix owned; they find a hidden compartment
- Ch23: The Codex Fragment — Inside the compartment, a single damaged page from a larger ledger — the Codex
- Ch24: The House Closes In — Simone's security team surveils Lila; the threats escalate
- Ch25: The Gambler's Alphabet — Lila decodes the fragment using a Prohibition-era gambler's argot cipher
- Ch26: The Last Survivor — Research reveals one person from the 1920s gambling ring is still alive
- Ch27: Henri's Memory — Henri Lambert, 104, remembers the heyday of illegal gambling in New Orleans
- Ch28: The Nursing Home Visit — Lila and Marcus interview Henri; he names names and describes the Codex's origin
- Ch29: The Bridge — Henri reveals the Codex was hidden under the Old Huey P. Long Bridge during construction
- Ch30: Simone's Betrayal — Lila discovers Simone has been feeding information to the casino's parent company trust

**Part IV: The Hunt (Chapters 31-40)**
- Ch31: The Night Dive — Lila and Marcus organize a covert dive to search under the bridge
- Ch32: The Drying Process — A deteriorated safe is recovered; documents must be carefully dried and preserved
- Ch33: The Names — The drying reveals names of politicians, police captains, and judges who took bribes
- Ch34: The House Revealed — "The House" is identified as a trust controlled by the Bouchard family since 1928
- Ch35: The Trustee — Simone's father, Antoine Bouchard, is the current trustee of the Bouchard Trust
- Ch36: The Confrontation — Lila confronts Simone with the evidence; Simone admits working for her father
- Ch37: The Moral Question — Lila debates whether to publish the Codex's names or let the dead bury the dead
- Ch38: Simone's Truth — Simone reveals her father is dying and she was trying to protect his legacy
- Ch39: The Missing Chip — Lila realizes the eighth chip holds the key to the complete Codex
- Ch40: Rollie's Confession — Rollie admits he has hidden the eighth chip for thirty years out of fear

**Part V: The Reckoning (Chapters 41-50)**
- Ch41: The Master Translation — With all eight chips, Lila completes the full cipher translation
- Ch42: The Live Player — The translation reveals a currently living politician whose father was named in the Codex
- Ch43: The Choice — Lila must decide: expose the living or protect the innocent descendants
- Ch44: The Leak — Someone at the archive leaks news of the discovery; the story hits local media
- Ch45: The Firestorm — Public reaction, political pressure, the casino's lawyers mobilize
- Ch46: The Arson — The State Archive suffers a suspicious fire; Elise barely escapes
- Ch47: The Archive — Lila finds the Codex pages were saved by Elise's quick thinking; the arsonist is caught
- Ch48: The Trial — Marcus presents the 1993 murder case; the Codex becomes key evidence
- Ch49: The Verdict — Justice for the 1993 murder; the Codex is entered into the public record
- Ch50: The Last Hand — Lila walks away from the PI firm, accepting a university research position; Rollie finds peace; Simone faces her choices
