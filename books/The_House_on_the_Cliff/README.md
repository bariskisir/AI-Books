# The House on the Cliff

**Genre:** Domestic Suspense
**Author:** Barış Kısır
**Status:** Complete (TXT + EPUB)

## Synopsis

The Harrison family moves into their dream home: a stunning glass-walled house on the California cliffs. But the house was built by Elias Voss, a charismatic cult leader who buried something beneath the foundation. When Kate discovers old blueprints showing a sealed chamber, she begins to unravel a fifty-five-year-old murder. Her husband Mark insists she let it go. The house itself seems to push back. As Kate digs deeper, she must confront the buried truth before the house claims another victim.

## Characters

- **Kate Harrison** — 38, former investigative journalist, mother of two. She discovers the chamber beneath the house and pursues justice against her husband's wishes.
- **Mark Harrison** — 42, tech executive, Kate's husband. Image-conscious and resistant to anything that threatens their perfect life.
- **Ezra Vance** — 87, the architect who designed the house for cult leader Elias Voss. He provides Kate with the key to the sealed chamber.
- **Elias Voss** — Deceased cult leader who buried his first disciple alive beneath the foundation.
- **Miriam Cross** — Voss's victim, buried alive in 1969. Her remains are discovered by Kate.
