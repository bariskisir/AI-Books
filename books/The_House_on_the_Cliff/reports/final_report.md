## Plot Summary

The Harrison family moves into their dream home on the California cliffs. Kate discovers the house was commissioned by cult leader Elias Voss, who buried his first disciple Miriam Cross alive beneath the foundation. As Kate investigates, her husband Mark opposes her, leading to marital breakdown. With the help of the dying architect Ezra Vance, Kate uncovers the truth. The house is demolished, Miriam is given a proper burial, and Kate finds closure.
