# The Tidal Pharmacy - Outline

## Premise

On the island chain of Vey Sound, pharmacist Dr. Leda Quill audits medicine failures after insulin shipments spoil, antibiotics vanish, and tide schedules appear in pharmacy inventory logs. A mainland distributor blames weather and island incompetence, but Leda finds saltwater in cold-chain crates, forged batch temperatures, and ferry manifests altered to favor a private clinic resort. With a ferry captain, a nurse, a refrigeration mechanic, and a harbor clerk, she follows tide tables, vial crystals, seal numbers, and hidden refrigeration routes to prove lifesaving medicines were diverted through the tides.

## Core Cast

- **Dr. Leda Quill** - Island pharmacist and cold-chain auditor, exacting and protective of patients.
- **Mara Senn** - Clinic nurse tracking medicine failures across the smaller islands.
- **Jonas Pike** - Ferry captain whose tide logs contradict the distributor's manifests.
- **Calder Vale** - Executive for Northstar Medical Logistics.
- **Iris Noll** - Harbor clerk who keeps handwritten tide and cargo books.
- **Talia Reed** - Refrigeration mechanic who reads compressor scars like witness statements.
- **Bram Hesk** - Retired fisherman who saw medicine crates moved at night.
- **Dr. Olan Voss** - Director of a private clinic resort with too-perfect supply.
- **Nadia Marr** - Diabetic schoolteacher whose spoiled insulin begins the audit.
- **Judge Elian Arda** - Emergency health magistrate.
