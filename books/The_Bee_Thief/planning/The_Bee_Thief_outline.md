# The Bee Thief — Outline

## Premise

Dr. Maya Lane, an entomologist whose career was destroyed by a fabricated-data scandal she did not cause, returns to her small Oklahoma hometown after her father's death. She finds the apiaries that once dotted the prairie are silent—hives emptied, bees dead by the thousands. Local beekeepers blame a mysterious affliction. Sheriff Wade Cobb, an old classmate, is out of his depth. As Maya investigates, she traces the die-off to a new pesticide being applied to neighboring canola fields—a product developed by Redland Agrochemicals, the company that ruined her career. The deeper she digs, the more dangerous the truth becomes. The Bee Thief is not a person. It is a system.

## Core Cast

- **Dr. Maya Lane** — 38, entomologist, former PhD researcher at Oklahoma State. Her reputation was destroyed when her advisor, Dr. Paulson, was found to have fabricated data on a grant-funded study. Maya was cleared but never recovered. She has worked extension jobs and small consulting gigs for a decade. Smart, stubborn, carrying quiet rage.
- **Sheriff Wade Cobb** — 42, Lincoln County sheriff. Played football with Maya's older brother in high school. He never left Blackwell, Oklahoma. He believes in order and hates complications. Maya's return is one. He will have to choose between his job and what is right.
- **Royce Redland** — 55, owner of Redland Agrochemicals. Third-generation agri-business. He presents as a friendly Oklahoma businessman. He is not.
- **Nell Porter** — 68, longtime beekeeper, Maya's mentor from childhood. First to notice the bees dying. She is the town's moral compass.
- **Dr. Henry Paulson** — 70, Maya's former advisor at OSU. Now retired and sick. He holds the key to the connection between Maya's past and the current conspiracy.
- **Dale Pritchard** — 45, Redland's head of regional operations. A local man who does the dirty work. He is the one who applies the chemicals.
- **Lorna Lane** — 62, Maya's mother, still living in the family home on the outskirts of Blackwell. She has emphysema and does not ask questions.

## 50 Chapter Plan

### Act I: The Return (Chapters 1–12)

1. The Call — Maya receives word of her father's death in a Boston hotel room, her life already in pieces.
2. Highway 177 — Maya drives south through Oklahoma, passing dead canola fields and empty apiaries.
3. The Family Home — Her mother Lorna waits on the porch. The house smells of dust and medicine.
4. The Funeral — A sparse gathering. Sheriff Cobb offers awkward condolences.
5. The Apiary — Maya visits her father's abandoned bee yard. Every hive is empty.
6. Nell Porter — Maya's old mentor appears at the back fence. She has something to show her.
7. The Dead Hive — Nell pries open a sealed hive. Thousands of bees lie in a perfect heap at the bottom.
8. The Sample — Maya collects tissue samples. She still knows how to do this work.
9. The Lab — Maya drives to Enid to use a university lab. The technician is an old colleague.
10. The Results — Preliminary tests show a neurotoxin. Not a disease. Poison.
11. The Sheriff's Office — Sheriff Cobb tells Maya to stay out of it. Redland pays taxes.
12. The Threat — A truck follows Maya home. Dale Pritchard leaves a message on her windshield.

### Act II: The Dig (Chapters 13–25)

13. The Drift Study — Maya maps the bee death pattern. It radiates from Redland's newest fields.
14. The Spray Schedule — Maya finds public records of aerial spraying permits. The timing matches.
15. Nell's Story — Nell tells Maya about the meeting where Redland warned beekeepers to move or lose everything.
16. The Anonymous Call — A former Redland employee calls Maya at midnight. He sounds scared.
17. The Meet — Maya meets the whistleblower at a truck stop outside Ponca City.
18. The Documents — He gives her internal Redland memos about "crop-compatible pollinator management."
19. The Fire — Nell's shed burns down that night. The sheriff calls it an accident.
20. The Hospital — Nell is hospitalized with smoke inhalation. Maya sits vigil.
21. The Old Photos — Maya finds her father's journals. He had been tracking the bee deaths for two years.
22. The Map — Her father had drawn a map of Redland's spray zones overlapping every dead apiary in the county.
23. The Threat Made Plain — Sheriff Cobb shows up at the hospital. He tells Maya Dale Pritchard has an alibi.
24. The Pressure — Maya's mother asks her to stop. People are talking. The town is turning.
25. The Cracks — A county commissioner quietly offers Maya old county health inspection reports.

### Act III: The Conspiracy (Chapters 26–37)

26. The Connection — Maya realizes the chemical is not approved for use on canola. It is being used illegally.
27. The Search — Maya digs into Redland Agrochemicals' history. Finds the same pattern in three other counties.
28. The Old Professor — Maya drives to Stillwater to see Dr. Paulson, her disgraced former advisor.
29. The Confession — Paulson admits Redland paid him to fabricate the safety data that Maya was blamed for.
30. The Bigger Picture — The chemical, NF-7, was fast-tracked through EPA loopholes. Redland bribed inspectors.
31. The Warning — Maya gets a call from the whistleblower's wife. He has gone missing.
32. The Search — Maya and Nell's son search for the missing man. They find his truck at the bottom of a ravine.
33. The Cover-Up — Sheriff Cobb rules it an accident despite Maya's protests. She sees the fear in his eyes.
34. The Evidence Box — Maya copies everything: the documents, the map, the lab results, Paulson's recorded confession.
35. The Tail — Maya realizes she is being followed. Two men in a black pickup.
36. The Confrontation — Royce Redland approaches Maya at the diner. He offers her a job and a settlement.
37. The Refusal — Maya walks away. She knows she is running out of time.

### Act IV: The Reckoning (Chapters 38–50)

38. The Plan — Maya decides to take the evidence to the state attorney general in Oklahoma City.
39. The Road Block — Her car is sabotaged. The brake line is cut.
40. The Rescue — Sheriff Cobb finds her on the side of the road. He has to make a choice.
41. The Choice — Cobb admits he knew about Redland's operations. His brother-in-law works for the company.
42. The Alliance — Cobb agrees to help Maya. He cannot live with the silence anymore.
43. The Raid — Cobb and Maya plan to seize the spray records from Redland's headquarters before they can be destroyed.
44. The Break-In — They enter at night. Cobb uses his badge to get past the night guard.
45. The Safe — The records are in a floor safe. Maya has to crack it using a combination from her father's journals.
46. The Alarm — Dale Pritchard arrives with two men. A standoff.
47. The Standoff — Cobb draws his weapon. Pritchard backs down. They escape with the files.
48. The Press Conference — Maya and Cobb present the evidence at the county courthouse. The state AG is present.
49. The Arrest — Royce Redland is taken into custody. The town watches in silence.
50. The Hive — Maya stands at her father's apiary. A single bee lands on her hand. She starts again.
