# The Bee Thief

**Genre:** Rural Noir
**Author:** Barış Kısır
**Status:** Complete (txt + epub)

## Description

When hives start dying across Oklahoma, a disgraced entomologist returns to her hometown and uncovers a pesticide conspiracy.

Dr. Maya Lane's career was destroyed by a fabricated-data scandal she did not cause. Now she returns to her small Oklahoma hometown after her father's death and finds the apiaries silent — hives emptied, bees dead by the thousands. Local beekeepers blame a mysterious affliction, but Maya knows the pattern.

Tracing the die-off to a new pesticide being sprayed on neighboring canola fields, Maya discovers the compound is experimental — unapproved for agricultural use and forty times more toxic to bees than anything legally permitted. The chemical was developed by Redland Agrochemicals, the same company that ruined her reputation years ago.

As Maya digs deeper, the threats escalate. A whistleblower vanishes. A mentor's shed burns. Sheriff Wade Cobb, an old classmate, must choose between his job and the truth. Maya risks everything to expose a conspiracy that stretches from the Oklahoma plains to the highest levels of agribusiness.
