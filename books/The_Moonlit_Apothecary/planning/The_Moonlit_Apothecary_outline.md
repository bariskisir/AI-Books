# The Moonlit Apothecary Outline

## Premise

In a border town famous for healing waters, apothecary Elian Voss inherits a locked moon garden and a ledger of night-blooming plants. When patients recover from impossible ailments and then die in precise lunar intervals, Elian joins a skeptical physician and a disgraced constable to trace a poison through family debt, civic pride, and a charitable hospital built over an older crime.

## Core Cast

- **Elian Voss** - a careful apothecary who trusts plants more than public men.
- **Dr. Mara Celyn** - a practical physician whose clinic receives the first impossible cures.
- **Inspector Rowan Vale** - a dismissed constable trying to prove his last case was not madness.
- **Sister Adela Pryce** - the hospital matron who knows which records were burned.
- **Ivor March** - a hotel owner whose fortune depends on the town's reputation for health.
