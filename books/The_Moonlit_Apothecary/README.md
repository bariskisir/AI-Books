# The Moonlit Apothecary

## Overview

In a border town famous for healing waters, apothecary Elian Voss inherits a locked moon garden and a ledger of night-blooming plants. When patients recover from impossible ailments and then die in precise lunar intervals, Elian joins a skeptical physician and a disgraced constable to trace a poison through family debt, civic pride, and a charitable hospital built over an older crime.
