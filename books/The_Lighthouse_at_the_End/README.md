# The Lighthouse at the End

**Genre:** Psychological Horror  
**Author:** Barış Kısır  
**Status:** Complete (TXT + EPUB available)

## About

Daniel Rourke accepts a six-month posting as lighthouse keeper at Cape Ash, the most remote station on the coast. He arrives to find the lighthouse operating perfectly, the kettle warm, the bed made -- but the previous keeper is nowhere to be found. In the cistern room, Daniel discovers the body of Edwin Marsh, dead for over two months.

Marsh's diary documents a slow unraveling: the hum in the tower, the light that changes color at 2:47 AM, the city beneath the waves. As Daniel reads, the same phenomena begin to claim him. The lighthouse is not a machine. It is a living threshold. And every keeper eventually steps through.
