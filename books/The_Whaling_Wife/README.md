# The Whaling Wife

**Genre:** Nautical Historical  
**Author:** Barış Kısır  
**Status:** Complete (txt + epub)

## Description

In 1845, Sarah Beckett's husband Thomas vanished on the whaling ship *Mary Rose*. The shipping company says he deserted. A cryptic letter from Thomas suggests otherwise.

Sarah cuts her hair, binds her chest, and signs onto the same ship as "Samuel Beckett" — a green hand seeking work. Under the watchful eye of harpooner Eben Cole, she learns the brutal trade of whaling while hunting for the truth about her husband's disappearance.

But Captain Nye harbors a dark secret, and when discovery comes, Sarah must fight not only for justice but for her life.
