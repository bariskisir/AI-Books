## Story Summary

Sarah Beckett disguises herself as a man to join a whaling voyage on the *Mary Rose*, the same ship her husband Thomas disappeared from a year earlier. She discovers Captain Nye murdered Thomas for uncovering fraud. With harpooner Eben Cole's help, she gathers proof and exposes the truth. A hurricane sinks the ship. Sarah and Cole survive in an open boat, are rescued, and return to Nantucket where Sarah establishes a fund for whalers' widows.
