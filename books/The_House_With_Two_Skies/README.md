# The House With Two Skies

**Author:** Barış Kısır
**Genre:** Literary Fiction
**Subgenre:** Parallel Lives / Speculative
**Model:** sonnet-4.6
**Chapters:** 50

## Synopsis

Clearing her late mother's remote Welsh farmhouse, a grieving translator discovers that the back-bedroom window opens, on still mornings, into a world where her mother is still alive and a different version of her own life was lived — and that lingering there is quietly costing her the daughter she still has.

A week after her mother's funeral, Eluned Pryce returns to the remote hill farm in Powys where she grew up, to empty it for sale. The farmhouse stands on an old geological fault the locals never named aloud, and from the two small windows of the back bedroom — the still room — on certain windless mornings, you can see two different skies. Through the second pane the orchard is kept, the kitchen lamp is lit, and her mother is alive, making tea.
