# The Rust Colossus

**Genre:** Dieselpunk / Alternate History War Fiction  
**Author:** Barış Kısır  
**Model:** deepseek-v4-flash  
**Status:** Complete

## Description

The Great War never ended. Across a poisoned continent, armies fight from massive armored trains crawling through the Ash Sea — a wasteland of chemical fog, rusting wreckage, and permanent twilight. The Rust Colossus is one such train, a battered warhorse held together by the genius of its chief engineer and the stubborn will of its captain.

When Anya Petrova discovers hidden schematics for a resonance engine — a weapon capable of leveling cities with seismic waves — she and Captain Elias Kovac face an impossible choice: deliver the weapon to their corrupt commanders and prolong a war that has already consumed a generation, or destroy the plans and become fugitives hunted by both sides.

## Characters

- **Elias Kovac** — Scarred veteran captain of the Rust Colossus
- **Anya Petrova** — Chief engineer who discovers the hidden weapon schematics
- **Mason Dryden** — Navigator with a guilty past
- **Sergeant Vera Harlow** — Gunnery chief seeking justice
- **Dr. Julian Cross** — Captured physicist who helped design the Resonance Engine
- **General Mikhail Rostov** — The corrupt commander behind the weapon
