# The Palermo Exchange — Final Report

## Summary

The Palermo Exchange is a 50-chapter crime thriller novel set in Palermo, Sicily. It follows Luca Ferrara, a money launderer for the Vitale mafia family, who loses two million euros in cash during a routine handoff and is given 72 hours to recover it or face execution. The novel traces his desperate search through Palermo's underworld, the parallel investigation by Inspector Chiara Rosi, and the discovery that the theft was an inside job.

## Characters

- Luca Ferrara — Money launderer, protagonist
- Don Matteo Vitale — Mafia boss
- Inspector Chiara Rosi — Postal Police investigator
- Ferruccio — Vitale's driver, antagonist
- Bruno Mancuso — Street-level thief
- Cataldo — Naples broker
- Scarpuzza, Rocco, Amato — Supporting characters
