# Silver and Bone — Final Report

## Summary

Silver and Bone is a complete urban fantasy novel set in Chicago. The story follows Mira Black, a private detective with the ability to see through fae glamour, as she uncovers a hidden war between the Summer Court and the Autumn Court that threatens to spill into the mortal world.

## What Was Created

- **Manuscript**: 50 chapters, 10,741 words
- **Outline**: 50-chapter plan with premise and cast
- **Cover**: 1600x2560 PNG with rainy Chicago street, glowing portal, detective silhouette
- **EPUB**: EPUB 3 format with all 50 chapters, cover image, and navigation
- **Metadata**: Full JSON metadata for production pipeline
