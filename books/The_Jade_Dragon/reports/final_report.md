## Summary

The Jade Dragon is a complete Wuxia Fantasy novel of approximately 11,076 words across 50 chapters. The story follows Master Liang, a disgraced martial arts master who must master the forbidden Fifth Style of the Jade Dragon to defeat Warlord Kang and the demon that empowers him. His disciple Mei serves as both student and conscience, anchoring him to his humanity as the dragon's power threatens to consume him.
