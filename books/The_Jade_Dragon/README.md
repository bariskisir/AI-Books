# The Jade Dragon

A Wuxia Fantasy novel by Barış Kısır.

## Premise

Master Liang, once the most celebrated martial artist in the Jade Kingdom, was disgraced and exiled after a student died during his instruction. Now Warlord Kang -- a man who sealed a pact with a demon called the Emperor of Ashen Thorns -- marches across the land with an army of shadow-bent warriors. To stop Kang, Liang must master the forbidden Fifth Style of the Jade Dragon, a technique that has killed every practitioner who attempted it. His sole remaining disciple, Mei, struggles to keep him anchored to his humanity as the style demands he surrender it.

## Chapters

50 chapters across five parts: The Exile, The Forbidden Style, The War Approaches, The Dragon Rises, and The Final Reckoning.
