# The Glass Shoe — Outline

## Premise

In the rain-slicked city of Aureliana, a masked ball ends with a dead industrialist and a single glass slipper left behind. Detective Ella Marlowe, a sharp-witted investigator with a past she keeps locked in a filing cabinet, is hired by the enigmatic Prince Valentine to find the slipper's owner. But the case twists through the city's gilded elite, crooked cops, and a stepmother whose secrets are worth killing for. What begins as a missing-object case becomes a murder investigation that forces Ella to choose between justice and the truth about her own family.

## Core Cast

- **Detective Ella Marlowe** — A private investigator in Aureliana. Sharp, guarded, running from her family name.
- **Prince Valentine** — The city's heir apparent. Charming, manipulative, hides a steel will behind a velvet smile.
- **Stepmother DeVane** — Widow of the murdered industrialist. Polished, ruthless, mourning in designer black.
- **Anastasia DeVane** — The stepdaughter. Quiet. Watches everything. Knows more than she says.
- **Lieutenant Corrigan** — Homicide detective. Old school. Owes Ella a favor.
- **Theo Vance** — Valentines fixer. A man who cleans up messes, including bodies.

## 50 Chapter Plan

1. Ella Marlowe takes a case from Prince Valentine at dawn in a diner.
2. Ella examines the glass slipper in her office — it is absurdly delicate, impossible to walk in.
3. She visits the ballroom crime scene; the staff have been paid to forget.
4. Stepmother DeVane receives Ella in a penthouse full of sharp angles.
5. Anastasia DeVane slips Ella a note in the hallway: "She lies."
6. Ella learns the dead man, Roland DeVane, died of a fall — or was pushed.
7. Lieutenant Corrigan shares the police file: the fall was ruled accidental.
8. Ella tracks down the ballroom's coat-check girl who saw something.
9. The girl describes a woman in silver arguing with Roland before the fall.
10. Ella visits the DeVane family cobbler; the slipper was custom-made.
11. The cobbler's ledger shows a name crossed out — C. Tremaine.
12. Ella searches the city registry for Tremaine; there is no current listing.
13. Prince Valentine invites Ella to a charity gala as his guest.
14. At the gala, Ella watches the elite and notes who avoids her.
15. A man approaches Ella at the bar — Theo Vance, Valentines fixer.
16. Theo warns Ella off the case with a smile and a threat.
17. Ella's office is ransacked that night; the slipper is untouched.
18. She moves the slipper to a safety deposit box at dawn.
19. Corrigan calls Ella to a second death — the coat-check girl, drowned.
20. Ella confronts Valentine at his club; he claims he wants justice for Roland.
21. Valentine admits Roland was blackmailing someone in city hall.
22. Ella digs into city hall records; finds a land deal tied to the DeVane estate.
23. Anastasia meets Ella in a tea shop; reveals her mother remarried for money.
24. Anastasia says Roland kept a private ledger in his study safe.
25. Ella breaks into the DeVane penthouse while the family is at a funeral.
26. She cracks the safe; the ledger is gone. A single page remains.
27. The page lists payments to a city councilman for rezoning votes.
28. Ella interviews the councilman; he claims ignorance, sweats through his shirt.
29. Councilman's car is found at the bottom of the harbor the next morning.
30. Ella realizes someone is cleaning house and she is the broom.
31. She lays a trap: leaks a false story about a witness to the push.
32. Theo Vance takes the bait; he is tailed to a warehouse.
33. Ella watches Theo burn documents in a barrel; photographs everything.
34. Corrigan arrests Theo on obstruction; Vance lawyered up in two hours.
35. Ella confronts Stepmother DeVane with the photo evidence.
36. DeVane admits she pushed Roland — he was about to leave her with nothing.
37. But she did not kill the coat-check girl; that was someone else.
38. Ella realizes Valentine was the one who pushed DeVane's secrets.
39. Prince Valentine wanted Roland dead because Roland knew about Valentine's embezzlement.
40. Valentine hired Ella not to find the slipper, but to be seen investigating.
41. Ella pieces together Valentine's motive: he was embezzling from the city's trust.
42. Roland found out and threatened to expose him at the ball.
43. Stepmother DeVane pushed Roland first, before Valentine could act.
44. Valentine had the coat-check girl killed to eliminate a witness.
45. Ella arranges a meeting with Valentine at the grand ballroom.
46. She wears a wire; Corrigan listens from a van outside.
47. Valentine confesses to ordering the girl's death, smug and relaxed.
48. Corrigan arrests Valentine; the city reels at the scandal.
49. Ella returns the glass slipper to Anastasia, who smiles for the first time.
50. Ella walks home through the rain, the case closed, the city breathing different.
