# The Glass Shoe — Final Report

## Summary

The Glass Shoe is a noir Cinderella retelling set in the fictional city of Aureliana. Detective Ella Marlowe is hired by Prince Valentine to find the owner of a glass slipper left at a masked ball where industrialist Roland DeVane died in a fall. The investigation uncovers a web of embezzlement, blackmail, and murder reaching the city's highest levels of power.
