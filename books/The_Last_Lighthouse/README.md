# The Last Lighthouse

![Cover](covers/The_Last_Lighthouse.png)

**Genre:** Dystopian Survival

The world's last lighthouse keeper refuses to abandon his post even as rising seas and roving gangs consume the coast. When a mysterious voice calls over the radio claiming to lead refugees toward his light, Jonah must decide whether hope is a delusion or the only thing worth dying for.
