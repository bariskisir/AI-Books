# The Dyer's Hand — Final Report

## Summary

The Dyer's Hand is a standalone literary historical novel set in 1954 Fez, Morocco. Master indigo dyer Idris ben Omar, whose hands have been blue for fifty-three years, must decide whether to sell his family's centuries-old formulas to a Paris fashion house when his friend Yacob Cohen — a Jewish dyer now dying in Marseille — returns for one last visit.
