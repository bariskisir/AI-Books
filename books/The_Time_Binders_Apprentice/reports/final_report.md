# The Time-Binder's Apprentice — Final Report

## Summary

A complete time travel novel has been created in the AI-Books repository. The book follows Tom Ashford, a young clockmaker's apprentice in Edwardian London, who discovers his master's Chronometric Loom — a machine that can weave the fabric of time itself. After accidentally tearing a fracture in the timeline, Tom must team up with Lady Cordelia Wren, a time agent from the Temporal Accord, to stop the rogue Time-Binder known as the Unraveler from destroying history.
