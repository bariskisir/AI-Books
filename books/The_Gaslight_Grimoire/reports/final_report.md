# The Gaslight Grimoire — Final Report

## Summary

The Gaslight Grimoire is a Gaslamp Fantasy novel set in Victorian London. The story follows Finn, a street urchin who discovers a grimoire that allows him to see and manipulate the magic woven through the city's gaslights. With the help of Lady Eleanor Blackwood and Inspector Edmund Graves, he must stop the Candle-Eaters from awakening the Drowned God and plunging London into eternal darkness.
