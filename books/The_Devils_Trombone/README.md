# The Devil's Trombone

**Genre:** Jazz Fiction  
**Author:** Barış Kısır  
**Status:** Complete (txt + epub available)

## Synopsis

A trombone player in 1950s New Orleans makes a deal at a crossroads — he plays better than anyone alive, but every note takes a year off his life.

Blue Madere is a journeyman trombonist who never breaks through. Desperate and drunk, he walks to the crossroads at St. Claude and Urquhart one midnight. Mama Legba appears and offers him the deal: his slide will sing like Gabriel's horn, but every note costs him one year of life.

Blue signs in blood. His playing becomes legendary — crowds weep, rivals quit, records sell. But each gig drains him. He grows thinner, older, stranger. His rival Dizzy figures out the price. Blue must decide how many years he is willing to burn before the trombone falls silent forever.
