# The Wolves of Winter — Outline

## Premise

In Ice Age Europe, twenty-five thousand years ago, a young huntress named Ayla must lead her wounded clan across a frozen plain to reach the spring hunting grounds. A pack of wolves tracks them through the endless white, drawn by blood and desperation. As the cold deepens and the food runs out, Ayla must decide what kind of leader she will become — and what price she will pay to keep her people alive.

## Core Cast

- **Ayla** — The clan's best hunter, young but skilled. She carries a spear tipped with mammoth-ivory, crafted by her dead father. Her mother was Old Mother's apprentice; now the role of leadership falls to Ayla.
- **Old Mother** — The clan's shaman and healer. Her leg was shattered by a falling boulder during a hunt. She carries the clan's stories and knows the way across the plain. Her voice grows weaker each day.
- **The Wolf Leader** — The alpha of the wolf pack, a grey-white male with a scar across his muzzle. He is not evil — he is hungry, and his pack is starving. He watches Ayla as often as she watches him.

### Secondary Characters

- **Daron** — A middle-aged hunter, skeptical of Ayla's leadership. He lost his wife to the winter sickness.
- **Kaya** — A young woman, barely adult, carrying her infant brother. She looks to Ayla for hope.
- **Renn** — An old tracker, one eye lost to a cave bear. He is Ayla's quiet ally.
- **The Pack** — Six wolves: the leader, his mate, two yearlings, and two adolescents.

## 50 Chapter Plan

### Part I: The Wounding (Chapters 1–10)

1. **The Broken Hunt** — A mammoth hunt ends in disaster. Old Mother is crushed by a falling rock.
2. **Blood on the Snow** — Ayla carries Old Mother back to camp. The first wolf howl sounds in the distance.
3. **The Shaman's Leg** — Old Mother's leg is beyond healing. She tells Ayla: "You must lead them now."
4. **The Gathering** — The clan debates. Some want to stay. Ayla argues they must cross the plain before the deep winter.
5. **The Pack Arrives** — Renn reports wolves have been seen circling the camp. The pack has found them.
6. **Leaving the Caves** — The clan packs what they can carry. They abandon the winter shelter.
7. **First March** — The group moves onto the frozen plain. The wind cuts like flint.
8. **Old Mother's Fever** — Old Mother grows hot with fever. Ayla treats her with snow and moss.
9. **The Wolf's First Kill** — The pack takes down a straggler reindeer. Ayla watches them feed.
10. **Daron's Doubt** — Daron challenges Ayla's decision. Kaya takes Ayla's side.

### Part II: The White World (Chapters 11–20)

11. **The Frozen River** — The clan must cross a frozen river. Renn tests the ice with his spear.
12. **Through the Ice** — One of the pack falls through thin ice. The others pull it out. Ayla sees their desperation.
13. **The Lost Hunter** — A young hunter named Torv wanders off and does not return.
14. **Smoke on the Plain** — Ayla spots smoke in the distance. Another group? A trap?
15. **The Strangers** — They encounter a small family fleeing from the south. They bring news of starvation.
16. **Sharing the Fire** — Ayla shares food with the strangers. Daron protests.
17. **Old Mother's Story** — Around the fire, Old Mother tells the story of the first wolf who chose humans.
18. **The Wolf Leader Watches** — The alpha stands on a ridge and stares at the camp for a full watch-cycle.
19. **Ayla's Dream** — Ayla dreams of running with the wolf pack, four-legged in the snow.
20. **The Storm** — A blizzard hits. The clan huddles in a shallow dip in the earth.

### Part III: Hunger (Chapters 21–30)

21. **When the Wind Dies** — The storm passes. Two of the strangers are dead. The wolves ate well.
22. **Counting Food** — Kaya inventories what remains. Eight days of food. Maybe ten.
23. **The Huntress Goes Out** — Ayla leaves the group to hunt. Renn goes with her.
24. **The Hare** — Ayla snares a hare. It is not enough. The wolves watch from a distance.
25. **Daron's Plan** — Daron wants to turn back. Ayla refuses. The argument nearly becomes a fight.
26. **The Pack Hunts** — The wolves bring down a sick bison. The clan waits until the pack leaves, then takes the scraps.
27. **Old Mother's Breath** — Old Mother's breathing grows ragged. Ayla sits with her through the night.
28. **The Left Hand** — Ayla must use her left hand to throw her spear. Her right is frostbitten.
29. **The Huntress Fails** — Ayla's spear misses a deer. The clan eats moss and bark that night.
30. **Kaya's Courage** — Kaya volunteers to help hunt. She is untrained but willing.

### Part IV: The Breaking (Chapters 31–40)

31. **Two Hunters** — Ayla teaches Kaya to throw the spear. The wolves watch the lesson.
32. **The First Blood** — Kaya wounds a young deer. Ayla finishes it. They drag it back together.
33. **The Feast** — The clan eats meat for the first time in five days. Hope flickers.
34. **The Wolf Takes a Deer** — The alpha steals a deer carcass the clan was saving. Daron blames Ayla.
35. **Renn's Last March** — Renn's old heart gives out. He dies sitting up, watching the horizon.
36. **Burying the Tracker** — They cover Renn with stones. Ayla speaks for him.
37. **The Split** — Daron and two others leave the group. They head back toward the caves.
38. **The Wolves Follow Daron** — Ayla watches the pack split. Half follow Daron's group.
39. **Old Mother's Gift** — Old Mother gives Ayla her medicine pouch and tells her the secret of the spring.
40. **Old Mother Dies** — Old Mother passes in the night. Ayla wakes to find her still.

### Part V: The Convergence (Chapters 41–50)

41. **Leading Alone** — Ayla leads Kaya and the infant toward the distant mountains. Three of them now.
42. **The Wolf Returns** — The alpha reappears. The other half of the pack rejoined him.
43. **The Last Deer** — Ayla and Kaya hunt together. They bring down a pregnant doe.
44. **The Birth** — Kaya's infant brother grows weak. Ayla feeds him bone broth from the doe.
45. **The End of the Plain** — They reach the edge of the frozen plain. Beyond it: green valleys.
46. **The Stand** — The wolves surround their camp on the last night. Ayla builds a ring of fire.
47. **The Wolf in the Firelight** — The alpha steps into the firelight. He is thin, limping. His mate is dead.
48. **Ayla's Choice** — Ayla could kill the wolf. She lowers her spear. She throws him the last of the meat.
49. **The Truce** — The wolf takes the meat and leaves. In the morning, the pack is gone.
50. **The Green World** — Ayla, Kaya, and the infant descend into the valley. The snow ends. The world is reborn.
