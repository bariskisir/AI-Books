## Synopsis

Dr. Vera Vesna, a Serbian particle physicist working on entanglement experiments at CERN, meets Luca Moretti, an Italian potter in Geneva for an artist residency. Their relationship mirrors the behavior of entangled particles — instantaneous connection across distance, irreducible uncertainty, and the way observation collapses possibility into fixed reality. When Vera's experiment detects a 4.1-sigma anomaly at 750 GeV, she must balance the pursuit of a world-changing discovery with the fragile reality of human connection.
