# The Half-Life of Love

**Genre:** Science Romance  
**Author:** Barış Kısır  
**Status:** Complete (TXT + EPUB)  

## Synopsis

Dr. Vera Vesna, a particle physicist at CERN, has spent seven years chasing an anomaly in her data — a bump at 750 GeV that could rewrite the Standard Model. Her world is precision, measurement, and control. Then she meets Luca Moretti, an Italian potter in Geneva for an artist residency, whose hands shape clay into forms that Vera can only describe with equations.

Their connection is instantaneous and inexplicable — like entangled particles. As Vera's experiment approaches a critical juncture, she must decide whether love is something that can be measured at all.

Set between the underground tunnels of the Large Hadron Collider and Luca's sunlit atelier, *The Half-Life of Love* explores the spaces where science and art converge, the terrifying nature of commitment, and the beauty of choosing uncertainty.
