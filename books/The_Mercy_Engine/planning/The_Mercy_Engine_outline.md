# The Mercy Engine Outline

## Premise

A trauma surgeon tests a triage machine whose numbers begin to change for powerful patients.

## Core Cast

- **Dr. Anika Ro** - an adult figure tied to the Mercy Engine terminal.
- **Tomas Ivek** - an adult figure tied to the Mercy Engine terminal.
- **Nurse Helen Marr** - an adult figure tied to the Mercy Engine terminal.
- **Detective Jun Vale** - an adult figure tied to the Mercy Engine terminal.
- **Director Celia Voss** - an adult figure tied to the Mercy Engine terminal.

## 50 Chapter Plan

1. **First Notice** - Dr. Anika Ro arrives at a crowded city trauma hospital and notices the first flaw in the official story.
2. **The Locked Room** - The mercy engine terminal is protected by a rule that makes no practical sense.
3. **A Name in the Margin** - A marginal name links the case to Director Celia Voss and an older debt.
4. **Old Weather** - Bad weather traps Dr. Anika Ro with people who know more than they say.
5. **The Quiet Witness** - Tomas Ivek becomes a witness when a physical detail contradicts the record.
6. **Inventory of Doubt** - Dr. Anika Ro inventories the evidence and separates fear from fact.
7. **A Door Left Open** - A door left open proves the breach came from inside.
8. **Terms of Access** - Detective Jun Vale offers access with conditions that force a choice.
9. **False Bearings** - A false route wastes a day and reveals who benefits from delay.
10. **The First Hearing** - The first formal confrontation puts Dr. Anika Ro on record.
11. **Bad Paper** - Forged paperwork points toward a respectable public office.
12. **The Night Inspection** - A night inspection gives one concrete clue and a new enemy.
13. **Proof of Life** - Someone thought lost sends proof that the timeline is wrong.
14. **A Private Map** - A private map of motives turns rumor into sequence.
15. **The Broken Seal** - A broken seal exposes an object hidden in plain sight.
16. **Two Versions** - Two accounts of the same event cannot both be true.
17. **The Man Who Paid** - The person who funded the first lie is named.
18. **Ash on the Table** - Residue on a table fixes the scene in time.
19. **The Second Key** - A second key changes the locked-room problem.
20. **Crossing the Line** - A boundary is crossed that cannot be uncrossed.
21. **Witness Marks** - Witness marks show where hands have been.
22. **The Hidden Account** - A hidden account explains why silence was profitable.
23. **No Safe Route** - The planned safe route collapses under pressure.
24. **A Favor Repaid** - Nurse Helen Marr repays an old favor and gives a missing name.
25. **The Failed Alarm** - An alarm fails exactly when it should have worked.
26. **An Honest Lie** - An honest lie from Detective Jun Vale keeps the inquiry alive.
27. **The Cold File** - A cold file turns personal without becoming sentimental.
28. **Pressure Change** - Pressure from Director Celia Voss forces the truth into motion.
29. **The Third Signature** - A third signature proves conspiracy rather than mistake.
30. **The Room Below** - The room below St. Caspar Hospital contains the oldest evidence.
31. **Against Procedure** - Procedure is broken to protect an adult witness.
32. **The Public Story** - The public story hardens while private facts begin to move.
33. **A Narrow Escape** - A narrow escape costs reputation but preserves evidence.
34. **Debt of Silence** - A debt of silence is called in by the wrong person.
35. **The Turned Lock** - A turned lock proves trusted access.
36. **A Record of Hands** - St. Caspar Hospital kept more than documents; it kept leverage.
37. **What the Archive Kept** - Every suspect is brought into one place.
38. **The Last Crossing** - The first beneficiary is confronted.
39. **Salt in the Wound** - The price of disclosure is named plainly.
40. **The Price Named** - The case enters open hearing.
41. **Open Court** - The central proof is tested under harsh light.
42. **The Fire Test** - Every false map collapses into one usable route.
43. **Every False Map** - A draft confession shows remorse without innocence.
44. **The Confession Draft** - Dr. Anika Ro faces Director Celia Voss directly.
45. **No More Intermediaries** - The long walk back gives allies time to choose courage.
46. **The Long Walk Back** - A witness speaks under oath and changes the balance.
47. **Witness Under Oath** - The real ledger is read aloud.
48. **The Real Ledger** - The final locked door opens without ceremony.
49. **The Door Unlatched** - The public consequence lands on the people who bought silence.
50. **After the Verdict** - After the verdict, truth proves useful but incomplete.
