# The Cloister Algorithm - Outline

## Premise

Digital archivist Dr. Elian Morse is hired to audit a machine-learning transcription project at Saint Oran's Abbey after its medieval land charters begin producing ownership claims favorable to a data-center developer. The monastery's manuscripts are not mystical; they are stubbornly physical, full of scraped parchment, wormholes, ink changes, and marginal hands. Elian follows OCR errors, training-set exclusions, altered metadata, and a missing palimpsest leaf to prove the algorithm was tuned to erase common-land rights hidden in abbreviated Latin.

## Core Cast

- **Dr. Elian Morse** - Digital archivist and manuscript technologist, calm with code and ruthless with provenance.
- **Sister Mara Vale** - Abbey librarian, not ordained but bound to the collection by fierce professional duty.
- **Jonas Quill** - Former monk and Latinist who knows the abbey's charter abbreviations.
- **Calder Pike** - Developer seeking abbey common land for a data-center campus.
- **Iris Senn** - County solicitor defending old grazing and water rights.
- **Talia Reed** - Machine-learning engineer who built the first honest model before management replaced it.
- **Bram Voss** - Night guard who saw a scanner run after hours.
- **Nadia Hesk** - Conservator tracing erased ink and scraped parchment.
- **Prior Olan Marr** - Abbey administrator tempted by survival money.
- **Judge Leona Arda** - Chancery judge weighing digital output against physical manuscripts.

## 50 Chapter Plan

1. **Blue Scanner Light** - Elian arrives as charters are being rescanned at night.
2. **Common Meadow** - Iris explains the land rights threatened by the transcription.
3. **Training Set** - Talia reveals the model was retrained without her approval.
4. **Abbreviated Latin** - Jonas shows how a small mark changes private land to common use.
5. **Metadata Saints** - Elian finds saint names used to mask altered file versions.
6. **Developer Visit** - Calder offers the abbey a data-center endowment.
7. **Wormhole Match** - Nadia proves a disputed image came from a different leaf.
8. **First Hearing** - The judge asks whether an algorithm can be cross-examined.
9. **Night Guard** - Bram reports after-hours scanning under Calder's consultant.
10. **Excluded Hands** - Talia finds the model ignores one medieval scribe.
11. **Palimpsest Rumor** - Sister Mara mentions a scraped charter leaf missing from the vault.
12. **Confidence Score** - The model is certain where the manuscript is ambiguous.
13. **Water Clause** - Iris needs the old water-right sentence restored.
14. **Prior's Ledger** - Olan's finances explain his silence.
15. **Ink Under Green** - Nadia images erased text beneath a later devotional note.
16. **Version Drift** - Elian tracks changed outputs across hidden model builds.
17. **Server Chapel** - The developer's demo runs from a server installed in a side chapel.
18. **Jonas Translates** - Jonas produces a human reading of the disputed clause.
19. **Cease Notice** - Calder threatens breach of contract.
20. **Audit Order** - Judge Arda orders source data preserved.
21. **Missing Leaf** - The palimpsest leaf is gone from its folder.
22. **Blue Gloves** - Bram identifies the consultant by nitrile gloves and a silver ring.
23. **False Expansion** - The output adds acres never named in the charter.
24. **Talia's Backup** - Talia has the original model weights and logs.
25. **Marginal Hand** - A note in the margin names meadow, millstream, and winter grazing.
26. **Locked Scriptorium** - Someone tries to remove the scanners.
27. **Nadia's Light** - Multispectral imaging restores the scraped text.
28. **County Memory** - Elder farmers confirm the medieval right was still used.
29. **Code Review** - Elian finds a postprocessor replacing common-right terms.
30. **Olan Confesses** - The prior admits he feared losing the abbey.
31. **Chancery Table** - Physical leaf, model logs, and charters are brought to court.
32. **Consultant Named** - Calder's consultant is tied to metadata changes.
33. **Abbreviation Trial** - Jonas demonstrates the disputed mark.
34. **Confidence Fraud** - Talia explains how certainty was inflated.
35. **Recovered Leaf** - Bram finds the missing leaf behind the scanner cabinet.
36. **Developer Cross** - Calder denies knowing the technical alteration.
37. **Silver Ring** - Security footage shows the consultant handling the leaf.
38. **Water in Court** - Iris links the restored clause to present-day wells.
39. **Injunction** - The judge halts the data-center claim.
40. **Abbey Vote** - The community chooses disclosure over the endowment.
41. **Public Dataset** - Elian rebuilds the archive with transparent versioning.
42. **Talia Repairs** - Talia retrains the model on all scribal hands.
43. **Mara's Rule** - Sister Mara writes handling rules for digital and physical custody.
44. **Calder Withdraws** - The developer abandons the meadow claim.
45. **Common Meadow Walk** - The community marks the boundary on foot.
46. **Olan Resigns** - The prior steps down from financial authority.
47. **Jonas Returns** - Jonas agrees to teach charter Latin at the abbey.
48. **Green Text** - The restored water clause enters the public record.
49. **Scanner Quiet** - The archive room returns to careful work.
50. **The Cloister Algorithm** - Elian leaves a system that admits uncertainty where manuscripts require it.
