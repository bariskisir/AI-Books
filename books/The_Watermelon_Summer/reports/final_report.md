## Story Summary

Three sisters in rural Georgia run their late father's watermelon stand and uncover long-buried family secrets. Delia, the oldest and most burdened, struggles with the farm's debt. Rose returns from Atlanta and faces her own escape from the family. Junie, who never left, keeps the stand going while grieving. Through the summer, they discover their mother's true identity (Lucy Beck), learn she left to protect them from a scandal in Charleston, and eventually pay off the farm's debt. The novel ends with the sisters united, the secrets buried, and the farm saved.
