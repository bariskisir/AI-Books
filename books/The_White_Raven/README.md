# The White Raven

**Genre:** Nordic Noir  
**Author:** Barış Kısır  
**Status:** Complete (txt + epub available)

## Synopsis

When three people vanish without trace during the polar winter in the remote Swedish mining town of Kiruna, Detective Liisa Kivi is sent from Stockholm to investigate. The disappearances defy logic — no tracks, no witnesses, no bodies. The only constant across each crime scene is a white raven, a leucistic anomaly that locals regard as an omen.

As the sunless days stretch on and the cold deepens, Liisa must navigate a web of old grudges, corporate secrets, and a killer who knows the frozen wilderness better than anyone. With the help of a reclusive hunter named Eirik Vinter, she descends into the labyrinthine tunnels of the iron mine — and discovers that the killer has been waiting for her all along.

## Characters

- **Detective Liisa Kivi** — A sharp, quiet investigator with Sami heritage, haunted by an unsolved case from Stockholm
- **Eirik Vinter** — A reclusive hunter and former mining engineer who knows the forest and tunnels
- **Gunnar Nilsson** — The local constable in Kiruna, weary but committed
- **Lars Magnusson** — A security guard at the mine with a past that connects to Liisa
- **The White Raven** — A leucistic raven that appears at each crime scene, trained as a signature
