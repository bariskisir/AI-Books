# The Bone Orchard Trail

**Genre:** Weird West
**Author:** Barış Kısır
**Status:** Complete (txt + epub available)

## Synopsis

Eli Crane hung up his marshal's star after a hanging that took the wrong man. Years later, a debt to a dead friend draws him to Quartz Hollow, a withering high-desert town that buries its dead beneath a grove of pale, twisted trees the locals call the bone orchard. The drought has killed every pasture in the basin except one — the green acres of the cattle baron Absalom Reede.

Reede keeps his land alive and his herd fat by feeding the orchard. Something old and thirsty waits in a dry well behind the ranch, and each dark of the moon it must be paid in the body of a drifter, a debtor, or the friendless. When the next name chosen is the woman who keeps the town's burial ledger, Eli pins on the old badge one last time.

## Characters

- **Eli Crane** — A former territorial marshal who quit the law after hanging an innocent man, drawn west by an old debt
- **Naomi Hale** — The widow who runs the failing way-station and keeps the town's burial ledger
- **Absalom Reede** — The courtly, terrifyingly calm cattle baron who made the bargain a generation ago
- **Doc Sennett** — The town's drunk physician, keeper of uncomfortable knowledge about the orchard
- **Cole Reede** — Absalom's weak, resentful son, who wants the bargain ended
- **The Sexton** — A mute old man who tends the orchard and knows its rules
- **Tom Hale** — Eli's dead friend, whose grave lies in the orchard
