## Characters

1. Eli Crane — A former territorial marshal who quit the law after hanging an innocent man, drawn west by an old debt
2. Naomi Hale — The widow who runs the failing way-station and keeps the town's burial ledger
3. Absalom Reede — The courtly, terrifyingly calm cattle baron who made the bargain a generation ago
4. Doc Sennett — The town's drunk physician, keeper of uncomfortable knowledge about the orchard
5. Cole Reede — Absalom's weak, resentful son, who wants the bargain ended
6. The Sexton — A mute old man who tends the orchard and knows its rules
7. Tom Hale — Eli's dead friend, whose grave lies in the orchard
