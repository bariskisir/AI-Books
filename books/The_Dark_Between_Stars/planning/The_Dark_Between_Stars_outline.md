# The Dark Between Stars — Outline

## Premise

Dr. Eleanor Marsh, a marine geologist from Miskatonic University, joins a research expedition aboard the trawler *Rodbroke* to map the seafloor off the coast of Innsmouth, Massachusetts. What begins as a routine bathymetric survey becomes a descent into nightmare when sonar reveals a structured city in the deepest trench — a city whose architecture predates human evolution. The Deep Ones, ancient amphibious beings, have been waiting.

## Core Cast

- **Dr. Eleanor Marsh** — Marine geologist, rational, skeptical, methodical. She trusts data. The data betrays her.
- **Captain Silas Olmstead** — Grizzled fisherman, knows the old stories, keeps a locked logbook with his grandfather's entries about "the ones below."
- **The Deep Ones** — Not a unified force. Some are builders, some are worshippers, some are something else entirely. They speak in pressure changes and bioluminescence.
