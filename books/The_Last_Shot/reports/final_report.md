# The Last Shot — Final Report

## Summary

The Last Shot is a tennis drama novel following Sloane Keegan, a former tennis prodigy who returns to Wimbledon at age forty for one final match against the twenty-year-old champion who ended her career. The story covers her journey from a Connecticut teaching pro to Centre Court, her five-set battle with Anika Voss, and the personal redemption she finds along the way.
