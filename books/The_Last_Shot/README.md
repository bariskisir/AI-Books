### About

Sloane Keegan was a teenage prodigy who made the Wimbledon semifinals at seventeen and seemed destined for greatness. But the pressure broke her. Fifteen years later, at forty, she has retired to teaching at a suburban tennis club in Connecticut. Then the letter arrives: a wild card invitation to Wimbledon.

The draw places her in the same quarter as Anika Voss, the twenty-year-old world number one and defending champion. Five years ago, a fifteen-year-old Anika ended Sloane's career in straight sets on Court Twelve. Now Sloane must win four matches just to reach her again.

With her elderly coach Ray at her side and her estranged daughter Maya watching from the stands, Sloane steps onto Centre Court for one last shot. What follows is a five-set battle that strips tennis to its bones and forces both women to confront what they are willing to sacrifice for the game they love.

The Last Shot is a tennis drama about pride, regret, and the quiet courage of a woman who refuses to stay down.
