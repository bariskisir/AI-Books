# The Oxblood Cantata - Outline

## Premise

When a newly discovered donor codicil threatens to sell a public concert hall's rare choir library, music conservator Lena Orsay is asked to authenticate the manuscript cantata named in the document. Its oxblood title initials look historic, but the pigment, paper ruling, and binding thread point to a modern forgery. Lena follows choral partbooks, rehearsal marks, donor seals, and a hidden recording to expose a plan to privatize the hall by rewriting its musical inheritance.

## Core Cast

- Lena Orsay - music manuscript conservator.
- Mateo Rusk - choir director fighting to keep the library public.
- Anika Venn - foundation lawyer.
- Felix Marr - donor heir seeking the sale.
- Petra Cole - pigment chemist.
- Jonas Bell - elderly copyist who knows the old choir hands.
- Mara Quill - civil judge overseeing the foundation dispute.
- Elise Rowe - archivist of the concert hall.
