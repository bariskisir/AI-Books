# The Porcelain Doll

![Cover](covers/The_Porcelain_Doll.png)

**Genre:** Japanese Literary

In post-WWII Kyoto, a Japanese dollmaker discovers that the dolls she crafts carry the souls of children who died in the war. A grieving American officer seeks her out, and together they navigate the chasm between cultures, the weight of memory, and the quiet courage of choosing life after loss.

**By Barış Kısır**
