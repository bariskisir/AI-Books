# The Brass Semaphore - Outline

## Premise

In the fogbound harbor city of Lorn, retired signal officer Ada Vey is asked to authenticate a restored brass semaphore before it becomes the centerpiece of a naval museum. When the instrument's gear ratios, flag-arm wear, and coded maintenance scratches do not match its heroic legend, Ada uncovers a wartime smuggling route hidden inside public signal drills. With a harbor archivist, a clockmaker, a tug captain, and a customs lawyer, she follows signal books, foghorn timing, brass filings, and altered watch logs to expose a respected admiral's family fortune.

## Core Cast

- **Ada Vey** - Retired signal officer and communications historian, exacting and blunt.
- **Jonas Reed** - Harbor archivist who guards signal books and watch logs.
- **Mara Quill** - Clockmaker restoring the semaphore gears.
- **Calder Pike** - Museum patron descended from the celebrated admiral.
- **Iris Senn** - Customs lawyer investigating historic smuggling claims.
- **Bram Vale** - Tug captain whose family kept private fog logs.
- **Talia Hesk** - Metallurgist tracing brass filings and repair alloys.
- **Olan Marr** - Museum director eager for the patron's funding.
- **Nadia Voss** - Dockyard clerk whose inherited notebook names false signals.
- **Judge Leona Arda** - Maritime magistrate.

## 50 Chapter Plan

1. **Fog Arm** - Ada sees the restored semaphore move with the wrong timing.
2. **Museum Legend** - Calder presents the instrument as a patriotic relic.
3. **Signal Book** - Jonas shows missing pages in the harbor signal code.
4. **Clockmaker's Gear** - Mara finds a replacement gear with altered teeth.
5. **False Drill** - Ada identifies a signal that was never part of official drill.
6. **Customs Question** - Iris connects the false signal to lost cargo cases.
7. **Foghorn Minute** - Bram's family logs contradict the public watch record.
8. **Brass Filings** - Talia finds modern filings inside an old bearing.
9. **Patron Dinner** - Calder pressures the museum to open the exhibit.
10. **Dock Notebook** - Nadia brings a clerk's notebook with coded entries.
11. **Semaphore Test** - Ada reproduces the false signal on the restored machine.
12. **Watch Tower** - Jonas and Ada inspect the old signal tower.
13. **Gear Ratio** - Mara proves the arms were modified for a faster code.
14. **Cargo Names** - Iris matches false signals to missing customs cargo.
15. **Admiral's Portrait** - Calder defends his ancestor's reputation.
16. **Maintenance Scratches** - Ada decodes scratches as private timing marks.
17. **Night Harbor** - Bram shows where smuggling boats could pass in fog.
18. **Museum Hold** - Olan agrees to delay only under pressure.
19. **Court Petition** - Iris seeks preservation of watch logs and signal books.
20. **Filing Cabinet** - Jonas finds a misfiled fog drill report.
21. **Counterweight** - Talia finds a hidden brass weight stamped with a ship mark.
22. **Nadia's Key** - The notebook key translates dock initials.
23. **False Heroism** - The famous rescue signal overlaps with a smuggling run.
24. **Calder's Archive** - Calder's private archive omits crucial dates.
25. **Public Hearing** - The magistrate orders the semaphore demonstrated.
26. **Fog Reenactment** - Ada uses horn timing to prove the false route.
27. **Mara's Repair** - The gear damage reveals deliberate wartime modification.
28. **Customs Ledger** - Iris finds duty payments rerouted to a family account.
29. **Bram Testifies** - Tug logs place a Pike vessel in the fog lane.
30. **Museum Rift** - The board splits over funding and truth.
31. **Signal Scratch** - Ada matches a scratch code to Nadia's notebook.
32. **Patron Threat** - Calder threatens withdrawal and litigation.
33. **Tower Clock** - Mara repairs the tower clock and restores exact timing.
34. **Lost Cargo** - Iris identifies families owed restitution.
35. **Hidden Drawer** - Jonas finds the missing signal pages.
36. **Admiral's Order** - The pages show the admiral authorized false drills.
37. **Brass Witness** - Talia links the hidden weight to the Pike ship.
38. **Injunction** - The exhibit opening is halted.
39. **Calder Breaks** - Calder admits he knew of rumors but not proof.
40. **Restitution List** - Iris files claims for cargo proceeds.
41. **New Label** - Ada and Jonas write the corrected exhibit text.
42. **Semaphore Restored** - Mara restores the machine without erasing modification marks.
43. **Harbor Walk** - Bram leads a public walk of the fog route.
44. **Olan's Apology** - The director admits the museum preferred the donor story.
45. **Patron Removed** - Calder's name comes off the gallery.
46. **Signal Lesson** - Ada teaches the false code as evidence.
47. **Fog Clear** - The harbor archives reopen fully.
48. **Cargo Settlement** - The first restitution payment is approved.
49. **Brass Quiet** - Ada closes the tower for the evening.
50. **The Brass Semaphore** - The instrument stands as a witness to signal, silence, and corrected history.
