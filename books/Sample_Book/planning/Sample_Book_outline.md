# Sample Book — Outline

## Premise
A template novel demonstrating the standard structure and conventions for AI-Books. Set in a quiet coastal town, a librarian discovers a hidden archive that reveals the intertwined stories of the town's residents across generations.

## Core Cast
- **Elena Marchetti** (38): Town librarian, curious and methodical, keeper of the archive
- **Thomas Webb** (52): Retired fisherman, Elena's neighbor, source of oral history
- **Dr. Amina Shah** (45): Historian visiting from the university, helps interpret the documents
- **Leo Costa** (29): Young carpenter restoring the old library building, Elena's love interest
