## Characters

1. Lt. Cmdr. Daniel Reyes — Executive officer who must take command and choose between the mission and the men
2. Cmdr. Walter Hale — The captain, gravely burned early in the casualty, whose orders become a question the boat must answer
3. Chief of the Boat Tom Garrity — The senior enlisted man, gruff and loyal, who steadies Reyes
4. Lt. Avery Cole — The young engineering officer who blames himself for the fire and jury-rigs the air
5. Sonarman First Class Eddie Mahon — The ears of the boat, who reads the surface no one else can hear
6. Doc Pruitt — The independent-duty corpsman fighting to keep the captain alive
7. Petty Officer Karl Brandt — A quiet electrician with a forged past on whom suspicion settles
8. Chief Auxiliaryman Joe Salas — Keeper of the air and water, who finds the second act of sabotage
9. Seaman Lewis Tran — The youngest aboard, a torpedoman striker who proves himself in the bilge
