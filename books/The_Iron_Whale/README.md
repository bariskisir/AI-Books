# The Iron Whale

**Genre:** Submarine Thriller
**Author:** Barış Kısır
**Status:** Complete (txt + epub available)

## Synopsis

In the spring of 1968, the aging diesel-electric submarine USS Cuttlefish creeps to the edge of Soviet waters on a covert listening patrol, recording the acoustic signature of a new class of warship. The terms of the mission are absolute: hear everything, be heard by nothing. Then a fire breaks out in the after battery — close enough to the boat's shielded auxiliary unit to terrify a crew that does not fully trust their refit — and in a single black hour the Cuttlefish loses her radio, her trim, and her contact with command.

Trapped three hundred feet down with a wounded captain, dwindling air, a failing battery, and hostile warships circling overhead, Executive Officer Daniel Reyes must hold a frightened crew together while the carbon dioxide climbs and the cold creeps in. Worse, two small acts of sabotage suggest there is a professional aboard who wants the boat to die quietly — and suspicion spreads through the hull like a second smoke. As the air runs out and a destroyer settles directly overhead, Reyes faces the only question left: stay silent and let the sea win, or surface into the guns of an enemy that must never know they were there.

## Characters

- **Lt. Cmdr. Daniel Reyes** — Executive officer who must take command and choose between the mission and the men
- **Cmdr. Walter Hale** — The captain, gravely burned early in the casualty, whose orders become a question the boat must answer
- **Chief of the Boat Tom Garrity** — The senior enlisted man, gruff and loyal, who steadies Reyes
- **Lt. Avery Cole** — The young engineering officer who blames himself for the fire and jury-rigs the air
- **Sonarman First Class Eddie Mahon** — The ears of the boat, who reads the surface no one else can hear
- **Doc Pruitt** — The independent-duty corpsman fighting to keep the captain alive
- **Petty Officer Karl Brandt** — A quiet electrician with a forged past on whom suspicion settles
- **Chief Auxiliaryman Joe Salas** — Keeper of the air and water, who finds the second act of sabotage
- **Seaman Lewis Tran** — The youngest aboard, a torpedoman striker who proves himself in the bilge
