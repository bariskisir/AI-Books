# The Last Hunt

**Genre:** Western Literary  
**Author:** Barış Kısır  
**Status:** Complete (txt, epub, cover)

## Description

An aging Comanche tracker is hired by the Texas Ranger who once hunted his people to find a kidnapped girl in the harsh Texas plains. The trail pushes both men across a landscape of dust and memory, forcing them to reckon with the violence that shaped them.

## Characters

- **Santiago** — Comanche elder and tracker, nearly seventy. Quiet, watchful, carrying the weight of his people's loss.
- **Ranger Elias McAllister** — Texas Ranger, former Indian fighter, haunted by the ghosts of his past.
- **Maria Gutierrez** — Fifteen-year-old kidnapped girl, fierce and resourceful.
- **Tomas Gutierrez** — Maria's father, a tenant farmer hollowed by grief.
