# The Cold Water

**Genre:** Debut Novel / Literary Fiction  
**Author:** Barış Kısır  
**Status:** Complete (TXT + EPUB available)

## About

In the summer of 1992, twelve-year-old River Corso is left alone in a central Florida trailer park when his mother drives to Tampa for a waitressing shift she never comes back from. When Hurricane Celia barrels across the peninsula, River must board up their double-wide by himself, ride out the worst of it in a bathtub, and emerge into a landscape where everything he knows is underwater or gone.

In the aftermath of the storm, River meets Mrs. Delgado, an elderly Cuban-American widow who lost her own home. Together they navigate the wreckage, the shelters, and the long wait for news. As River searches for his missing mother and learns the truth about the night she left, he discovers that survival is not about being tough. It is about letting people in.
