## Core Cast

### Leo Ashworth
- **Role:** Protagonist / Disgraced occult PI
- **Description:** A former magical investigator who lost his license when a case went wrong and a creature witness died under his protection. Now running a small detective agency in Camden, cynical and withdrawn, but possessing sharp instincts and a moral core he cannot fully suppress.

### Dr. Mira Chandrasekhar
- **Role:** Folklore scholar and reluctant consultant
- **Description:** A meticulous academic at the British Museum who inadvertently discovered the hidden world while cataloging Greek artifacts. Pragmatic, methodical, and brave under pressure, she becomes the team's researcher and moral compass.

### Cassia Verney
- **Role:** Keeper of the Accord / Sphinx
- **Description:** An ancient sphinx who has served as the Accord's archivist and mediator for centuries. Works undercover as a British Museum curator. Weary but fiercely principled, she carries the weight of keeping the peace between species.

### DCI Tom Fletcher
- **Role:** Police liaison / Metropolitan Police detective
- **Description:** A Scotland Yard detective who handles the 'weird' cases the regular force cannot explain. Jaded, pragmatic, and doggedly loyal, he provides police resources and protection while navigating a chain of command that does not believe in monsters.

## Supporting Characters

- **Edgar Pillsworth:** Leo's mentor and cryptozoologist
- **Medea Gorgon:** Reluctant Gorgon descendant and Ashford's unwilling partner
- **Asterion:** Minotaur envoy of the First-Born faction
