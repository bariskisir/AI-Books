# The Concrete Chorus

## About

**Genre:** Urban Fantasy / Mythological Creatures

**Model:** deepseek-v4-flash

Disgraced occult investigator Leo Ashworth is pulled from self-imposed exile when mythological creatures begin turning to stone across London — victims petrified in poses of terror. He joins forces with folklore scholar Dr. Mira Chandrasekhar and the ancient sphinx Cassia Verney, Keeper of the Accord, to uncover a conspiracy. The killer is wielding the Aegis of Perseus, a mythical artifact that channels a modified Gorgon's curse, and the attacks are claimed by the First-Born — a purist faction who believe creatures should rule humanity. Racing against a deadline that threatens every creature in London, Leo must confront his own past failure and redeem himself before the hidden world is shattered forever.
