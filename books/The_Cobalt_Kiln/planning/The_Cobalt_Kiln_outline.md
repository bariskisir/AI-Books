# The Cobalt Kiln - Outline

## Premise

Ceramic conservator Dr. Mara Voss is asked to authenticate a set of cobalt-glazed bowls before a museum accepts a major donor's collection. The bowls are said to come from a lost kiln village, but their glaze bubbles, ash scars, clay inclusions, and firing shadows tell a different story. Mara follows shard chemistry, kiln ledgers, repair lacquer, and a forged excavation permit to prove the donor's prized collection was assembled from looted temple storage and modern workshop substitutions.

## Core Cast

- **Dr. Mara Voss** - Ceramic conservator specializing in glaze chemistry and kiln marks.
- **Jonas Senn** - Archaeological ceramicist with field notes from the real kiln village.
- **Iris Quill** - Museum registrar who distrusts donor paperwork.
- **Calder Rusk** - Donor whose collection is funding a new wing.
- **Talia Reed** - Materials scientist analyzing cobalt isotopes and clay inclusions.
- **Bram Pike** - Retired kiln builder who reads firing scars by touch.
- **Nadia Hesk** - Provenance lawyer working with a temple trust.
- **Olan Marr** - Dealer who supplied the donor's bowls.
- **Leona Vale** - Storage curator who finds matching temple crate marks.
- **Judge Elian Arda** - Cultural-property magistrate.

## 50 Chapter Plan

1. **Blue Bowl** - Mara sees a glaze bubble pattern that does not match the claimed kiln.
2. **Donor Wall** - Calder pushes the museum to accept his collection quickly.
3. **Registrar Doubt** - Iris shows gaps in loan and import paperwork.
4. **Kiln Village** - Jonas describes the true lost kiln site's clay and ash.
5. **Cobalt Line** - Talia begins isotope testing on a glaze fleck.
6. **Ash Scar** - Bram finds firing scars from a different kiln design.
7. **Temple Trust** - Nadia warns that similar bowls vanished from temple storage.
8. **First Board Meeting** - The museum board resists delay.
9. **Repair Lacquer** - Mara sees modern lacquer hiding old inventory marks.
10. **Dealer Visit** - Olan insists the bowls are cleanly sourced.
11. **Shard Drawer** - Jonas compares a field shard to the donor bowl.
12. **False Permit** - Iris finds an excavation permit issued after the export.
13. **Cobalt Source** - Talia traces cobalt to a modern supplier.
14. **Bram's Kiln** - Bram reconstructs the firing chamber from scorch marks.
15. **Storage Crate** - Leona finds a temple crate number under lacquer.
16. **Temporary Hold** - Nadia seeks a hold on accession.
17. **Donor Pressure** - Calder threatens to withdraw wing funding.
18. **Clay Inclusion** - Mara identifies river shell not found at the claimed site.
19. **Olan's Ledger** - Dealer records show mixed lots.
20. **Hearing Notice** - The magistrate orders the bowls preserved.
21. **Second Bowl** - A supposedly matching bowl is a modern substitute.
22. **Glaze Crawl** - Talia proves uneven firing from a gas kiln.
23. **Temple Photographs** - Nadia produces old storage photographs.
24. **Board Split** - The museum fractures over money and ethics.
25. **Kiln Rain** - Jonas reads weather damage from the real site.
26. **Hidden Number** - Mara lifts lacquer from an inventory mark.
27. **Dealer's Assistant** - Olan's assistant admits crate relabeling.
28. **Cobalt Dust** - Modern cobalt dust appears in a repair seam.
29. **Court Table** - Evidence moves into court under seal.

32. **Export Route** - Iris maps the bowls' false route through freeport storage.
33. **Temple Claim** - The temple trust identifies missing objects.
34. **Substitution Pattern** - Mara separates old stolen bowls from new fakes.
35. **Olan Breaks** - Olan gives up the workshop and freeport records.
36. **Kiln Search** - Investigators find the cobalt kiln still warm.
37. **Blue Hands** - Workshop workers confirm modern copies.
38. **Injunction** - The magistrate blocks accession and export.
39. **Museum Vote** - The board rejects Calder's conditional gift.
40. **Return Terms** - Nadia negotiates conservation before restitution.
41. **Restoration Lab** - Mara stabilizes old bowls without hiding evidence.
42. **Public Label** - Iris writes a label naming theft and substitution.
43. **Jonas's Shards** - Field shards return to the study collection.
44. **Bram's Lesson** - Bram teaches staff to read kiln scars.
45. **Calder Withdraws** - Calder withdraws from the museum circle.
46. **Temple Storage** - The old bowls are returned to trust custody.
47. **Fake Archive** - The modern copies become teaching objects.
48. **Blue Wing** - The museum reopens the gallery without donor vanity.
49. **Kiln Quiet** - Mara closes the case file.
50. **The Cobalt Kiln** - The bowls' blue glaze remains beautiful without being allowed to lie.
