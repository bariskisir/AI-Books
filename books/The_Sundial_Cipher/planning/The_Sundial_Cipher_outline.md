# The Sundial Cipher Outline

## Premise

After her former tutor is found beneath the public sundial with a gear clenched in his hand, instrument maker Beatrice Sforza discovers that his last commission hides a cipher readable only by shadow at noon. The code points to treasury fraud, a private militia, and a political murder planned for the feast day when every clock in the city will be reset.

## Core Cast

- **Beatrice Sforza** - an instrument maker whose precision masks a history of exile.
- **Matteo Orsini** - a printer who can set type faster than he can keep secrets.
- **Captain Livia Renzi** - a city guard officer loyal to law rather than faction.
- **Girolamo Vettori** - a banker who buys silence in sealed packets.
- **Father Neri** - a mathematician-priest who understands why the sundial was altered.
