# The Stormheart Crown

**Genre:** Fantasy Romance  
**Author:** Barış Kısır  
**Model:** deepseek-v4-flash

A royal cartographer and a reclusive storm mage must find a legendary artifact before a fanatical inquisitor destroys them both. Set in the Stormreach Archipelago, where perpetual storms have shaped both the landscape and the culture.

## Characters
- **Serina Vex** — A meticulous royal mapmaker who believes all mysteries can be charted.
- **Kaelen Stormriven** — A storm mage whose volatile power grows harder to control.
- **Commander Heston Vane** — A zealot of the Celestial Church who executes weather-workers.
- **Lyra Deepwater** — A smuggler captain who knows every hidden cove in the archipelago.
- **Aldric Norwood** — A disgraced scholar exiled for researching forbidden storm magic.
