## Premise

At the Vela Ridge Observatory, sold off and slated to go dark, the last staff astronomer, Tomas Reye, must close the place down. Before a century of glass photographic plates is crated and dispersed, a young digitization technician, Sable Okonkwo, finds a star on a 1970 plate that no catalogue accounts for — circled in grease pencil by a hand neither of them knows. The hand belonged to Ada Mireu, a "computer" erased from the record for a discovery the men around her quietly claimed. Tomas, who was there as a junior man of twenty-two and said nothing, must finally set it right.
