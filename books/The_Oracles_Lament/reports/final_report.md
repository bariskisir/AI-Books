# Final Report — The Oracle's Lament

## Summary

The Oracle's Lament is a complete mythological fiction novel retelling the Trojan War from the perspective of Cassandra, the cursed prophet of Troy. The novel follows Cassandra through the ten years of the war, from the first sighting of the Greek fleet to the fall of Troy and her own death at Mycenae.

- **EPUB**: Valid EPUB 3 file with cover, title page, all chapters, and table of contents
- **Metadata**: Full JSON metadata with descriptions and character list
