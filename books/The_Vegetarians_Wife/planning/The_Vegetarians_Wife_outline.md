# The Vegetarian's Wife — Outline

## Premise

Hana's husband Jae stops eating. Not as a diet or a protest — he simply ceases to want food. At first she tries to accommodate him: special broths, protein drinks, doctor visits. But as weeks pass, Jae's body grows leaner, his eyes brighter, his skin translucent. He begins to speak of things he could not know. He begins to forget things he should remember. Something is hollowing him out from the inside, and Hana must decide whether to fight for the man she married or accept the thing he is becoming.

## Core Cast

- **Hana** — Wife, a careful woman, a keeper of routines, a witness.
- **Jae** — Husband, a quiet architect, now a stranger inside his own skin.
- **Dr. Elias** — A physician who wants to help but cannot explain what he sees.

## 50 Chapter Plan

### Part I: The First Hunger (Chapters 1-10)

1. **The Empty Plate** — Hana clears Jae's untouched dinner and washes the dish.
2. **A Small Explanation** — Jae says he simply is not hungry. Hana waits.
3. **The First Week** — Broth goes cold. Jae drinks water and seems content.
4. **The Second Week** — Hana calls Dr. Elias. Jae submits to an examination.
5. **Vital Signs** — Jae is healthy. Thinner, but healthy. No explanation.
6. **The Dream of Light** — Jae tells Hana he dreamed of a white field. No animals. No sound.
7. **A Body Changing** — The bones of Jae's face become visible. Hana watches him shrink.
8. **The Smell of Cooking** — Hana fries onions. Jae does not react.
9. **The Doctor Returns** — Dr. Elias runs blood tests. Everything is normal.
10. **The Third Week** — Jae stops drinking water. Hana begins to be afraid.

### Part II: The Disappearing (Chapters 11-20)

11. **Thirst** — Jae's lips crack. He says he is not thirsty.
12. **The Dream of Silence** — Jae tells Hana the white field had a house in it.
13. **IV Fluids** — Dr. Elias insists. Jae allows the needle, does not resist.
14. **A Wife's Work** — Hana takes leave from her job. She watches him sleep.
15. **The Things He Knows** — Jae mentions a childhood scar Hana knows he never had.
16. **The Forgotten Year** — Jae cannot remember their wedding day.
17. **Maps in His Palm** — Jae draws a city Hana does not recognize.
18. **The House in the Field** — Jae describes the house: green door, cracked window.
19. **Dr. Elias Worries** — The doctor recommends a specialist.
20. **Hana Says No** — Hana refuses to let them put Jae in a hospital.

### Part III: The Between (Chapters 21-30)

21. **The Eyes Change** — Jae's irises have paled. Hana notices in sunlight.
22. **No Sleep** — Jae lies awake all night. His breathing slows to nothing.
23. **Weight** — Jae weighs forty kilos. His skeleton is visible.
24. **The Bed** — Hana sleeps in the guest room now. Jae does not ask why.
25. **The Things He Forgets** — Jae asks who the president is. He does not know the year.
26. **The Things He Knows** — Jae describes Hana's grandmother's kitchen. He never met her.
27. **The Green Door** — Jae says the house is getting closer. The door is opening.
28. **Dr. Elias's Final Visit** — The doctor tells Hana there is nothing medical left to try.
29. **A Quiet Kitchen** — Hana sits alone in the kitchen. The house is silent.
30. **The Reflection** — Hana sees herself in the dark window. She looks older.

### Part IV: The Threshold (Chapters 31-40)

31. **Jae Stops Speaking** — Three days of silence. Hana talks to him anyway.
32. **The Narrow Bed** — Hana lies beside him. He feels like a bundle of sticks.
33. **The Dream Shared** — Hana dreams of the white field. She wakes crying.
34. **What Remains** — Hana finds Jae's old photographs. She does not recognize the man in them.
35. **The Door Opens** — Jae speaks: "The door is open now."
36. **A Visitor** — Dr. Elias comes without being called. He is afraid.
37. **The Vigil** — Hana sits by the bed. She holds Jae's hand. It is cold.
38. **The Last Word** — Jae says Hana's name. Then nothing more.
39. **The Empty Room** — The bed is empty. The window is open.
40. **The Search** — Hana walks the neighborhood. Jae is nowhere.

### Part V: The After (Chapters 41-50)

41. **The Police** — Hana files a missing person report. They find nothing.
42. **The House in the Field** — Hana drives into the country looking for a house with a green door.
43. **An Old Woman** — A farmer's wife tells Hana about a man who stopped eating twenty years ago.
44. **The Second Story** — Another woman, another husband, the same vanishing.
45. **Grief and Bread** — Hana bakes bread for the first time in months. She eats alone.
46. **The Letter** — A note arrives in Jae's handwriting. He says he is in the white field.
47. **The Green Door Found** — Hana finds an abandoned farmhouse with a green door.
48. **Inside** — The house is empty. Dust. A single chair. Jae's sweater on the floor.
49. **The Field** — Hana walks into the field behind the house. The grass is silent.
50. **The Return** — Hana drives home. She makes dinner. She sets one plate. She eats.
