# The Aurora Lease - Outline

## Premise

At the edge of the polar night, atmospheric physicist Dr. Leda Marr operates aurora instruments on a research ridge leased to a private hydrogen consortium. When her magnetometer records controlled ground pulses beneath a protected migration corridor, the consortium claims natural auroral interference and prepares to activate a drilling array. Leda follows ion traces, ice-road permits, altered lease clauses, and testimony from winter crews to prove that the lease boundary was quietly shifted under the cover of sky science.

## Core Cast

- **Dr. Leda Marr** - Adult atmospheric physicist, station lead, exacting and exhausted by corporate sponsorship.
- **Anik Vale** - Adult local logistics chief who knows the ice roads and migration corridors.
- **Dr. Ro Senn** - Adult magnetometer engineer who built the ridge instruments.
- **Marta Quill** - Adult hydrogen consortium legal officer defending the lease.
- **Evan Pike** - Adult project director pushing winter activation.
- **Suri Noll** - Adult wildlife path monitor tracking caribou and sensor collars.
- **Tomas Iver** - Adult ice-road driver who saw rigs move outside the marked corridor.
- **Nadia Pell** - Adult government lease auditor sent after Leda's complaint.
- **Iris Vey** - Adult station medic managing crew frost injuries and morale.
- **Judge Olan Rees** - Adult northern land-use magistrate who hears the emergency boundary petition.

## 50 Chapter Plan

### Part I: Sky Noise
1. **Green Curtain** - Leda watches an aurora and sees ground pulses in the magnetometer.
2. **Lease Ridge** - The hydrogen consortium prepares to activate the winter test array.
3. **Anik's Map** - Anik shows the old migration corridor crossing the new lease line.
4. **Instrument Check** - Ro proves the signal is not auroral noise.
5. **Legal Weather** - Marta calls the readings inconclusive and commercially sensitive.
6. **Collar Silence** - Suri loses wildlife collar signals near the array pads.
7. **Ice Road** - Tomas reports night rigs moving beyond approved stakes.
8. **Clause Shift** - Leda finds a revised lease map replacing a watershed marker.
9. **Station Pressure** - Evan threatens to pull funding from the research station.
10. **First Complaint** - Leda files a boundary complaint before activation.

### Part II: Below the Lights
11. **Frozen Cable** - Ro discovers a buried pulse cable near the protected corridor.
12. **Old Marker** - Anik finds the original boundary cairn under snow.
13. **Blank Telemetry** - Suri's collar records show synchronized dropouts.
14. **Lease Auditor** - Nadia Pell arrives and demands raw data.
15. **Ridge Hearing** - Marta argues winter work must proceed before thaw.
16. **Driver's Log** - Tomas produces fuel receipts for unauthorized rig runs.
17. **Aurora Forecast** - Leda predicts the company will hide pulses under a storm.
18. **Medic's Ward** - Iris treats a worker who mentions pressure to move stakes.
19. **The Wrong Watershed** - Ro proves the revised map uses a false drainage line.
20. **Temporary Halt** - Nadia orders a 48-hour pause.

### Part III: Lease Fire
21. **Green Storm** - The aurora storm begins and the ground pulses resume.
22. **Snow Traverse** - Leda and Anik cross the ridge to document live cable activity.
23. **Pad Four** - They find Pad Four outside the legal boundary.
24. **Worker Statement** - Iris records a crew member's account of moved stakes.
25. **Marta's Offer** - Marta offers research funding for a narrow technical correction.
26. **Suri's Herd** - The caribou herd turns away from the corridor.
27. **Public Feed** - Leda releases nonproprietary magnetometer plots.
28. **Evan's Denial** - Evan claims sabotage by anti-energy activists.
29. **Nadia's Chain** - Nadia traces the false map through government upload logs.
30. **Emergency Petition** - The case goes to Judge Rees.

### Part IV: Boundary Night
31. **Court by Satellite** - The hearing opens by low-bandwidth video.
32. **Instrument Truth** - Ro explains the difference between sky and ground signal.
33. **Ice Witness** - Tomas testifies about night convoys.
34. **Migration Path** - Suri shows the herd's failed crossing.
35. **Funding Threat** - Evan admits activation is tied to investor deadline.
36. **Legal Margin** - Marta argues the shift falls within survey tolerance.
37. **Cairn Under Snow** - Anik presents the original marker and field coordinates.
38. **Leda's Overlay** - Leda overlays pulses, pads, collars, and lease maps.
39. **Auditor's Finding** - Nadia declares the public lease record unreliable.
40. **Injunction** - Judge Rees halts activation.

### Part V: True North
41. **Quiet Array** - The test pads go silent and the ridge feels larger.
42. **Corrected Lease** - The boundary returns to the watershed line.
43. **Herd Crossing** - Suri documents the herd using the corridor again.
44. **Station Vote** - The station chooses public funding over consortium control.
45. **Marta's File** - Marta releases internal notes showing pressure from Evan.
46. **Winter Repair** - Anik and Tomas remove false stakes.
47. **Ro's Archive** - Ro builds a public sky/ground signal archive.
48. **Evan Removed** - The consortium removes Evan from the project.
49. **Aurora Watch** - Leda returns to observing lights without corporate silence.
50. **The Aurora Lease** - The corrected map hangs beside the magnetometer record.
