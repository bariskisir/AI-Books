# The Thirty-Seventh Floor - Final Report

## Summary

The Thirty-Seventh Floor is a 50-chapter conspiracy thriller novel written by Barış Kısır. The story follows investigative journalist Kate Ross as she discovers a hidden floor in a Manhattan skyscraper where the architect Elias Voss has built a consciousness-uploading facility for the ultra-rich.

## Characters

- **Kate Ross** - Investigative journalist who uncovers the secret of the 37th floor
- **Omar Hassan** - Meridian Tower Security Chief who ultimately helps Kate
- **Elias Voss** - The Architect, creator of the hidden floor's technology

## Structure

- 50 chapters organized in three acts
- Act I (Chapters 1-15): The Anomaly - Kate discovers the missing floor
- Act II (Chapters 16-35): The Hidden Floor - Kate explores the 37th floor and meets Voss
- Act III (Chapters 36-50): The Revelation - Kate escapes and exposes the facility
