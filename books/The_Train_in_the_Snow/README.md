# The Train in the Snow

A Winter Mystery

## About

A luxury express train bound for St. Moritz becomes trapped by an avalanche high in the Swiss Alps. As passengers wait for rescue, one of them is found dead in a locked compartment — murdered. Inspector Felix Brandt, traveling incognito, must identify the killer among a group of strangers, all of whom have perfect alibis. The victim was a blackmailer. Every passenger had motive. But nobody could have done it.

A locked-room mystery set against the relentless white of an Alpine storm.
