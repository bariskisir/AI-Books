## Concept

The daughter of the River Styx ferryman must guide a living Roman soldier through the underworld to save her father's soul from a rogue god.

## Core Cast

- **Kore** — Daughter of Charon. Raised on the shores of the Styx.
- **Marcus** — Roman legionnaire of the XIV Gemina. Pragmatic, brave.
- **Charon** — The Ferryman of the Styx. Kore's father.
- **Stygius** — A rogue chthonic god seeking to control the passage of souls.
