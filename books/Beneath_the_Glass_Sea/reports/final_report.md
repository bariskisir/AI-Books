# Beneath the Glass Sea — Final Report

## Summary

A complete climate fiction novel by Barış Kısır. Set in 2089, Atlantica is the last habitable human settlement, an underwater dome city sealed beneath the ocean. When microfractures appear in the dome's crystal structure, marine engineer Kai Takeda discovers a geothermal vent beneath the city is causing catastrophic stress. With 50,000 lives at stake, she must navigate bureaucratic obstruction, recover exiled research, and perform an impossible deep-sea repair.

## Characters

- **Kai Takeda** — Chief Structural Engineer. Practical, stubborn, haunted by past failure.
- **Dr. Elara Nereus** — Former lead materials scientist, exiled for her accurate crack models.
- **Councilor Hal Voss** — Head of the Atlantica Council. Pragmatic, controlling, believes in managing perception.
- **Sol Chen** — Dockmaster and mechanical engineer. Kai's ally and fabricator.
