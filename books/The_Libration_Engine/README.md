# The Libration Engine

## Science Fiction / Hard Sci-Fi Space Colony Thriller

In the 23rd century, the Stanford-torus habitat Veriditas orbits the Earth-Moon L5 Lagrange point — a self-sustaining wheel a kilometer in diameter, housing 50,000 colonists who have built a fragile independence from a climate-ravaged Earth ruled by an authoritarian regime.

When the ancient libration stabilizer at the habitat's heart begins a stealth failure that will doom the colony within weeks, Chief Engineer Anara Voss discovers a conspiracy stretching back to Veriditas's founding: Earth secretly sabotaged the stabilizer decades ago and now offers rescue in exchange for permanent submission.

Racing against structural decay, an approaching invasion fleet, and their own political divisions, Anara and her unlikely team must find Dr. Reiner Faust's hidden failsafe — a second stabilizer — before Veriditas drifts irrevocably from its Lagrange point into destruction.
