# The Truth About Monsters

A Children's Fantasy novel by Barış Kısır

## About the Book

Ten-year-old Elodie has a secret: she can see the monsters that live under other people's beds. These creatures are not imaginary — they are living embodiments of human fears, and each one needs a host to survive. When people outgrow their fears, their monsters begin to fade away, and Elodie must help them find new hosts before they vanish forever.

With the help of her own gentle monster Grumble, Elodie navigates a hidden world of fear and courage. But a sinister figure called The Collector is hunting the same monsters, trapping them in glass jars to feed his own dark purpose. As Elodie confronts The Collector and the mysterious void-thing that threatens all fading monsters, she learns that the bravest thing you can do is let go.
