# The Fragile Mind

**Author:** Barış Kısır
**Genre:** Psychological Fiction
**Model:** claude-sonnet-4-6
**Chapters:** 50

## Synopsis

Mara Elford is thirty-four and has been losing time for years. She wakes in parking lots, finds meals she didn't cook, reads journal entries in handwriting that isn't hers. When her sister insists she seek help, Mara enters the care of Dr. James Whitfield — and begins the extraordinary process of meeting the eight alternate identities she has been sharing her life with, unknown, since childhood.

There is Nell, the seven-year-old who lives in a remembered summer garden. There is Marcus, the furious teenager who has kept a terrible secret for twenty years. There is Sylvie, the quiet librarian who has been managing the system's information for decades. And there is Vera, who cannot speak but draws her way toward a door she has been approaching for years.

A novel about the mind's extraordinary capacity for self-protection, and the long work of coming home to a life that has always, in its way, been yours.
