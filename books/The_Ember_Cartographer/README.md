# The Ember Cartographer

## Overview

Cartographer Sera Halden is hired to revise imperial maps after a new crater opens on the island of Anaga. The old charts show a village officials insist never existed, while ashfall reveals roads, shrines, and survey stones buried by a previous eruption. Sera leads a small expedition through lava fields to recover the truth before the military uses the volcano as cover for annexation.
