# The Ember Cartographer Outline

## Premise

Cartographer Sera Halden is hired to revise imperial maps after a new crater opens on the island of Anaga. The old charts show a village officials insist never existed, while ashfall reveals roads, shrines, and survey stones buried by a previous eruption. Sera leads a small expedition through lava fields to recover the truth before the military uses the volcano as cover for annexation.

## Core Cast

- **Sera Halden** - a cartographer who believes a blank space is never innocent.
- **Tomas Aru** - an island guide whose family history was erased from official maps.
- **Dr. Celia March** - a volcanologist reading danger in tremors and glass.
- **Major Edwin Pike** - an imperial officer turning survey lines into claims.
- **Nico Vale** - a photographer whose plates catch evidence others miss.
