# The Miracle of Saint John's

## Inspirational Fiction

A crumbling inner-city church scheduled for demolition begins to repair itself overnight, sparking a battle between faith, science, and real estate.

---

## Story

Saint John's Catholic Church stands abandoned in the heart of a blighted neighborhood. The diocese has sold the property to a developer who plans to demolish it for luxury condominiums. But three days before the wrecking crew arrives, the church begins to repair itself overnight.

Cracked pews mend. Stained glass windows re-form from shards. The ruined altar gleams as if newly consecrated. Father Tom, the aging priest who watched his congregation dwindle to nothing, finds himself caught between Dr. Anika Kapoor, a structural engineer who insists there is a scientific explanation, and Julian Crosse, the developer who needs the church gone. As the repairs escalate and the city takes notice, each man must confront what he truly believes -- and what he is willing to sacrifice for it.

---

## Characters

- **Father Tom** — The aging priest of Saint John's who rediscovered his faith
- **Dr. Anika Kapoor** — A structural engineer and skeptic who cannot explain the phenomena
- **Julian Crosse** — The real estate developer who must choose between profit and wonder
- **Ezra** — A homeless man whose crippled hands are healed inside the church
- **Manny Reyes** — The faithful custodian who witnessed the first changes
