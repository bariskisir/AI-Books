## Description

Mara Venn is asked to review a forgotten court docket that releases Juniper Ridge from a conservation easement. The document would let a resort consortium clear the old forest before anyone can object.

The paper looks official, but the plants disagree. Pollen sits in the wrong fold, cultivated berries appear in an old envelope, and a survey call points to a boundary stone someone tried to bury. With an archivist, a retired surveyor, a beekeeper, and a nursery owner, Mara turns small natural facts into courtroom proof.
