# The Juniper Docket - Outline

## Premise

When a protected ridge is released from conservation by a newly discovered court docket, land-use attorney Mara Venn suspects the document was planted to let a resort consortium clear an old juniper forest. Her proof is not dramatic. It is botanical: pollen in a fold, a wrong survey nail, nursery-grown juniper berries, and a clerk's erased index number. As the county rushes toward a sale, Mara follows the paper trail back through adult witnesses who remember the ridge as common ground and forward to a developer willing to make old records disappear.

## Core Cast

- Mara Venn - land-use attorney and former field botanist.
- Elias Roake - retired county surveyor with failing patience and precise field books.
- Priya Sol - courthouse archivist who knows the docket room better than anyone.
- Tomas Vale - resort developer seeking title to Juniper Ridge.
- Leda Marsh - nursery owner whose cultivated junipers expose the forgery.
- Judge Ansel Reed - chancery judge determined to keep procedure clean.
- Ruth Fen - beekeeper and ridge neighbor.
- Niko Hale - title examiner who first finds the suspicious release.
