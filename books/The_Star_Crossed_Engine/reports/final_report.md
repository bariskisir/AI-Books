# The Star-Crossed Engine — Final Report

## Summary

The Star-Crossed Engine is a complete steampunk novel written for the AI-Books project. It follows Elara Vance, a principled airship engineer, and Silas Cog, a clockwork revolutionary, as they race to stop Lord Ironvale's superweapon, the Titan's Heart, from sparking a devastating empire-ending war.

## What Was Created

- **Manuscript:** 50 chapters, ~13,700 words, in literary spare prose
- **Outline:** Full chapter-by-chapter plan in five acts
- **Cover:** 1600x2560 PNG with steampunk-themed imagery (airship, Victorian cityscape, gears, metallic gradient)
- **EPUB:** EPUB 3 file with proper formatting, cover, and table of contents
- **Metadata:** JSON metadata with descriptions, character list, and keywords
- **Tools:** Custom cover generation and EPUB build scripts
- **Reports:** Final report, manifest CSV, and progress tracker

## Characters

- Elara Vance — Chief engineer, principled and precise
- Silas Cog — Clockwork revolutionary, scarred by Ironvale's ambition
- Lord Ironvale — Industrial magnate and antagonist
- Mina Harker — Elara's loyal apprentice
- Commander Voss — Ironvale's chief enforcer
- Professor Aris Thorne — Retired weapons theorist
- Kael Dorn — Undercity information broker
