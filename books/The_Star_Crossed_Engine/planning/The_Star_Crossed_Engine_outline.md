# The Star-Crossed Engine

## Premise

In the floating city of Aurelia, where brass dirigibles tether between clockwork towers, airship engineer Elara Vance discovers a catastrophic design flaw in the Titan's Heart -- a weapon engine commissioned by the powerful Lord Ironvale. When she refuses to sign off on its deployment, she is marked for elimination. Forced to flee into the undercity, she crosses paths with Silas Cog, a clockwork revolutionary who has been sabotaging Ironvale's operations for years. Together they must race against Ironvale's relentless enforcers to expose the weapon's true purpose: to spark an empire-ending war that would leave Ironvale as the sole power. Their journey takes them from the glittering spires of Aurelia to the grim foundries of the Rusted Earth, where the Titan's Heart awaits ignition.

## Core Cast

- **Elara Vance** -- Brilliant airship engineer, meticulous and principled. She trusts systems and blueprints over people, but her conscience will not let her sign away millions of lives.
- **Silas Cog** -- Clockwork revolutionary, part machine, part man. A former Ironvale mechanic who lost his arm to the Titan's Heart prototype and now fights with cunning and an explosive temper.
- **Lord Ironvale** -- Industrial magnate and power broker. Cold, calculating, and utterly convinced that only his iron hand can save the empire from itself.
- **Mina Harker** -- Elara's apprentice, loyal but torn between duty and survival.
- **Commander Voss** -- Ironvale's chief enforcer, a man who believes in order above all else.
- **Professor Aris Thorne** -- Retired weapons theorist who understands the Titan's Heart better than anyone alive.
- **Kael Dorn** -- Undercity scavenger and information broker, Silas's contact in the shadows.
- **The Council of Gears** -- Silas's revolutionary cell, a half-dozen broken men and women who want to tear down the old order.

## Chapter Plan

### Part I: The Blueprint

1. Elara Vance examines the Titan's Heart blueprints in her workshop and spots a miscalculation that would cause catastrophic power feedback.
2. Lord Ironvale visits the skydock to inspect progress, and Elara confronts him about the flaw.
3. Ironvale dismisses her concerns and orders her to proceed; she refuses, and he warns her of consequences.
4. Elara's apprentice Mina warns her that Ironvale's enforcers are coming; Elara flees her workshop with prototype schematics.
5. Elara escapes through the maintenance shafts of the upper city as Clockwork Constables close in.
6. She crashes through a market skylight and lands in the undercity, where scavengers strip her of valuables.
7. Silas Cog finds Elara bleeding in an alley and brings her to his hideout in an old boiler room.
8. Elara explains the weapon to Silas; he reveals he lost his arm to an earlier version of the same engine.
9. Silas introduces Elara to the Council of Gears in a candlelit basement beneath a clock tower.
10. The Council votes to help Elara sabotage the Titan's Heart, though Kael Dorn warns it is suicide.

### Part II: The Hunt

11. Commander Voss questions Mina about Elara's whereabouts; Mina lies but Voss sees through it.
12. Ironvale activates emergency powers, sealing all airship ports and ordering a citywide sweep.
13. Silas leads Elara through the steam tunnels to reach an old telegraph line to contact Professor Thorne.
14. They find the telegraph office guarded; Silas creates a diversion with a boiler explosion.
15. Elara sends a coded message to Professor Thorne, who lives in the remote Rusted Reach.
16. Voss's enforcers raid the undercity, burning Kael Dorn's market stalls and killing two of the Council.
17. Silas and Elara flee Aurelia aboard a stolen cargo dirigible, the Rust-Wing.
18. Mid-flight, they discover a tracking beacon hidden in the cargo; Silas disarms it with seconds to spare.
19. The Rust-Wing's engine fails over the Ash Flats; they make an emergency landing in the wasteland.
20. They repair the engine using salvaged parts from a wrecked automaton, arguing about the cost of their mission.

### Part III: The Rusted Reach

21. Professor Thorne receives Elara's message in his mountain observatory and begins calculating the weapon's failure points.
22. Silas and Elara cross the Ash Flats on foot, chased by a pack of feral clockwork hounds.
23. They take shelter in an abandoned mining town where Silas reveals how Ironvale took his arm.
24. Voss arrives at Thorne's observatory with a squad of enforcers, capturing the professor.
25. Thorne burns his research rather than let Voss take it, buying Silas and Elara time.
26. Silas and Elara reach the Rusted Reach and find Thorne's observatory in flames.
27. They rescue Thorne from a collapsed lower level; the professor is injured but alive.
28. Thorne reveals that the Titan's Heart can only be stopped by reversing its ignition sequence from inside the core.
29. Voss's enforcers surround the observatory; Silas rigs the structure to collapse as a distraction.
30. The three escape through a mining tunnel into the mountains, with Voss in pursuit.

### Part IV: The Iron Citadel

31. Ironvale moves the Titan's Heart to his fortified citadel at Mount Forge for final assembly.
32. Silas, Elara, and Thorne arrive at a resistance outpost in the mountains, where Thorne's former students work.
33. Elara designs a plan to infiltrate the citadel using a supply dirigible carrying coal for the forge.
34. Silas argues with Elara about sacrificing innocent workers in her plan; they reach a tense compromise.
35. They infiltrate Mount Forge disguised as coal stokers, witnessing the Titan's Heart being assembled.
36. The weapon is massive -- a sphere of interlocking brass rings with a furnace core that could level cities.
37. Voss captures one of Thorne's students, who reveals the infiltration plan under torture.
38. Ironvale orders the weapon readied for a demonstration; he plans to destroy a rebel city as proof of power.
39. Elara and Silas hide in the fuel bunkers, forced to wait a full day before the weapon's ignition.
40. Thorne creates a diversion by detonating a gas line in the eastern foundry, buying them time.

### Part V: The Reckoning

41. Elara reaches the Titan's Heart core chamber and begins reversing the ignition sequence.
42. Silas fights through the citadel's clockwork guards to reach the control room and override the safety locks.
43. Voss corners Elara in the core chamber; she stalls him by explaining the true destructive force of the weapon.
44. For a moment, Voss hesitates -- he sees the devastation the weapon would cause.
45. Ironvale arrives, shoots Voss for his hesitation, and prepares to manually ignite the weapon.
46. Silas reaches the control room and engages Ironvale in a brutal hand-to-hand fight across the machinery.
47. Elara completes the reversal but the core is already heating; she must physically jam a gear to stop the chain reaction.
48. Silas defeats Ironvale, throwing him into the furnace shaft, but the weapon is already overloading.
49. Elara jams the gear with a crowbar, cracking the core casing; superheated steam vents and the weapon fails.
50. Silas and Elara escape the collapsing citadel as Mount Forge erupts in steam. They emerge into dawn, the empire saved but forever changed. They part ways at the crossroads -- revolution and rebuilding are different trades.
