## Premise

1947, Madison County, North Carolina. Caleb Prater returns from the Pacific War to find his mountain hollow changed — his brother Eli is dead, a sawmill dominates the ridge, and the community is bound by a wartime covenant of land and blood. As Caleb presses for answers, he uncovers a secret pact made while he was fighting overseas, still enforced by the men who benefited from it.
