## Story Summary

A grief-stricken meteorologist and a free-diving instructor find each other on a remote Greek island during the quiet winter season. The novel follows their slow, reluctant connection as they navigate parallel losses and learn that healing together might be worth the risk.

## Characters

- Dr. Eleni Vakrinos — Greek-American meteorologist, late 30s, grieving her husband
- Nico Angelidis — Free-diving instructor, early 40s, grieving his dive partner
- Maria Petridou — Taverna owner, 60, the island's quiet matriarch
