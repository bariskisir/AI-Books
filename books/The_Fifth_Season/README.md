# The Fifth Season

**Genre:** Literary Romance
**Author:** Barış Kısır

## About

Dr. Eleni Vakrinos, a grief-stricken meteorologist haunted by her husband's death in a flood, retreats to the remote Greek island of Nissos during its empty winter season. She expects solitude and the clean data of winter weather patterns. She finds Nico Angelidis, a free-diving instructor who spends the off-season alone on the island, still grieving the loss of his dive partner to the sea.

Forced into proximity by Maria, the taverna owner who mothers them both, Eleni and Nico begin a slow, reluctant dance. Against the backdrop of the Aegean, during the season when tourists have gone and the island belongs only to the wind, they discover that some losses have no words.

## Characters

- **Dr. Eleni Vakrinos** — A Greek-American meteorologist in her late thirties, grieving the loss of her husband. Precise, guarded, and slowly learning to breathe again.
- **Nico Angelidis** — A free-diving instructor in his early forties, still searching for his lost dive partner. Patient, observant, afraid of the shore.
- **Maria Petridou** — The sixty-year-old owner of the only taverna open in winter. She feeds people because feeding is easier than speaking.
