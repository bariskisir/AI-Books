## Story

James has kept the lighthouse on Eilean Mor for twelve years since the car accident that killed his wife and daughter. When a storm strands journalist Caleb Ross on the island, the two men are forced into close quarters. Over three days, Caleb's investigation into keepers who walked into the sea forces James to confront his buried grief. The novel follows their journey through the storm and toward a reckoning.
