# The Perfect Neighbor — Final Report

## Summary

The Perfect Neighbor is a domestic thriller novel by Barış Kısır, following Claire Brennan as she discovers that her seemingly perfect neighbor Becca is a serial identity thief. The story traces Claire's journey from suspicious new mother to key witness in a federal case, culminating in her recovery and return to a normal life.
