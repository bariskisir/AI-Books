# The Last Cowboy — Outline

## Premise

Aging rodeo champion Wes Hardin, now in his late sixties, lives on the outskirts of Pinyon, Nevada — a dying town where the feed store closed and the high school graduated its last class of twelve. His son Sonny inherited the family ranch and a mountain of debt. A casino developer from Reno has been buying up parcels along the highway, and Sonny's ranch sits at the center of the last undeveloped stretch. Wes has not been on a bull in twenty years. But the Silver State Rodeo in Winnemucca is offering a forty-thousand-dollar purse. One ride. Eight seconds. That is all it would take.

## Core Cast

- **Wes Hardin** — Aging rodeo champion, former PRCA bull rider. Proud, stubborn, worn down by years but not broken. A man who measures his life in eight-second increments.
- **Sonny Hardin** — Wes's son. Forties. Practical, tired, loves the land but cannot make it pay. He carries the weight of the ranch and the weight of his father's legacy.
- **Marcus Webb** — The Developer. Polished, patient, professional. He has bought half of Pinyon County already. He wants Sonny's land for a casino resort.
- **Lena** — Sonny's wife. Grounded. She sees the numbers clearly. She loves Sonny but worries the ranch is a hole they will never climb out of.
- **Dale Parsons** — Wes's old rodeo partner. Runs the last feed store in Pinyon. He is the one person Wes still calls a friend.

## 50 Chapter Plan

### ACT I: THE DYING TOWN (Chapters 1–12)

1. **Dust and Silence** — Wes wakes before dawn on the Hardin ranch. The house is quiet. The land is dry. He walks the fence line and finds more posts down.
2. **The Kitchen Table** — Sonny and Lena go over the books. The numbers are red. The bank called again.
3. **Pinyon** — Wes drives into town. He sees what is left: boarded-up storefronts, a shuttered grocery, the old feed store barely hanging on.
4. **The Feed Store** — Wes visits Dale at Pinyon Feed & Grain. They drink coffee. Dale tells him Webb bought the old garage property.
5. **The Offer** — Marcus Webb arrives at the ranch in a clean sedan. He makes Sonny an offer. Sonny says no.
6. **Aftermath** — Sonny tells Wes about the offer. Wes says nothing. He walks out to the barn.
7. **The Rodeo Flyer** — Wes finds a flyer for the Silver State Rodeo tacked to a bulletin board at the feed store. Forty thousand dollars.
8. **The Numbers** — Lena shows Sonny the spreadsheet. If they sell, they can walk away clean. If they stay, they lose everything by winter.
9. **Webb's Pitch** — Webb takes Sonny to lunch. He paints a picture: a resort, jobs, a future for the town. Sonny listens.
10. **What Is Left** — Wes drives out to the old rodeo grounds outside town. The bleachers are rusted. The chutes are gone. He stands in the dust.
11. **The Body Remembers** — Wes finds his old rigging bag in the barn. He pulls on his glove. His hand still fits.
12. **A Quiet Decision** — Wes sits alone on the porch after dark. He makes up his mind.

### ACT II: THE PREPARATION (Chapters 13–25)

13. **The Lie** — Wes tells Sonny he is going to help Dale at the feed store. He drives to Elko instead.
14. **The Stock Contractor** — Wes finds Buck Crenshaw, a stock contractor who still remembers him. Buck has a practice bull.
15. **First Try** — Wes gets on the bull. He lasts one second. His back screams. He hits the dirt hard.
16. **Dale Knows** — Dale figures it out. He does not try to stop Wes. He offers his barn for practice.
17. **The Arena** — Dale clears his back lot. They build a makeshift chute from scrap lumber and steel pipe.
18. **Soreness** — Every morning Wes is stiff. He does stretching exercises in the dark. He does not tell anyone.
19. **The Young Bull** — Buck brings a younger, tougher bull. Wes gets thrown. He gets up. He gets back on.
20. **Lena's Doubt** — Lena notices Wes is gone more. She asks Sonny. Sonny shrugs it off.
21. **Phone Call** — Webb calls Sonny. The offer has a deadline. Sonny says he needs more time.
22. **The Old Hands** — Dale invites two old cowboys to watch Wes ride. They say nothing. They nod.
23. **Second Wind** — Wes stays on for three seconds. Then four. His rhythm is coming back.
24. **What Sonny Sees** — Sonny sees Wes loading hay bales into his truck. He does not ask why.
25. **Six Seconds** — Wes rides a practice bull to the whistle. He climbs off shaking. Dale claps him on the back.

### ACT III: THE GATHERING STORM (Chapters 26–38)

26. **The Deadline** — Webb sends a formal letter. Two weeks. Then the offer drops by half.
27. **Sonny's Burden** — Sonny walks the ranch at night. He cannot sleep. He cannot decide.
28. **The Route** — Wes maps the drive to Winnemucca. Four hours. He tells Dale he is going.
29. **Gear** — Wes drives to Reno to buy a new vest and a mouth guard. The kid at the tack shop does not know who he is.
30. **Registration** — Wes registers for the rodeo. The clerk reads his name twice. She asks his age.
31. **The Roster** — The event list goes online. Dale prints it. Wes is the oldest man entered by twenty-five years.
32. **Lena Finds Out** — Lena sees the rodeo roster on Dale's counter. She confronts Wes.
33. **The Fight** — Lena tells Sonny. Sonny drives to the feed store. He and Wes yell at each other in the parking lot.
34. **What Lena Says** — Lena talks to Wes alone. She tells him Sonny does not need a dead father. Wes tells her he needs to try.
35. **The Town Hears** — Word spreads through Pinyon. People Wes has known for forty years look at him differently.
36. **Webb Watches** — Webb hears about the old man planning to ride. He does not understand. He files it away.
37. **The Night Before** — Wes packs his truck. Dale helps him load the gear. They do not say much.
38. **Departure** — Wes leaves at dawn. The sky is pink and gold. He does not look back.

### ACT IV: THE RIDE (Chapters 39–50)

39. **Winnemucca** — Wes arrives at the rodeo grounds. The arena is big and loud. He feels small.
40. **The Draw** — Wes draws a bull named Dust Devil. Eight years old. No one has ridden him this season.
41. **Dale Arrives** — Dale shows up in the stands. He did not tell anyone he was coming.
42. **Waiting** — Wes sits in the locker room. He tapes his hand. He breathes.
43. **Sonny and Lena** — Sonny makes a decision. He and Lena get in the truck. They drive north.
44. **The Rookie** — A young rider talks to Wes in the chute area. He tells him he is crazy. Wes almost smiles.
45. **The Chute** — Wes climbs onto the chute. Dust Devil shifts beneath him. The gate is all that separates them.
46. **The Nod** — Wes nods. The gate swings open. Dust Devil explodes into the arena.
47. **Eight Seconds** — Wes rides. The world becomes motion and noise and the animal beneath him. He hears the whistle.
48. **The Landing** — Wes dismounts. He hits the ground. His knee buckles. The medics run.
49. **The Number** — The score comes up. Sixty-eight points. It is enough. It might be enough.
50. **Home** — Wes wakes in a hospital bed. Sonny is there. Lena is there. Dale is there. The ranch is saved.
