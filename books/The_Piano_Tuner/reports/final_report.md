## Premise

Mr. Silas Croft, a blind piano tuner, is summoned to the Ashworth house on Royal Crescent where a grand piano plays on without a pianist. The player, William Ashworth, is dead — yet the instrument produces his compositions each night. Croft uncovers a story of forbidden love, a dismissed maid named Catherine, and a mother's cruel interference. The piano is not haunted. It is speaking — playing the unfinished confession William could not make in life.
