## Synopsis

A man searches for his wife who vanished from a locked hotel room. The only clue: a Beatles song she hummed the night before. The investigation reveals she had a twin sister, a hidden past, and a secret she carried for decades. The novel follows Tom Caldicott's journey from baffled husband to reluctant detective to a man who must learn to let go.
