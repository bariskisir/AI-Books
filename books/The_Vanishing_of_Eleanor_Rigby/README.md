# The Vanishing of Eleanor Rigby

**Genre:** Psychological Mystery  
**Author:** Barış Kısır  
**Status:** Complete (TXT + EPUB + Cover)

## Description

Tom Caldicott and his wife Eleanor check into the historic Hotel Adair for a weekend getaway. In the morning, she is gone. The door was locked from inside. The chain was on. Security footage shows nothing. No sign of struggle. No forced entry.

The only clue: the night before, Eleanor hummed the Beatles song "Eleanor Rigby."

As Detective Margaret Harris works the case, Tom discovers his wife had secrets he never imagined: a second phone, a hidden past, and a twin sister she thought was dead. The search for Eleanor Rigby becomes a search for the woman Tom never truly knew.
