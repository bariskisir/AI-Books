# The Clockwork Rabbi — Final Report

## Summary

The Clockwork Rabbi is a complete historical fantasy novel by Barış Kısır, set in 1592 Prague. The story follows Rabbi Judah Loew ben Bezalel, who creates a golem of clay and clockwork to protect the Jewish ghetto from blood libels and religious violence. The creature, named Yossele by the rabbi's daughter Esther, develops a soul and consciousness, leading to a crisis of faith and morality as Emperor Rudolf II and the Vatican Inquisition seek to control or destroy it.
