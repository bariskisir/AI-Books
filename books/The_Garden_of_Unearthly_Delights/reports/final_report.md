## Characters

- **Iris Vasquez** — Bio-artist. Former genetic researcher. Creates living sculptures from illegally modified DNA.
- **Agent Elara Cross** — Senior regulator, Bio-Regulation Bureau. Methodical, principled, increasingly conflicted.
- **The Collector** — Wealthy patron of forbidden bio-art. He acquires pieces by any means necessary.

## Plot Summary

In a near-future where the Genetic Integrity Act prohibits unauthorized gene-editing, Iris Vasquez runs a hidden studio growing living sculptures. When Agent Elara Cross discovers her work, she is ordered to destroy the garden. A wealthy patron known as the Collector wants to own Iris's art at any cost. As Iris runs from both the law and the Collector's men, she creates her most ambitious piece yet — a flying chimeric moth with a nervous system capable of learning. The regulator and the artist become fugitives, forced to choose between the law and the beauty they have been ordered to destroy.
