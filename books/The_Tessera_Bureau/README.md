# The Tessera Bureau

**Author:** Barış Kısır
**Genre:** Speculative Fiction
**Subgenre:** Parallel Worlds / Institutional Thriller
**Model:** sonnet-4.6
**Chapters:** 50

## Synopsis

A grief-hardened reconciler in a city long ago fractured into parallel versions of itself discovers that someone inside her own bureau is deliberately collapsing the worlds together — and that the architect's reasons are unbearably close to her own.

The city of Halloway tessellated long ago — fractured into several adjacent versions of itself, near-identical cities occupying the same ground, divided by a thin membrane called a seam. Most people live and die in one pane, never knowing the others exist. The Tessera Bureau is the unglamorous civil service that keeps the panes from bleeding into one another, and its field agents, the reconcilers, are licensed to cross between adjacent worlds to repair the leaks: a person who wandered across, an object that duplicated, a death that did not match its neighbor.
