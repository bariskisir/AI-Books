## Synopsis

When legendary perfumier Violette Lasserre dies in her Grasse atelier, her estranged granddaughter Céline Moreau — a forensic chemist from Lyon — inherits a locked cabinet containing a cipher notebook and a single glass vial labeled *N° Inconnu*. As Céline decodes the double-layered cipher, she discovers that the notebook does not contain perfume formulas at all: it encodes the location of a Renaissance reliquary stolen from a Roman convent during the Sack of 1527 and protected by a chemical authentication system called Lacrima Sancti. The trail leads from Grasse's archives to a decaying Florentine palazzo, the Apuan Alps above Carrara, and a dynasty that has guarded the reliquary for five centuries — and will kill to keep it hidden.
