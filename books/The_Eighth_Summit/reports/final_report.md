## Characters

1. Vera Sorensen — Renowned alpinist, forty-six, who returns to the peak that killed her partner a decade ago
2. Daniel Quayle — Charismatic expedition leader, paid by the sponsor to deliver a summit by a date
3. Tenzin Norgyel — Lead sirdar who was with Jonas when he died and carries his own grief
4. Mara Lindqvist — Producer who is really verifying a product for the sponsor, untrained for the height
5. Owen Reyes — Ambitious young climber whose father ran the expedition that fixed the fatal rope
6. Priya Anand — Blunt expedition doctor, the team's conscience on the math of survival
7. Jonas Holt — Vera's partner, dead ten years on the summit ridge, present in memory
8. Aerolex — The sponsor, a gear company testing an unproven cold-weather suit it must launch
