# The Eighth Summit

**Genre:** Mountaineering Drama
**Author:** Barış Kısır
**Status:** Complete (txt + epub available)

## Synopsis

Ten years after a storm on the Himalayan peak Khumri killed her climbing partner Jonas, the renowned alpinist Vera Sorensen accepts an invitation to return — a small, well-funded expedition led by a charismatic stranger and bankrolled by a gear company with reasons of its own. Vera climbs seven of the world's highest mountains and turned her back on this eighth one for a decade. Now she walks back into the thin air to set down a grief she has carried too long.

As the team works up the mountain, Vera learns the expedition is a trap built of incentives: a 'documentary' that is really a product verification, a summit deadline tied to a launch date rather than the weather, an unproven heating suit that fails in the cold, and a young teammate whose father fixed the rope that killed Jonas. When the storm arrives a day early and pins the climbers near the top, old guilt, the sponsor's hidden agenda, and the brutal arithmetic of altitude force a series of impossible choices about who can move, who must be carried, and who is brought down alive.

## Characters

- **Vera Sorensen** — Renowned alpinist, forty-six, who returns to the peak that killed her partner a decade ago
- **Daniel Quayle** — Charismatic expedition leader, paid by the sponsor to deliver a summit by a date
- **Tenzin Norgyel** — Lead sirdar who was with Jonas when he died and carries his own grief
- **Mara Lindqvist** — Producer who is really verifying a product for the sponsor, untrained for the height
- **Owen Reyes** — Ambitious young climber whose father ran the expedition that fixed the fatal rope
- **Priya Anand** — Blunt expedition doctor, the team's conscience on the math of survival
- **Jonas Holt** — Vera's partner, dead ten years on the summit ridge, present in memory
- **Aerolex** — The sponsor, a gear company testing an unproven cold-weather suit it must launch
