# The Eighth Summit — Outline

## Premise

Ten years after a storm on the Himalayan peak Khumri killed her climbing partner, renowned alpinist Vera Sorensen accepts an invitation to return — a small, well-funded expedition led by a man she barely knows and bankrolled by a sponsor with reasons of its own. As the team works up the mountain, Vera learns the expedition is not what it was sold as: a film crew that isn't a film crew, a summit deadline tied to money rather than weather, and a teammate who was on the mountain the day her partner died. When a storm pins them near the top, old guilt, the sponsor's hidden agenda, and the brutal arithmetic of altitude force a series of impossible choices about who is brought down and who is left.

## Core Cast

- **Vera Sorensen** — Forty-six, Norwegian-American alpinist, once famous, now a guide and teacher. Returns to Khumri to close a wound she has carried for a decade. Cold, exact, watchful.
- **Daniel Quayle** — Expedition leader, charismatic, hired by the sponsor. Competent on rock, but driven by a contract and a clock rather than the mountain.
- **Tenzin Norgyel** — Sirdar and lead climbing Sherpa. Knows Khumri better than any of them; carries his own grief from the old disaster.
- **Mara Lindqvist** — "Documentary" producer who is really there to verify a product for the sponsor. Honest in the end, unprepared for the height.
- **Owen Reyes** — Young, strong, ambitious climber. Was a teenager on Khumri base camp the year Vera's partner died; his father ran that earlier expedition.
- **Priya Anand** — Expedition doctor, blunt, the team's conscience on the math of survival.
- **Jonas Holt** — Vera's partner, dead ten years on Khumri's summit ridge. Present throughout in memory.
- **The Sponsor (Aerolex)** — A gear company testing an unproven cold-weather system; wants a summit on film to launch it.

## 50-Chapter Plan

### Act I — The Invitation and the Approach (1–12)
1. Vera receives the offer in a Colorado climbing gym; the name Khumri stops her.
2. She visits Jonas's sister; the old guilt surfaces; she decides to go.
3. Kathmandu: meeting Daniel, Mara, Owen, Priya; first unease.
4. The flight to Lukla; the mountain shows itself; Vera's body remembers.
5. Trek begins; Tenzin recognizes her; an unspoken history between them.
6. A village rest; Owen lets slip he was at Khumri base camp as a boy.
7. Vera confronts the memory of the day Jonas died, on the trail.
8. Base camp; the gear is strange — Aerolex prototypes, sealed cases.
9. Mara's questions reveal she is verifying a product, not making a film.
10. Daniel lays out an aggressive schedule tied to a launch date.
11. First acclimatization rotation; the mountain tests them; small failures.
12. A near-miss in the icefall; the team's fault lines show.

### Act II — Higher (13–26)
13. Camp One established; Vera and Tenzin talk about the old storm.
14. Owen pushes too hard; Vera reads ambition like weather.
15. Priya warns Daniel the schedule ignores acclimatization.
16. A weather window forecast; Daniel commits the team to a fast push.
17. Vera finds Jonas's old fixed line, frayed, still on the route.
18. Camp Two; the Aerolex suits perform — and conceal a flaw.
19. A storm scare; they retreat; trust frays.
20. Rest at base; Mara confesses the sponsor's full agenda to Vera.
21. The contract: summit by a date or the funding — and lives risked — collapse.
22. Tenzin tells Vera what really happened the day Jonas died.
23. Vera realizes Owen's father cut a corner that killed Jonas.
24. Owen and Vera collide; the past detonates.
25. A second window opens, narrow; Daniel forces the decision.
26. The team commits to the summit push against Priya's counsel.

### Act III — The Push and the Storm (27–40)
27. Up through the icefall again, faster, thinner air.
28. Camp Two in failing light; one prototype suit malfunctions.
29. Camp Three on the shoulder; the summit a day away.
30. Night at altitude; Vera and Tenzin keep watch; the cold deepens.
31. Pre-dawn start; headlamps on the ridge; Jonas everywhere in memory.
32. The Aerolex heating system fails on Mara; frostbite begins.
33. Daniel pushes on; Priya demands they turn Mara around.
34. The split: a turnaround party and a summit party.
35. Owen summits first; the moment is hollow.
36. Vera reaches the top — the eighth summit she swore she'd never seek.
37. On the summit, Vera finds a relic of Jonas; grief, quiet and exact.
38. The storm arrives early and total; descent becomes survival.
39. Whiteout on the ridge; the rope, the wind, the arithmetic.
40. A fall; someone is hurt below the Hillary-like step.

### Act IV — The Arithmetic and the Descent (41–50)
41. Daniel injured; the team must choose who can move and who carries.
42. Tenzin's strength against the mountain; the long lowering.
43. Mara's frostbite worsens; the suit's failure laid bare.
44. Vera's impossible choice: the slow injured man or the fading window.
45. Owen redeems his name; carries beyond his strength.
46. A bivouac in the open; the night that decides everything.
47. Dawn count; who is left, who is brought down — restrained, grief over spectacle.
48. The icefall in collapse; the last descent.
49. Base camp; the reckoning with Aerolex and Daniel.
50. After: Vera, lighter and heavier both; the mountain unchanged; she goes home.
