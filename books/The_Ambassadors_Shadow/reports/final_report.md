# The Ambassador's Shadow — Final Report

## Summary

The Ambassador's Shadow is a political thriller novel set in the United Nations headquarters in New York. It follows Ana Kallan, a junior diplomat who discovers a conspiracy within the Security Council to manufacture a false-flag attack and trigger a cascading world war. With 50 chapters and approximately 10,000 words, the manuscript traces her investigation from a single suspicious codename to a full-blown international crisis involving five permanent members of the Security Council.

## Characters

- **Ana Kallan** — Junior diplomat, UN Department of Political and Peacebuilding Affairs
- **Mikhail Volkov** — Former SVR intelligence officer, burned and seeking redemption
- **Director Chen Wei** — Director of the UN Office of Internal Oversight
- **Ambassador Youssef Hamidi** — Permanent Representative of Algeria, Ana's mentor
- **Viktor Sorokin** — Russian Ambassador to the UN, smooth and dangerous
- **James Hollings** — US Deputy Permanent Representative, compromised
- **Daria Kuznetsova** — SVR officer, Volkov's former protégée
- **Secretary-General Lindström** — UN Secretary-General, hemmed in by realpolitik
- **Claude Renard** — French diplomat, Ana's colleague and friend
- **Fatima Diallo** — ICC prosecutor

## Key Themes

- Institutional betrayal and the machinery of diplomacy
- The gap between the ideals of international law and the realities of power
- Ordinary people making extraordinary choices within broken systems
