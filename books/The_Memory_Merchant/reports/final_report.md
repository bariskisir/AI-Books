## Characters

1. Mara Veil — A licensed memory broker in her late thirties who has sold most of her own happiness to survive losing her husband
2. Otto Renn — A mild civil engineer who buys a stranger's memory while hunting for his missing brother
3. Daniel Veil — Mara's late husband, an archivist with a hidden past in the early memory trade
4. Pell — Mara's young, loyal, sharp-eyed assistant, scarred by her own mother's erasure
5. Inspector Sana Cole — A patient detective from the Bureau of Recollection tracing an extraction ring
6. Lou Vesper — A charming, dangerous back-alley distiller who cuts and resells illegal memories
7. Halloran — The smooth, complicit owner of the brokerage chain Mara contracts under
8. Edie — An elderly regular who sells her dead sister's memory one sip at a time
