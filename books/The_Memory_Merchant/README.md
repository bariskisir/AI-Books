# The Memory Merchant

**Genre:** Speculative Fiction
**Author:** Barış Kısır
**Status:** Complete (txt + epub available)

## Synopsis

In the city of Sable, memories can be drawn out of the mind, distilled into a pale fluid, bottled, and sold. Mara Veil is a licensed broker who has spent six years numbing her grief by quietly selling off her happiest recollections of her late husband, Daniel — keeping the facts of him, letting go of the warmth. Her private shelf is nearly bare when a soft-spoken client named Otto Renn brings her a bottle he bought legally and asks her to verify it is real. Inside the recording is a stranger's last afternoon: a yellow kitchen, an argument, a man falling. And at the edge of the frame, half cut away, a grey-haired woman in a doorway whose face Mara knows the way she knows her own.

To learn what she did, Mara must buy herself back one piece at a time — from collectors, from a back-alley distiller, from the chain that owns her license — reassembling an afternoon someone paid to scatter and a year she paid to forget. What she finds is not the crime she feared but a harder truth about the man she married, the death he hid, and the mercy he died trying to give her. When the broker who buried it all offers to pour her whole spent life back into her in exchange for her silence, Mara must weigh warmth against truth, and decide which she can stand to live inside.

## Characters

- **Mara Veil** — A licensed memory broker in her late thirties who has sold most of her own happiness to survive losing her husband
- **Otto Renn** — A mild civil engineer who buys a stranger's memory while hunting for his missing brother
- **Daniel Veil** — Mara's late husband, an archivist with a hidden past in the early memory trade
- **Pell** — Mara's young, loyal, sharp-eyed assistant, scarred by her own mother's erasure
- **Inspector Sana Cole** — A patient detective from the Bureau of Recollection tracing an extraction ring
- **Lou Vesper** — A charming, dangerous back-alley distiller who cuts and resells illegal memories
- **Halloran** — The smooth, complicit owner of the brokerage chain Mara contracts under
- **Edie** — An elderly regular who sells her dead sister's memory one sip at a time
