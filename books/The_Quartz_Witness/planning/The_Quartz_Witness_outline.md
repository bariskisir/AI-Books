# The Quartz Witness - Outline

## Premise

Forensic geologist Dr. Mira Vale is asked to authenticate a quartz sample in a mining arbitration between a mountain town and a lithium company. The company claims the disputed vein lies inside its legal claim and that a fatal tunnel collapse was a natural fault. Mira reads the crystals differently: saw marks, pressure shadows, swapped core boxes, and a silent seismic station point to a staged sample chain meant to steal a spring-fed ridge and bury negligence under mineral language.

## Core Cast

- **Dr. Mira Vale** - Forensic geologist hired as a neutral expert, precise in the field and skeptical of elegant reports.
- **Jonas Pike** - Former mine surveyor who knows the old adits and carries guilt over signing a bad map.
- **Leona Senn** - Town solicitor fighting to keep the spring ridge outside the lithium claim.
- **Calder Rusk** - Regional director of Aster Lithium, polished, patient, and comfortable with controlled facts.
- **Inez Quill** - Core shed technician who notices box seals and handwriting better than executives do.
- **Dr. Owen Marr** - Company petrologist whose thin sections are accurate until they are inconvenient.
- **Talia Reed** - Seismic station operator whose missing log becomes the hinge of the case.
- **Bram Noll** - Rescue captain from the collapsed tunnel, blunt and tired of technical excuses.
- **Suri Voss** - Hydrologist tracing the spring system beneath the quartz ridge.
- **Judge Elian Arda** - Arbitration judge who demands physical proof over rhetoric.

## 50 Chapter Plan

### Part I - The Sample

1. **White Vein** - Mira arrives to examine a contested quartz specimen before arbitration begins.
2. **Claim Line** - Leona explains the town's spring ridge and the company's expanded claim.
3. **Core Shed** - Inez shows Mira sealed boxes whose labels do not match the drill plan.
4. **Old Surveyor** - Jonas admits the historic mine map was copied from a damaged plate.
5. **Pressure Shadows** - Mira sees crystal growth that contradicts the company's origin story.
6. **First Hearing** - Calder presents a clean technical narrative to the judge.
7. **Thin Section** - Owen's slide shows an inclusion that should not be in the sample.
8. **Rescue Mark** - Bram points Mira toward the tunnel wall where the collapse began.
9. **Water Taste** - Suri proves the spring carries minerals from the disputed ridge.
10. **Chain of Custody** - Mira finds a time gap in the sample transfer log.

### Part II - The Ridge

11. **Silent Station** - Talia's seismic station has a blank hour on the collapse night.
12. **Quartz Dust** - Dust in a company vehicle carries sawed rather than drilled grains.
13. **The Wrong Tray** - Inez discovers a core tray photographed in reverse order.
14. **Aster's Offer** - Calder offers the town a settlement that would surrender the ridge.
15. **Fault Language** - Owen defends the natural-fault theory with careful evasions.
16. **Adits Below** - Jonas leads Mira into an old tunnel beneath the ridge.
17. **Spring Room** - Suri maps underground water that crosses the claim line.
18. **Filed Late** - Leona finds the expansion filing was timestamped after the collapse.
19. **Broken Prism** - Mira identifies a broken quartz face matching a missing core.
20. **Stay Denied** - The judge lets drilling preparations continue under strict monitoring.

### Part III - Substitution

21. **Night Inventory** - Inez and Mira audit the shed and find a substituted box.
22. **Green Tags** - The genuine core tags were moved to a barren interval.
23. **Seismic Copy** - Talia recovers a backup that records a blast-like pulse.
24. **Company Road** - Bram shows tire tracks from the restricted ridge gate.
25. **Crystal Memory** - Mira explains how quartz strain records pressure history.
26. **Owen's Margin** - Owen left a correction note out of the final report.
27. **Hydrograph** - Suri's water chart shows the spring dropped during the hidden work.
28. **Witness Bench** - Mira's first testimony is attacked as academic speculation.
29. **Drill Sound** - Jonas hears machinery at an abandoned adit after midnight.
30. **Second Collapse** - A small controlled failure exposes fresh saw cuts in the wall.

### Part IV - Proof

31. **Exclusion Zone** - Calder closes the ridge under a safety order.
32. **Back Door** - Leona obtains a limited inspection warrant through the arbitrator.
33. **Cairn and Cable** - Mira finds a buried seismic cable near an old boundary cairn.
34. **The Missing Meter** - A drill core is one meter short where the vein should be.
35. **Inclusion Trail** - The rare inclusion matches a lower tunnel, not the ridge sample.
36. **Owen Breaks** - Owen admits his original slide contradicted the company claim.
37. **Black Water** - Suri detects drilling additive in the town spring.
38. **Locked Shed** - Inez protects the genuine core boxes from removal.
39. **Judge on the Ridge** - The arbitrator orders an in-person view of the evidence.
40. **Quartz Witness** - Mira aligns the broken prism, saw marks, and seismic pulse.

### Part V - The Line Restored

41. **Emergency Order** - Drilling is stopped while the sample chain is investigated.
42. **Calder's Map** - Calder's private map shows the real vein outside the claim.
43. **Bram's Men** - Rescue workers testify about the tunnel's unnatural fracture.
44. **Talia's Hour** - The blank seismic hour is restored in court.
45. **Leona's Clause** - Leona uses the original water covenant to protect the ridge.
46. **Aster Recedes** - The company withdraws its expansion to save the larger permit.
47. **Jonas Corrects** - Jonas files a corrected survey with the old plate attached.
48. **Spring Hearing** - The town wins interim protection for the water system.
49. **White Ridge** - Mira returns the genuine quartz to the public evidence room.
50. **The Quartz Witness** - The final ruling treats the crystal record as testimony.
