## Description

London, 1921. Thomas Gale, a cynical former stage magician turned investigator for the London Rationalist Society, is dispatched to expose Madame Seraphina, a rising medium whose séances have captivated wealthy grieving families still reeling from the Great War. But when a séance attendee dies in a locked room, Thomas discovers that Seraphina herself is terrified — convinced that something genuine and predatory has latched onto her performances. As the body count rises and Scotland Yard circles closer, Thomas must confront the possibility that a fraud can summon a truth she never believed in.
