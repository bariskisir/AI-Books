## Premise

1873, Tuamotu Archipelago, French Polynesia. Hina Aroha, the daughter of a Tahitian mother and a French trader father, has been diving the lagoon of Avatoru since she was nineteen. When a French survey party arrives to chart the archipelago's pearl beds, she must navigate between the maps that would expose the lagoon's richest resources and her community's desperate need for trade revenue.
