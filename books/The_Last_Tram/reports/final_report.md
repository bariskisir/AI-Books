# The Last Tram — Final Report

## Summary

*The Last Tram* is a slipstream novel completed for the AI-Books project. It tells the story of Jorge Mendes, a Lisbon tram driver whose midnight route passes through a version of the city frozen in 1955, where he falls in love with a woman who does not know she has been dead for sixty years.
