# The Evergreen Promise

A holiday literary fiction novel set in the Pacific Northwest.

## Story

The Hartwell family's Christmas tree farm, Evergreen Acres, has anchored the town of Fir Haven for over a hundred years. When a development corporation arrives with an offer Maggie Hartwell cannot refuse — and her sons cannot accept — the family fractures along fault lines decades in the making. Through the annual Christmas market, the town council fight, a late-night vigil in the barn, and a community rally that restores their faith, the Hartwells must confront the question of what a legacy is really worth.

## Characters

- **Margaret 'Maggie' Hartwell** — Matriarch and farm owner, 67
- **David Hartwell** — Eldest son, corporate attorney, 44
- **Thomas Hartwell** — Younger son, farm manager, 38
- **Eleanor Hartwell** — David's daughter, journalist, 26
- **Sarah Hartwell** — David's wife, landscape architect, 42
