# The Beekeeper's War

A WWI Literary novel by Barış Kısır

## About

Sgt. Thomas Bell, a village beekeeper from Sussex, serves on the Western Front in 1917. Before the war he kept thirty hives on the South Downs. Now he tends a small apiary behind the reserve lines, producing honey for the wounded and sending jars home to his wife, Clara. But Clara has stopped writing back.

Between bombardments, gas attacks, and the daily horror of the trenches, Bell tends his bees and writes letters that go unanswered, haunted by the silence from home while the war devours everything around him.
