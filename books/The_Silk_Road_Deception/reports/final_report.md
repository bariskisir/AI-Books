## Main Characters

- Dr. Arthur Everett — British archaeologist, 43, Oxford-trained
- Leyla Demir — Turkish guide and translator, 34, raised along the Silk Road
- Vasily Volkov (The Merchant) — Russian antiquities dealer, antagonist

## Plot Summary

Dr. Everett uncovers the Jade Seal of Samarkand near Kashgar. The seal is stolen by Volkov. Everett hires Leyla Demir to guide him across the Taklamakan Desert, through the Bone Pass, and into the Ferghana Valley. They pursue Volkov through Samarkand and Bukhara, uncovering a web of deception involving forgery and the black market. Leyla reveals Volkov was her father's business partner who betrayed him. A confrontation in a cave leads to Volkov's capture. Everett returns the seal to the British Museum but is changed by the journey. Leyla rides east to reclaim her father's fortune.
