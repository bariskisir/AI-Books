# The Elevator

**Genre:** High-Stakes Thriller  
**Author:** Barış Kısır  
**Status:** Complete (txt, epub, cover)

## Synopsis

Twelve strangers board a midtown Manhattan elevator on a Tuesday morning. Between floors, the car shudders to a halt. The control panel resets to a single red display: 59:59 and counting down. One of the twelve is wearing a bomb vest. Maya Kessler, a former FBI hostage negotiator now working corporate security, recognizes the pattern immediately. With no working phones, thinning oxygen, and a timer that does not pause, she must identify the bomber, keep eleven panicking strangers alive, and find a way out before the numbers hit zero.
