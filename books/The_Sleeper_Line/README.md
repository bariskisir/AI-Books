# The Sleeper Line

A Lovecraftian Horror novel by Barış Kısır.

Night-shift maintenance worker Elias Voss discovers an unscheduled train at Platform 9 of Grand Central Terminus, its interior lined with living black fungus. This triggers a chain of revelations connecting the Wyrmwood Transit Authority's subway grid to a dormant cosmic entity buried beneath the city.

50 chapters. 25,004 words. Complete.
