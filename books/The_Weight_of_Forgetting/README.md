# The Weight of Forgetting

## About

**Genre:** Psychological Thriller / Amnesia Suspense

**Model:** deepseek-v4-flash

Claire Ashworth wakes from a car accident to discover seven years of her life have vanished. She is told she is married to Julian Mercer, a neuropsychologist she does not recognize, and that she co-owns an architecture firm she never built. As she navigates a world she supposedly created but cannot remember, a cold-case detective arrives asking about a woman named Eleanor Vance who disappeared four years ago. Claire's search for her missing years leads her to blueprints she does not recall drawing, a buried truth beneath a greenhouse, and the terrifying realization that the man beside her bed may be the reason she cannot remember at all.

## Characters

- Claire Ashworth
- Dr. Julian Mercer
- Detective Sarah Holt
- Marcus Bell
