# The Weight of Forgetting

## Premise

Claire Ashworth wakes from a car accident to discover seven years of her life have vanished. She is told she is married to Julian Mercer, a neuropsychologist she does not recognize, and that she co-owns an architecture firm she never built. As she navigates a world she supposedly created but cannot remember, a cold-case detective arrives asking about a woman named Eleanor Vance who disappeared four years ago. Claire's search for her missing years leads her to blueprints she does not recall drawing, a buried truth beneath a greenhouse, and the terrifying realization that the man beside her bed may be the reason she cannot remember at all. She must recover not only her past but the strength of the woman she became â€” a woman brave enough to investigate her own husband while faking normalcy, and resourceful enough to survive what she found.

## Setting

Present-day New England. The novel unfolds across a midsize coastal city, a suburban house on Bellflower Lane, a cold-case detective's office, a rural property ninety miles inland, and a county courthouse. The world is contemporary America â€” smartphones, surveillance, forensic science â€” but Claire's interior landscape is one of temporal dislocation, where every familiar object carries an unfamiliar history.

## Core Cast

### Claire Ashworth
- **Role:** Protagonist
- **Description:** A 38-year-old architect who suffers retrograde amnesia covering the past seven years of her life. Her last intact memory is being 31, engaged to a carpenter named Marcus Bell, and working at a small firm. She must reconstruct who she became during the lost years while navigating a marriage she does not remember, a career she never planned, and a dangerous truth buried in her own erased mind.

### Dr. Julian Mercer
- **Role:** Antagonist
- **Description:** A 42-year-old neuropsychologist and Claire's husband of six years. To the outside world, he is a devoted, patient caregiver managing his wife's recovery with grace. Beneath the calm surface lies a man who controls Claire's environment, her medications, and her access to the truth â€” and who has a violent secret the lost-years Claire was dangerously close to exposing.

### Detective Sarah Holt
- **Role:** Ally
- **Description:** A 45-year-old cold-case detective who has spent four years investigating the disappearance of Eleanor Vance. She believes Claire is not only a witness but the key to solving the case. Pragmatic, relentless, and protective of Claire once she recognizes the pattern of coercion, Holt becomes Claire's most trusted ally as the truth emerges.

### Marcus Bell
- **Role:** Ally
- **Description:** A 39-year-old carpenter and Claire's former fiancÃ© from seven years ago. He represents the life Claire remembers wanting â€” simple, honest, grounded. His shock at learning she has amnesia is genuine, and the version of their breakup he tells contradicts Julian's account. ## Supporting Characters

- **Dr. Rachel Okonkwo:** Independent neurologist who treats Claire and privately flags evidence of memory manipulation through medication
- **Lena Crawford:** Claire's neighbor of five years who reveals that Claire had become withdrawn and fearful in the months before the accident
- **Eleanor Vance:** Julian's former partner who disappeared four years ago; her recorded testimony naming Julian as her abuser becomes the key piece of evidence
