# The Clockwork Estuary — Outline

Lio Mercer returns to Vey to repair the tidal clock that keeps the estuary city habitable. A hidden log reveals the clock has begun prioritizing property values over people. With barge mechanic Ansel, archivist Mara, and an undocumented shoreline community, Lio uncovers the political code buried by her father and mentor. During a king tide, she must choose between preserving the city wall and giving every resident a voice in the machine that governs the water.
