## Summary

The Milkman's Son is a 50-chapter Irish literary novel set in 1980s Dublin. The story follows Declan O'Brien, a scholarship boy from the Liberties, through one year as he navigates poverty, shame, faith, and first love. His father, a milkman who still uses a horse-drawn float, is the silent center of a story about class, family, and the hard work of becoming yourself.
