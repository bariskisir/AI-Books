## Characters

1. Nora Ellery — Sous-chef at Saltmarsh; precise, watchful, and loyal to the kitchen if not always to the man who ran it. The investigator.
2. Daniel Reyes — Head chef and part-owner; brilliant, exacting, and secretly cheapening his own famous broth. The victim.
3. Imogen Vale — Front-of-house manager and Daniel's business partner, cut out of a sale she helped build.
4. Theo Brandt — Hot-tempered fish cook, passed over for promotion, a thief of small things but no killer.
5. Pavel Novak — Quiet pastry chef who logs everything in a careful notebook.
6. Sadie Quinn — Young commis chef and Nora's protégée, frightened she has covered for the wrong person.
7. Marcus Friel — Lead investor who wants Saltmarsh sold and the review ruined.
8. Lena Coyle — Rival restaurateur from Porthcove, once Daniel's partner and the true author of the saffron broth.
9. DS Geraldine Okafor — A tired, fair detective willing to listen if Nora gives her reason.
10. Hassan Reyes — Daniel's estranged brother and saffron supplier.
