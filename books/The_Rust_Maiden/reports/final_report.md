# The Rust Maiden — Final Report

## Summary

The Rust Maiden is a complete dieselpunk novel set in an alternate 1930s Dust Bowl transformed by giant war machines. The story follows Scout March, a mechanic who discovers that her salvaged walker Rusty carries the downloaded consciousness of Lena Voss, a test pilot killed to protect a secret project. Together they wage war against Baron Hargrave and his empire of oil and iron.

## What Was Created

- **Outline:** 50-chapter plan with premise, core cast, and chapter summaries
- **Manuscript:** 50 chapters, approximately 11,800 words, literary but spare prose
- **Metadata:** Full JSON metadata with descriptions, character list, and keywords
- **Cover:** 1600x2560 PNG cover with walker silhouette, mechanic figure, rust gradient
- **EPUB:** Valid EPUB 3 file with cover, title page, navigation, and all chapters
- **README:** Book summary with cover and download link
- **Reports:** Summary, manifest, and progress tracking
