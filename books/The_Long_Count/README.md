### About

Ray "The Hammer" Mason was a contender once, a decade ago, before the losses piled up and the booze blurred the years. Now pushing forty in 1970s Detroit, he works an assembly line and sleeps in a walk-up apartment. When his old trainer Sal surfaces with an improbable opportunity, Ray steps back into the gym for one last shot at the middleweight championship.

The road back is brutal. Ray's body rebels, his daughter refuses to see him, and his ex-wife carries scars he put there. Standing across the ring is Tony "The Tiger" DeMarco, the undefeated champion at the peak of his powers. But the fight that matters most is the one Ray has been losing for a decade: the battle to become a man worth remembering.
