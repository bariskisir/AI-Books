# The Long Count — Final Report

## Summary

The Long Count is a sports drama novel set in 1970s Detroit, following Ray "The Hammer" Mason, a washed-up middleweight boxer who gets one last shot at the championship. The story covers his journey from the assembly line to the ring, his fight with undefeated champion Tony "The Tiger" DeMarco, and the harder battle to rebuild his life and relationships outside the ropes.
