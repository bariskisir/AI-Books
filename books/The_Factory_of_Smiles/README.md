# The Factory of Smiles

**A Dystopian Satire by Barış Kısır**

In the gleaming city of Optima, every citizen must earn their smile quota through mandatory happiness shifts at the state-run Factory of Smiles. Joy Mendes, a model worker on Assembly Line Seven, has spent three years packaging smile-cubes for export to the outer districts. When she discovers that the happiness she handles is chemically harvested from human emotions — piped directly from the brains of non-compliant citizens kept in the factory's hidden basement — she must decide between the comfort of compliance and the cost of the truth.

With the help of the Underground, Joy plans to expose the Ministry of Happiness during the annual Day of Gratitude broadcast. But Supervisor Voss, the grim overseer of the factory floor, has his own reasons for letting her find the truth: one of the subjects in the basement is his sister.
