## Core Cast

- **Wren** — Twelve-year-old girl, observant and quiet, sent to her aunt's cottage for the summer while her parents separate.
- **Kit** — Fourteen-year-old boy who navigates the estuary as his private kingdom; his father died in a fishing accident.
- **Aunt Margie** — Wren's maternal aunt, a potter who lives alone on the coast and understands the value of silence.

## Setting

A small island off the coast of Maine, centered on a tidal estuary — a landscape of channels, mudflats, marsh grass, and hidden ponds that change shape twice daily with the tides.

## Structure

The novel is divided into five parts of roughly ten chapters each:

1. **The Incoming Tide** (Ch 1-10) — Wren's arrival, her first encounters with the estuary and Kit.
2. **The Estuary's Grammar** (Ch 11-20) — Learning to read the tides, the creatures, the rhythms of the marsh.
3. **The Full Moon Tide** (Ch 21-30) — Discovery of the hidden pond, deepening friendship, revelations about loss.
4. **The Turning** (Ch 31-40) — The summer's end approaches; Kit reveals his family is moving; Wren finds her voice.
5. **The Ebb** (Ch 41-50) — Goodbyes, return home, and the understanding that some things end but are not lost.
