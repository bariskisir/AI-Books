# The Deep Between Stars — Outline

## Premise

At the floor of the Mariana Trench, the Hadal-7 research station monitors abyssal biology at pressures that crush steel. Dr. Elena Reyes, a benthic ecologist, notices seismic patterns that do not match tectonic models — rhythmic pulses rising from deeper than any known geological process. Captain Dmitri Orlov, the station's commander, dismisses her concerns until the station's power grid begins failing in sequence, and the ocean itself starts to respond to something waking below. What has waited millions of years in the Challenger Deep is rising, and it is not indifferent.

## Core Cast

- **Dr. Elena Reyes** — Benthic ecologist, analytical, persistent, 38
- **Captain Dmitri Orlov** — Station commander, pragmatic, weathered, 52
- **The Chthonic Entity** — The presence beneath the trench

22. Orlov authorizes emergency ascent; the thrusters fail to engage.

40. The creature circles the station, sending probing vibrations.
41. Reyes detects a second signature — the creature is not alone.

43. Orlov uses emergency thrusters to drag the station along the trench wall.
44. The creatures pursue, collapsing the canyon behind the station.
