# The Night Market — Final Report

## Summary

A complete magical realism novel set in Oakland, California, about a young man named Tomas who discovers a mysterious market that appears only on full moons. The market sells impossible things: bottled dreams, woven memories, second chances. Tomas must navigate its rules, uncover his family's connection to it, and decide how much he is willing to give to save it from fading away.
