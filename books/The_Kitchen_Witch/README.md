# The Kitchen Witch

**Genre:** Cozy Fantasy  
**Author:** Barış Kısır  
**Status:** Complete (txt + epub)

## Blurb

Hazel Marrow has spent fifteen years wandering from town to town, using her gift as a truth-witch to expose corruption and move on. But when she arrives in the mist-shrouded village of Ashcombe, she decides to stay. She rents the old confectionery shop on Market Lane and begins baking pies with a peculiar magic: whoever eats one cannot tell a lie.

Her pies crack the village wide open. The mayor has been stealing from the village fund. The baker's boy is hiding from a crime he did not commit. A young mother's husband did not leave — he is buried in the mayor's cellar. As secrets spill like hot filling, Hazel must decide whether her gift is a weapon or a balm.

*The Kitchen Witch* is a cozy fantasy about community, courage, and the difference between forcing the truth and inviting it.
