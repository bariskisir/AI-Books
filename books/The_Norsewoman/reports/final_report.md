## Prose Notes

The novel is written in a literary but spare style appropriate to the setting — short declarative sentences, concrete sensory detail, dialogue with distinct character voices. No lyrical refrains are repeated across chapters. Each chapter advances character and plot. The five-act structure moves from commission through investigation to confrontation and ultimately public performance of the memorial at the thing.
