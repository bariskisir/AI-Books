# The Basalt Testament - Outline

## Premise

On a volcanic island, probate investigator Nora Vale is asked to verify a late fisherman's testament that gives a basalt quarry and shoreline commons to a luxury developer. The signature appears sound, but the attached stone witness sample comes from the wrong lava flow. With a geologist, a harbor clerk, a quarry mason, and a village doctor, Nora follows cooling fractures, tide ledgers, ash layers, and funeral records to prove the testament was assembled after the man's death to steal the island's common shore.

## Core Cast

- Nora Vale - probate investigator with a talent for material evidence.
- Dr. Selen Arda - island geologist and lava-flow mapper.
- Tomas Reed - harbor clerk who keeps tide and landing ledgers.
- Mira Holt - quarry mason whose family cut basalt for generations.
- Cal Rusk - developer's attorney.
- Father Iven Marr - parish priest who recorded the funeral.
- Ada Pike - village doctor who treated the fisherman.
- Leif Orin - nephew of the dead fisherman and first suspect.
