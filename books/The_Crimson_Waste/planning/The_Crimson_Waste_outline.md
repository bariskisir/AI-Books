# The Crimson Waste - Outline

## Premise

Zara, a disgraced swordswoman exiled from the city of Kel-Vash for a crime she did not commit, is hired to steal the Red Dhara, a polished stone the size of a fist that pulses with heat and whispers at night. The theft goes wrong when a demon bound to the stone awakens and begins tracking her across the Crimson Waste, a desert of red sand stretching seven hundred miles to the sanctuary of the Sunken Temple. The Inquisitor, a fanatical priest who claims the stone for his god, follows with a company of assassins. Zara must deliver the relic or die in the sand.

## Core Cast

- **Zara** - A swordswoman in her thirties, stripped of rank and name, carrying a long blade and a shorter one. She trusts no one and regrets everything.
- **The Red Dhara (the relic)** - A fist-sized crimson stone that radiates heat, pulses with a low hum, and speaks to whoever carries it. It wants to be returned to the Sunken Temple.
- **The Inquisitor** - A gaunt man in black leather, former priest of the Temple of Scales. He speaks to his god through blood and silence. He wants the stone for the ritual of binding.

## Chapter Plan

1. Zara accepts a job from a veiled woman in a crowded tavern.
2. She breaks into the Temple of Scales and steals the Red Dhara.
3. The stone whispers its first words inside her pack as she flees.
4. The Inquisitor discovers the theft and begins the ritual of naming.
5. Zara reaches the city gate and buys passage with a sand cart trader.
6. The merchant convoy enters the Crimson Waste at dusk.
7. The stone burns through her pack and she wraps it in wet cloth.
8. The Inquisitor dispatches three assassins on swift riding lizards.
9. Zara dreams of a sunken temple beneath a red sky.
10. The first assassin attacks at the dawn watch and Zara kills him.
11. The merchant turns against her after finding blood in the cart.
12. Zara walks alone into the Waste with one waterskin.
13. The stone shows her a vision of the demon's face.
14. The second assassin catches her at a dry well and she wounds him.
15. She finds a half-collapsed shrine and shelters for the night.
16. The demon pulls tendrils of smoke from the stone and takes form.
17. Zara fights the demon in the dark and drives it back with iron.
18. The Inquisitor arrives at the shrine and reads her tracks.
19. Zara discovers the stone grants her glimpses of hidden paths.
20. She finds a spring of brackish water and refills her skin.
21. The third assassin uses a blowpipe but misses in the wind.
22. Zara turns and kills him with a thrown knife.
23. The Inquisitor performs the rite of binding and collapses a mile of dune.
24. Zara digs herself out of the sand, bleeding from the nose.
25. The stone guides her toward a canyon the merchants never used.
26. She finds bones of previous carriers in the canyon floor.
27. The demon finds her again, larger now, and tears her blade arm.
28. Zara binds the wound with torn cloth and keeps walking.
29. The Inquisitor sends a sandstorm through the canyon.
30. She shelters in a cleft of rock and talks to the stone for answers.
31. The stone admits it was forged by the Sunken Temple to contain a star.
32. Zara learns the demon is the star's shadow, not the star itself.
33. She decides to reach the temple before the Inquisitor catches her.
34. The demon attacks at midnight and she stabs it through the mouth.
35. The wound leaks light and the demon recoils, diminished.
36. Zara walks forty miles without water, hallucinating.
37. The Inquisitor catches up and they face each other at a stone bridge.
38. They duel across the bridge; Zara loses two fingers on her left hand.
39. The Inquisitor takes the stone and binds it to his chest with wire.
40. Zara follows at a distance, now unarmed and half-blind with thirst.
41. The stone speaks to the Inquisitor and he begins to doubt his god.
42. Zara picks up a fallen assassin's blade and stalks them.
43. The demon abandons the stone and possesses the Inquisitor instead.
44. Zara fights the possessed Inquisitor at the temple entrance.
45. She drives her blade through his chest and the demon escapes as smoke.
46. The stone falls silent, cold for the first time in centuries.
47. Zara enters the Sunken Temple and places the stone on the altar.
48. The temple seals itself behind her, trapping her inside.
49. She finds an exit through a flooded tunnel and emerges in a green valley.
50. Zara walks south toward a new name and a life without the stone.
