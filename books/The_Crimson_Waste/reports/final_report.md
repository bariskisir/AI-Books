# The Crimson Waste - Final Report

## Summary

The Crimson Waste is a complete Sword and Sorcery novel by Barış Kısır. It tells the story of Zara, a disgraced swordswoman who steals the Red Dhara, a relic that contains a piece of a fallen star and its shadow demon. She must cross the Crimson Waste to return the stone to the Sunken Temple while pursued by assassins, a fanatical Inquisitor, and the demon itself.
