# The Orchid Thief of Calcutta

**A Colonial Adventure by Barış Kısır**

In 1870s India, British botanist James Whitby hears whispers of a legendary flower hidden deep in the Assam jungles — the Fire Orchid, said to bloom once every seven years with petals like living embers. He hires Priya Mukherjee, a brilliant Bengali naturalist who knows the hills better than any European and carries a terrible secret: her husband died searching for the same flower.

Racing against Marcel Dubois — the Collector, a ruthless French aristocrat with unlimited resources — James and Priya navigate fever-haunted jungles, treacherous cliffs, and their own growing trust. The orchid is not merely a prize. It is a test of what they are willing to sacrifice.

## Characters

- **James Whitby** — British botanist, Royal Horticultural Society fellow. Methodical, earnest, carrying colonial assumptions. Yearns for a career-defining discovery.
- **Priya Mukherjee** — Bengali naturalist and guide. Widow of botanist Anjan Mukherjee. Sharp-tongued, patient, deeply knowledgeable about native flora.
- **Marcel Dubois** — The Collector. French orchid hunter. Aristocratic, charming, ruthless.
