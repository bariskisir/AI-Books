## Story Structure

The novel follows a three-act structure:

- **Act I - Calcutta (Chapters 1-12):** James receives the letter, meets Priya, and departs for Assam. The Collector is introduced.
- **Act II - The Journey (Chapters 13-35):** The expedition travels through the Khasi Hills, encounters dangers (leopard, fever, landslide), reaches the orchid's valley, and confronts Dubois.
- **Act III - The Orchid (Chapters 36-50):** Storm escape, the orchid's rescue, Dubois's redemption, and James's return to Calcutta with a cutting.
