# The Vellum Engine - Outline

## Premise

Patent archivist Elian Mott is asked to authenticate a vellum drawing that would transfer ownership of an old calculating engine and the factory built around it. The drawing looks antique, but its scrape marks, gear ratios, ink flow, and calfskin preparation point to a modern substitution. As investors move to sell the machine to a private collector, Elian follows workshop ledgers, worn cams, estate seals, and a hidden service notebook to prove that the true design belongs to a workers' trust.

## Core Cast

- Elian Mott - patent archivist and machine historian.
- Mira Saye - estate solicitor trying to keep the sale lawful.
- Otto Brann - retired machinist who maintained the engine.
- Celia Voss - vellum conservator.
- Jarin Pike - investor's agent pushing the sale.
- Dr. Iona Bell - mathematics historian.
- Thom Kest - factory trustee accused of losing the patent file.
- Judge Mara Quill - probate judge overseeing the estate dispute.
