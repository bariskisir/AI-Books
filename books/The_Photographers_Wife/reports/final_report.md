## Premise

In 1957 Tokyo, Margaret Finch lives in the orbit of her husband Jack, a celebrated American war photographer whose work made him famous but whose silence has hollowed out their marriage. Jack was in the Pacific from 1942 to 1945 and came home with a trunk of undeveloped film he has never printed. When Margaret discovers the trunk, she begins to develop the film at a local photo lab run by Sato, a Japanese technician who lost his family in the war. The images she uncovers reveal a photograph of American soldiers looting the belongings of the dead — and Sato recognizes a kimono in the image as belonging to his missing daughter Yuko. Margaret must confront the man she married and the cost of looking away.
