# The Gilded Menagerie

**Genre:** Circus Historical
**Author:** Barış Kısır
**Status:** Complete (txt + epub available)

## Synopsis

In the spring of 1911, the Hollis & Crane Combined Shows opens its season under a patched canvas big top. Nell Sparrow, a trapeze artist who came to the tent as a runaway and never left, learns that the show's generous, beloved owner, August Crane, has been quietly mortgaging the circus's future to the Apex Amusement Syndicate — a combine that buys failing shows for their routes, wagons, and animals, then dissolves them.

As the season grinds across muddy Midwestern towns through rain and dwindling crowds, Nell and Silas Roe, the show's taciturn animal handler, piece together what Crane has signed away — including Juno, the old elephant who is the heart of the menagerie. Together with a fortune-teller, a gruff boss canvasman, and a flying partner tempted by the syndicate's money, they wager everything on a benefit season to buy back their own paper.

## Characters

- **Nell Sparrow** — A fearless trapeze artist raised in orphan poverty before the circus took her in
- **Silas Roe** — The quiet animal handler who speaks more easily to the old elephant than to people
- **August Crane** — The aging, beloved owner and ringmaster, drowning in debt and shame
- **Cornelius Pratt** — The Apex Syndicate's smooth, patient, ruthless advance man
- **Madame Lorca** — The fortune-teller and de facto den mother, sharp-eyed and loyal
- **Tom Bellamy** — Nell's flying partner and catcher, ambitious and tempted by Apex
- **Juno** — The show's elderly Asian elephant, Silas's charge and the heart of the menagerie
