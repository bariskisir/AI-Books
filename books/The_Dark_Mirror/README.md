# The Dark Mirror

**Author:** Barış Kısır
**Genre:** Psychological Fiction
**Model:** claude-sonnet-4-6
**Chapters:** 50

## Synopsis

Dr. Cara Lund has spent two decades learning to read people. When Edmund Hale walks into her Chicago practice — charming, articulate, and apparently eager for self-knowledge — she believes she has seen his type before. She has not.

Over months of twice-weekly sessions, Cara begins to notice that Edmund's polished self-awareness may be a performance, that his accounts of victimhood never quite align with independent reality, and that his presence is spreading through her marriage and social world with a precision she cannot yet name.

A spare, searching novel about covert narcissistic exploitation within the therapeutic frame — and the slow work of recovering clarity after a skilled assault on perception.
