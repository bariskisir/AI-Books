# The Radio Operator

**Genre:** War Romance  
**Author:** Barış Kısır  
**Status:** Complete (txt + epub)

## About

A WAC radio operator in 1944 England falls in love with a voice on the encrypted line — a German resistance fighter on the other side of the war.

Sergeant Hannah Markham works the night shift at a covert signals station in Hampshire, relaying intelligence between Allied command and resistance networks across occupied Europe. When she picks up a faint signal from a man who calls himself Grey Wolf, a German signals officer secretly feeding information to the French Resistance, she begins a correspondence that will change both their lives.

Through months of coded transmissions, private ciphers, and fragments of Rilke poetry, Hannah and Dieter build an impossible love across enemy lines. But when D-Day approaches and Dieter's cover begins to fray, they must risk everything for a chance to meet face to face.
