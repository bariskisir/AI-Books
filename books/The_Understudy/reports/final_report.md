## Premise

Corina Vance has spent three years as the understudy for Lena Voss, Broadway's reigning star, in a sold-out psychological thriller called *The Hollow Hour*. Night after night she watches from the wings as Lena receives standing ovations for a role Corina knows she could play better. When a mysterious backstage accident leaves Lena hospitalized with a fractured skull, Corina finally gets her curtain call. But as she steps into the spotlight, she begins to suspect that the attack was meant for her — and that someone is still watching from the shadows, waiting for the understudy to fall.

## Core Cast
