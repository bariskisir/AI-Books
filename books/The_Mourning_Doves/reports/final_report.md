# The Mourning Doves — Final Report

**Genre:** Gothic Romance
**Author:** Barış Kısır
**Status:** Complete (TXT + EPUB + Cover)

## Summary

A complete gothic romance novel of 50 chapters set on the Cornish coast. The story follows Eleanor Morwen, a young widow who travels to her late husband's crumbling estate, Penhallow Grange, to uncover the truth of his death. She encounters a cold mother-in-law, a haunted groundskeeper, a cursed artifact carved in the shape of a dove, and the restless ghost of Elias Morwen himself.

## Word Count

~16,000 words across 50 chapters.
