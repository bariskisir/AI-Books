# The Unwritten City

![Cover](covers/The_Unwritten_City.png)

**Genre:** Political Utopia

In the city of Annua, all laws expire at midnight on the winter solstice and are rewritten by citizen assemblies — but when political analyst Elara Venn discovers that a faction has learned to manipulate the assemblies through procedural capture, she must expose the corruption without breaking the democratic process itself.
