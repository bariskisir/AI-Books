## Premise

In the iron-grey city of Gearwright, every soldier is wound like a clockwork automaton. The Winder implants brass heart-keys into conscripts at age sixteen. These cylinders wind weekly, eroding will and replacing it with engraved orders. Master Ticker, the finest clockmaker in Gearwright, has spent forty years repairing these mechanisms without question — until his former apprentice returns as Sergeant Brass, a soldier whose eyes still hold flickers of the boy who loved music. Ticker discovers the seventh gear: a mechanism that can either enforce obedience or unlock freedom. But every act of defiance starts a hundred-day countdown to death.
