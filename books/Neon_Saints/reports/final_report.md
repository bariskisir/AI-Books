# Neon Saints — Final Report

## Summary

Neon Saints is a complete cyberpunk novel by Barış Kısır. Set in the megacity of Prospect, it follows Null, a neural hacker who discovers a backdoor in the Architect's control system that could free every citizen from their neural implants — or destroy them all.
