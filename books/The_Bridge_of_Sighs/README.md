# The Bridge of Sighs

A Venetian Mystery by Barış Kısır

## Synopsis

Dr. Eleanor Chase, a British art historian specializing in Venetian Renaissance painting, arrives in Venice to authenticate a newly discovered Tintoretto sketch. She soon realizes the sketch contains a hidden code — a pentimento revealing a 500-year-old conspiracy buried within the official paintings of the Doge's Palace. The Doge's propaganda paintings were designed to glorify Venice, but beneath the gilded surface lies a systematic cover-up of a secret treaty, a murdered Doge, and a fortune in stolen gold.

With Commissario Bianchi of the Venetian art crimes unit and a mysterious restorer named Marco Valieri, Eleanor must decode the hidden geometry and expose a conspiracy that has shaped Venetian history for five centuries.

## Characters

- **Dr. Eleanor Chase** — British art historian, meticulous and intuitive
- **Commissario Paolo Bianchi** — Venetian police, art crimes division
- **Marco Valieri** — The Restorer, descendant of a Renaissance painter
- **Antonio Donato** — Wealthy Venetian patron, head of the conspiracy
- **Elena Morosini** — Archivista at the Venetian State Archives
- **Luca Ferrante** — Donato's lieutenant and enforcer
