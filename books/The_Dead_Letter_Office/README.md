# The Dead Letter Office

An Epistolary Mystery

Arthur Finch sorts undeliverable mail in the dead letter office of the Central Post Office — letters addressed to nowhere, to the departed, to people who never existed. Then he opens an envelope postmarked next Thursday and finds a letter describing events that haven't happened yet. More follow. A catastrophe is coming. The letters might be the only warning.
