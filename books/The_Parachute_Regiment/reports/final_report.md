# Final Report: The Parachute Regiment

## Summary

The Parachute Regiment is a complete 50-chapter war drama novel set during the D-Day invasion of Normandy in June 1944. The story follows Sergeant Tom Miller, Private Leo Flynn, and Lieutenant James Ashford as their company of British paratroopers is scattered across the wrong drop zone and must fight to secure a vital bridge against heavily fortified enemy positions.
