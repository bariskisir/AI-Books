# Zero Day Protocol — Final Report

## Summary

Zero Day Protocol is a techno-thriller novel written by Barış Kısır. The story follows Maya Chen, a cyber-security analyst at the National Cyber Security Center, who discovers a sophisticated zero-day exploit embedded in the nation's power grid control systems. With only 72 hours before the exploit triggers a cascading blackout across the Eastern Seaboard, Maya must race against time to trace the attack's origin and stop it.
