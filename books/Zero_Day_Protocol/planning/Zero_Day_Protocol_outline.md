# Zero Day Protocol — Outline

## Premise

Maya Chen, a cyber-security analyst at the National Cyber Security Center, discovers a sophisticated zero-day exploit embedded deep within the SCADA systems controlling the nation's power grid. The exploit, planted by a hacker known only as "Ghost," is set to activate in 72 hours, triggering a cascading failure that would plunge the entire Eastern Seaboard into darkness. With General Reeves of Cyber Command breathing down her neck and Ghost always one step ahead, Maya must race against time to trace the exploit's origin, understand its full scope, and find a way to stop it before the countdown reaches zero.

## Core Cast

- **Maya Chen** — Senior cyber-security analyst, National Cyber Security Center
- **Ghost** — Elusive hacker, real name Jake Parsons
- **General Marcus Reeves** — Head of U.S. Cyber Command
- **Director Sarah Okonkwo** — Director of the NCSC
- **Dmitri Volkov** — Russian intelligence asset, Ghost's handler

## Chapter Plan

1. Detection — Maya finds anomalous code in a routine SCADA log review.
2. Verification — She confirms it is a zero-day exploit embedded in the grid.
3. Alert — Maya reports to Director Okonkwo, who escalates to Cyber Command.
4. The Briefing — General Reeves arrives with a grim assessment of the threat.
5. Countdown — Ghost's timer is discovered: 72 hours until activation.
6. The Signature — Maya traces the code pattern to a known hacking group.
7. Deep Dive — She begins reverse-engineering the exploit structure.
8. The First Clue — A hidden IP address surfaces in the code comments.
9. Ghost's Call — Maya receives an encrypted message from Ghost directly.
10. The Ransom — Ghost demands fifty million dollars in cryptocurrency.
11. The Debate — Reeves wants to go public; Okonkwo argues for secrecy.
12. Two Paths — Maya splits efforts: trace the payment and trace the code.
13. The Dead End — The IP leads to a compromised university server in Ohio.
14. A Second Look — Maya discovers a secondary payload in the exploit code.
15. The Cascade — She simulates the attack. The damage is worse than projected.
16. The Mole — A leak inside the NCSC alerts Ghost to their progress.
17. The Purge — Ghost remotely wipes the compromised server, erasing evidence.
18. Starting Over — Maya must find a new angle from scratch.
19. The Underground — She reaches out to a former hacker contact.
20. The Whisper — The contact knows Ghost's real alias on the dark web.
21. The Profile — Maya and the team build Ghost's psychological profile.
22. Mobility — Analysis shows Ghost is operating from inside the country.
23. The Trail — They trace Ghost's communications to a Chicago hub.
24. The Team — Reeves deploys a field team to Chicago.
25. The Safehouse — The team stakes out a suspected location in Pilsen.
26. The Raid — They hit the safehouse. It is empty. Ghost is gone.
27. The Trap — Maya realizes Ghost wanted them to go to Chicago.
28. The Distraction — The Chicago operation was a deliberate feint.
29. The Real Target — Ghost is operating from Washington D.C.
30. The Connection — Maya finds Ghost's D.C. handler in the data.
31. Volkov — Dmitri Volkov, Russian intelligence, is the handler.
32. The Geopolitics — Russia is testing U.S. infrastructure vulnerabilities.
33. The Ultimatum — Ghost contacts Maya with personal threats.
34. Fighting Back — Maya deploys a counter-intrusion tool against Ghost.
35. The Backdoor — She finds an opening into Ghost's remote system.
36. The Trace — She traces Ghost's active connection in real time.
37. The Location — Ghost is in a secure apartment two miles from the White House.
38. The Standoff — Reeves wants to move in; Maya wants more intel first.
39. The Clock — Twelve hours remain on the countdown.
40. The Decision — Reeves authorizes the move on Ghost's location.
41. The Confrontation — Maya faces Ghost in person.
42. The Revelation — Ghost is Jake Parsons, a former NCSC colleague.
43. The Motive — Jake was fired after a whistleblower incident and wants revenge.
44. The Key — Maya must extract the kill-switch code from Jake.
45. The Negotiation — Maya tries to reason with her former friend.
46. The Countdown — Three hours left. The team attempts to brute-force the code.
47. The Compromise — Maya offers Jake immunity in exchange for the code.
48. The Choice — Jake gives them the code under duress.
49. The Code — Maya discovers a fourth-layer obfuscation. Final puzzle.
50. The Resolution — Maya cracks the final layer with forty-seven seconds remaining.
