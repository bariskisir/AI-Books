# The Watcher on the Wall — Final Report

## Summary

A complete military sci-fi novel by Barış Kısır. Private Elias Kane is stationed at The Spire, a remote fortress on the frozen planet Ostrac, where he watches the Barrens for signs of the Hush — an alien species that has been silent for fourteen years. When peculiar occurrences begin inside the fortress, Kane suspects the enemy is already within the walls. As the Hush mimic threat spreads through the structure, Kane discovers the fortress was built on top of an alien nest, and the walls meant to keep the enemy out were actually keeping something in.

## Characters

- **Private Elias Kane** — A sharp-eyed soldier who requested the most remote posting available. He watches, notices, and trusts no one.
- **Commander Marcus Holt** — Veteran of the last Hush war. Rigid, respected, burdened by what he knows. Runs The Spire with iron discipline.
- **The Entity** — The Hush central node beneath The Spire. A collective intelligence that absorbs, mimics, and replaces.
- **Corporal Reyes** — A field surgeon and survivor who hides during the collapse and helps Kane escape.
