# The Moscow Protocol — Final Report

## Summary

The Moscow Protocol is a complete 50-chapter spy thriller novel, written by Barış Kısır. The story follows disgraced MI6 officer Alex Gray as he is sent to Moscow for a routine dead drop that turns into a conspiracy implicating his own intelligence service. With FSB Captain Irina Volkov as his unlikely ally, Gray uncovers a secret operation called the Moscow Protocol — an off-the-books program of black sites, corrupt alliances, and targeted eliminations.
