# The Blacktop Requiem

**Genre:** Mystery / Thriller
**Setting:** Desert highways, truck stops, and small towns across the American Southwest (Nevada, Arizona, New Mexico)
**Model:** deepseek-v4-flash
**Status:** Complete (TXT + EPUB)

## Synopsis

Investigative journalist Maya Kessler arrives in rural Nevada to document a string of hitchhiker disappearances along a remote desert highway corridor. While researching, she picks up a disoriented amnesiac woman she calls Soledad, who was found wandering the shoulder with no memory and a primal fear of trucks. As Maya digs deeper, she discovers a pattern of vanishings spanning decades and multiple states, all connected to a rogue trucking network called the Corridor. With the help of a retired trucker, a desert tracker, and a conflicted local deputy, Maya unravels a conspiracy involving human trafficking, a long-buried DEA operation called Nightbird, and the sheriff himself — a former agent who turned his badge into a shield for the syndicate.

## Characters

- **Maya Kessler** — Investigative journalist from Austin, Texas
- **Soledad / Elena Vargas** — Amnesiac survivor, former immigration reporter
- **Sheriff Danforth Holloway** — Corrupt sheriff, former DEA agent
- **August "Gus" Theroux** — Retired trucker with a guilty conscience
- **Deputy Rick Molina** — Conflicted local deputy
- **Elias "Coyote" Yazzie** — Desert tracker
- **Lena Okonkwo** — Maya's editor
