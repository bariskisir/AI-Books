# The Frequency of Truth

**Genre:** Detective / Cold War Conspiracy  
**Author:** Barış Kısır  
**Model:** deepseek-v4-flash  
**Status:** Complete (TXT + EPUB)

A detective novel set in Port Ferris, a fog-shrouded coastal city in the Pacific Northwest. When a man is found dead inside a locked radio room with cryptic numbers on the wall, Detective Sarah Chen recognizes the markings from her past as a signals intelligence analyst. The numbers are a broadcast from a clandestine numbers station, and the killer is using ghost frequencies to orchestrate a countdown to a catastrophic revelation.
