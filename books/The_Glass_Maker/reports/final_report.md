# The Glass Maker — Final Report

## Summary

The Glass Maker is a historical fiction novel set in 1658 Venice. Matteo Dandolo, a master glass blower on Murano, has discovered a technique for producing ruby-red glass of unprecedented purity. When the Inquisition learns of his secret, they send Father Ambrogio to investigate. Matteo must choose between surrendering his family's secret — the culmination of a lifetime's work — and risking everything to protect it. His assistant and muse, Elena Morosini, becomes his unlikely savior.
