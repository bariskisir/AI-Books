# The Bone Garden — Final Report

## Summary

The Bone Garden is a medical thriller novel about a CDC epidemiologist racing to stop a genetically modified fungal pathogen that dissolves human bone from within. The story follows Dr. Lena Cross as she investigates a mysterious death, discovers a corporate cover-up by AeternaGen biotech, and develops a phage therapy to save the infected. The novel explores themes of corporate greed, military secrecy, scientific integrity, and the ethical boundaries of genetic engineering.

## What Was Created

- **Manuscript:** 50 chapters, ~10,400 words in `txt/The_Bone_Garden.txt`
- **Outline:** 50-chapter plan in `planning/The_Bone_Garden_outline.md`
- **Metadata:** JSON file with title, author, synopsis, and character list
- **EPUB:** EPUB 3 file with cover image, title page, and all 50 chapters
- **Cover:** 1600x2560 PNG with biohazard imagery, petri dish, and clinical design
- **Tools:** build_epub.py (EPUB generation) and create_cover.py (cover generation)
- **README:** Book description with download link
- **Reports:** This report, manifest.csv, progress.md
