# Red Phoenix Rising — Final Report

## Summary

Red Phoenix Rising is a 50-chapter superhero novel written as part of the AI-Books project. It tells the story of Dominic Cross, a retired superhero who once protected Iron City as Phoenix, forced back into action when a brilliant arsonist called the Inferno begins systematically burning the city's landmarks.

## Characters

- **Dominic Cross / Phoenix** — Retired superhero, now a firefighter Captain
- **Detective Elena Park** — Iron City PD detective investigating the Inferno
- **Leo Marchetti / The Inferno** — Scarred arsonist seeking to burn the city
- **Marcus Webb** — Dominic's former sidekick
- **Captain Lorraine Voss** — Dominic's fire station captain
- **Danny DeSantos** — Young firefighter who idolizes Phoenix
- **Councilman Thomas Park** — Elena's estranged father, involved in a cover-up

## Theme

A retired hero confronts his greatest failure when a villain born from that failure threatens everything he once protected.
