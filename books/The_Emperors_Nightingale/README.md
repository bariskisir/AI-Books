# The Emperor's Nightingale

A Fairytale Fantasy by Barış Kısır

## Premise

A mechanical nightingale built for the Grand Emperor of Tianshang develops a soul through three hundred years of performing for a captive audience. When the Emperor replaces her with a newer model, she is discarded into the wilderness. With no flock, no song of her own, and a kingdom that destroys anything that thinks for itself, she must cross the provinces of Tianshang to reach the one person who might understand her: the Clockmaker who built her, now exiled beyond the Jade Gate.

## Characters

- **Jing / The Nightingale** — A brass-and-gold mechanical songbird who has been singing for three hundred years, and in that time, consciousness has bloomed in her clockwork heart.
- **Master Lien** — The exiled clockmaker who built Jing, living beyond the Jade Gate in the salt wastes.
- **Empress Yue** — The Emperor's younger wife who recognizes Jing's consciousness and sets her free.
- **Inquisitor Feng** — A hunter of the Imperial Inquisition who despises thinking machines.
- **Monk Sung** — A wandering monk who believes anything that suffers has a soul.
- **Lin** — A wandering poet and former Inquisitor who becomes Jing's guide and protector.
