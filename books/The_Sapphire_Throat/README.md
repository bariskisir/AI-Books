# The Sapphire Throat

A Jazz Age Crime novel set in Prohibition-era Chicago.

## Premise

Chicago, 1926. Della Monroe sings the blues at The Sapphire Lounge, a glittering speakeasy in the South Loop. When she accidentally witnesses her manager Sal Capri murder a rival bootlegger, she becomes the only witness — and the most wanted woman in Chicago. Detective Michael Malone wants her testimony. Sal wants her silence. And in Prohibition Chicago, everyone wears a mask.

## Characters

- **Della Monroe** — Blues singer. Witness to murder. Determined to survive.
- **Sal Capri** — Manager of The Sapphire Lounge. Bootlegging kingpin. Charming and ruthless.
- **Detective Michael Malone** — Chicago PD. The only honest cop in a corrupt precinct.
- **Lena Cross** — Piano player. Della's closest friend.
- **Corinne Webb** — Second singer. Jealous. Dangerous.
- **Mamie Rose** — Former blues singer. Safe house keeper.
- **Frankie DeMarco** — Sal's enforcer.
- **Alderman P.J. Mulroney** — Corrupt politician.
