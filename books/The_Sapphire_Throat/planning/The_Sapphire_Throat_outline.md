# The Sapphire Throat — Outline

## Premise

Chicago, 1926. Della Monroe sings the blues at The Sapphire Lounge, a glittering speakeasy in the South Loop. Her voice draws crowds, her beauty draws admirers, and her manager Sal Capri draws a cut of every dollar she earns. When she accidentally witnesses Sal murder a rival bootlegger in the alley behind the club, she becomes the only witness — and the most wanted woman in Chicago. Detective Michael Malone of the Chicago PD wants her testimony. Sal wants her silence. And the line between lawman and gangster blurs as Della discovers that in Prohibition Chicago, everyone wears a mask.

## Core Cast

- **Della Monroe** — 24, blues singer. Raised in Memphis, came to Chicago chasing a recording contract. Sharp, observant, but still naive about how deep the corruption runs.
- **Salvatore "Sal" Capri** — 42, manager of The Sapphire Lounge. Immigrant from Sicily, built his bootlegging empire from nothing. Charming to his patrons, merciless to his enemies.
- **Detective Michael Malone** — 38, Chicago PD, Sixth Precinct. Irish-American, former boxer. He has been trying to pin something on Sal for years. Della's testimony is his best shot — or is it?
- **Lena Cross** — 30, piano player at The Sapphire Lounge. Della's closest friend. Hard-boiled on the outside, loyal underneath.
- **Frankie "Two-Gun" DeMarco** — 35, Sal's enforcer. Does the dirty work. Silent, watchful.
- **Corinne Webb** — 28, second singer at the club. Jealous of Della. Will sell anyone for a step up.
- **Alderman P.J. Mulroney** — 55, corrupt politician in Sal's pocket. Controls the cops who take bribes.

## 50 Chapter Plan

### Act I: The Songbird's Cage (Chapters 1-15)

1. **The Sapphire Lounge** — Della finishes her last set. The crowd loves her. Sal watches from the shadows.
2. **The Manager's Smile** — Sal takes Della to dinner. He is charming, generous. She trusts him.
3. **Midnight Rehearsal** — Della stays late to work on a new song. She hears voices in Sal's office.
4. **The Argument** — Two men argue behind a closed door. Della hears a name: Mulroney.
5. **Alley After Dark** — Della steps out for air. She sees Sal and Frankie with a man on his knees.
6. **The Shot** — A single gunshot. The man falls. Sal turns — and sees Della in the doorway.
7. **The Promise** — Sal swears it was self-defense. He begs Della to keep quiet. She agrees.
8. **Detective Malone** — Malone arrives at the club the next day. He asks questions. Della lies.
9. **The Piano Girl's Warning** — Lena tells Della that Sal is not who he seems. She has heard things.
10. **Corinne's Whisper** — Corinne overhears Malone's interview. She starts piecing things together.
11. **A Second Body** — A newsboy on the corner is found dead — the same night. Malone connects the dots.
12. **The Alderman's Visit** — Mulroney comes to the club. He and Sal talk behind closed doors.
13. **Della's Doubt** — Della searches Sal's office. She finds ledgers, names, payments.
14. **The Invitation** — Sal asks Della to sing at a private party. The guest list: half the Chicago underworld.
15. **The Party** — Della sees judges, cops, politicians all drinking Sal's illegal liquor. She understands now.

### Act II: The Hunt (Chapters 16-35)

16. **The Decision** — Della decides she must go to Malone. But she is scared.
17. **The Tail** — Frankie has been watching Della. She cannot shake him.
18. **The Note** — Lena slips Della a note: "They know you were in the office."
19. **The Chase** — Della runs. Through the club, through the alleys. Frankie pursues.
20. **Safe House** — Della escapes to Lena's apartment. They plan her next move.
21. **Malone's Offer** — Della reaches Malone. He offers protection in exchange for testimony.
22. **The Statement** — Della gives her statement at the precinct. But someone in the station tips off Sal.
23. **The Raid** — Sal's men hit the safe house. Della barely escapes.
24. **Underground** — Della goes into hiding in the Black Belt district. A different world.
25. **The Blues Mother** — An older blues singer named Mamie Rose takes Della in. Teaches her about survival.
26. **The Pianist's Secret** — Lena arrives with news: Corinne is working for Sal, feeding him information.
27. **The Trap** — Malone sets a sting to catch Sal's operation. Della is the bait.
28. **The Betrayal** — The sting goes wrong. Corinne tipped off Sal. Malone is suspended.
29. **Sal's Offer** — Sal sends word: drop the testimony, and Della can have her career back. Fame. Fortune.
30. **The Temptation** — Della considers it. She is tired of running. Mamie Rose talks sense into her.
31. **The Recording Contract** — A scout from Okeh Records offers Della a deal. But it is a front — Sal's doing.
32. **The Proof** — Lena finds a receipt linking Sal to the dead newsboy. It is the break they need.
33. **The Handoff** — Della and Lena plan to deliver the proof to the state's attorney. But they are watched.
34. **Midnight Train** — They try to flee Chicago. Frankie is at the station.
35. **The Shootout** — A gunfight at Union Station. Lena is wounded. Frankie escapes. Della is recaptured.

### Act III: The Reckoning (Chapters 36-50)

36. **Sal's Basement** — Della is held in the basement of Sal's warehouse. She must find a way out.
37. **The Confession** — Sal visits. He tells Della everything: how he built his empire, the murders, the bribes.
38. **The Lock** — Della picks the lock with a hairpin. She is free — but in Sal's territory.
39. **The Phones** — Malone has been reinstated. He traces Sal's calls to the warehouse.
40. **Corinne's Regret** — Corinne finds Della hiding in the club. She helps her — out of guilt.
41. **The Tunnel** — Corinne shows Della a hidden tunnel out of the club. The old speakeasy escape route.
42. **Malone's March** — Malone assembles a raid. But half the officers are on Sal's payroll.
43. **The Loyal Few** — Malone picks only men he trusts. Three officers. A piano player. A blues singer.
44. **The Warehouse** — The raid begins. Della guides Malone's men through the maze of crates.
45. **Frankie's Stand** — Frankie ambushes them. A shootout in the dark. Frankie is wounded, captured.
46. **Sal's Escape** — Sal tries to flee through the roof. Della follows him.
47. **The Rooftop** — Della and Sal face each other on the roof. He tries to bargain.
48. **The Fall** — Sal lunges at Della. She steps aside. He tumbles off the edge.
49. **The Aftermath** — The papers call Della a hero. Sal's empire crumbles. Malone gets his promotion.
50. **The Sapphire Throat** — Della sings one last time at The Sapphire Lounge. The club is under new management. She is free. She takes a train out of Chicago — a recording contract in her pocket, Memphis in her future.
