## Synopsis

When the world's primary solar geoengineering system — Project Stratus, a UN-managed network of stratospheric aerosol injection nodes spanning seven continents — is hijacked and held for ransom, climate scientist Dr. Maya Chen faces an impossible race. The blackmailer, Victor Radek, a former ICSA engineer radicalized after a climate-exacerbated cyclone destroyed his coastal hometown, demonstrates his control by redirecting atmospheric rivers away from California, triggering a crippling drought. His demand: the immediate cessation of all fossil fuel extraction within five years, with escalating climate catastrophes every two weeks until compliance. As Maya races to identify a technical solution, she uncovers a deeper conspiracy involving a hidden backdoor, a private military contractor, and a race against time to shut down the system before it can be weaponized permanently.
