## Story

Geotechnical engineer Lena Voss spent two years warning that the Alpine Nexus high-speed rail tunnel had been built through unstable geology, but was silenced by the project's powerful construction consortium. On opening day, during peak morning service, a catastrophic collapse traps 187 passengers and crew under millions of tons of rock, 400 meters underground. Inside the sealed tunnel, conductor Markus Jäger struggles to maintain order and hope among terrified passengers as oxygen runs low and water seeps in. On the surface, Lena must navigate a desperate rescue operation while powerful interests work to bury the truth.
