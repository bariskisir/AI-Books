# The Diamond Wake

**Genre:** Crime / Heist  
**Setting:** Mediterranean Sea — Monaco, Nice, Marseille, Barcelona, and international waters  
**Author:** Barış Kısır  
**Model:** deepseek-v4-flash  
**Status:** Complete (TXT + EPUB)

## Synopsis

Disgraced maritime security architect Leo Korzeniowski assembles a five-person crew to steal the Eye of the Mediterranean — a legendary 200-carat sapphire — from tech billionaire Damon Kincaid's superyacht during a gala crossing from Monaco to Sardinia. Leo designed the yacht's original security system and knows its every flaw, but Kincaid has retrofitted layers Leo never planned for, including a ruthless ex-French-naval security chief and a private investigator with a shared history with team infiltrator Maya Solis. As the crew navigates a locked-down vessel, a rogue storm, and an inside betrayal, the heist becomes less about the stone and more about who survives the wake it leaves behind.

## Characters

- **Leo Korzeniowski** — Polish former maritime security engineer, late forties, mastermind
- **Maya Solis** — Colombian-British art consultant and former intelligence officer, mid-thirties, infiltrator
- **Viktor Nazarov** — Former Russian naval engineer and safecracker, early fifties, engineer
- **Nina Okoro** — Nigerian-British offshore powerboat champion, late twenties, pilot
- **Ezra Cole** — British document forger and electronics specialist, early forties, forger
