# The Ice Below — Final Report

## Summary

The Ice Below is an Arctic Thriller set at McMurdo Station, Antarctica, during the winter darkness. Dr. Lena Shaw, a glaciologist, uncovers an ancient organism in a deep ice core. As crew members begin dying, Station Chief Vogt imposes a brutal lockdown. Dr. Akiyama traces the deaths to a subglacial lake sealed for millions of years. Shaw descends into the ice, makes contact with the organism — a collective intelligence — and must decide whether to destroy it or let it live.
