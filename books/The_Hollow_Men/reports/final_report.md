# The Hollow Men — Final Report

## Summary

The Hollow Men is a supernatural thriller set in the remote Appalachian town of Crag's Hollow. Sheriff Eli Collier discovers that hikers and townspeople who vanish into the woods are not dying — they are being replaced by an entity called the Hollow Man, born from the trapped souls of thirty-seven miners who died in a cave-in a century ago.

The novel follows Collier, Deputy Lena Hayes, and Rebecca Tolliver as they uncover the entity's origin, confront the possessed townspeople, and ultimately destroy the Hollow Man by collapsing the mine shaft that anchors it to the world. Sixteen people die. The survivors rebuild. But the dark beneath the mountain may not be empty.
