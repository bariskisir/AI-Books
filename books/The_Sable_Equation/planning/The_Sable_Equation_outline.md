# The Sable Equation - Outline

## Premise

Actuary Dr. Iris Vale audits a life-insurance portfolio after a quiet burial society reports impossible loss ratios. The insurer blames demographic drift, but Iris finds a predictive model tuned to price policies cheaply in one neighborhood while quietly betting against them through reinsurance. Mortality tables, address clustering, altered medical codes, and a missing model-validation memo point to a scheme that turned statistical bias into profit.

## Core Cast

- **Dr. Iris Vale** - Actuary and model auditor, precise and unwilling to let equations hide intent.
- **Mara Senn** - Burial society treasurer who brings the first complaint.
- **Jonas Quill** - Former underwriter who knows the insurer's old manual rating rules.
- **Calder Rusk** - Executive at Sable Mutual, smooth and fluent in model language.
- **Talia Reed** - Data scientist whose validation memo was buried.
- **Bram Pike** - Reinsurance broker with a private side agreement.
- **Nadia Hesk** - Community clinic director whose patients were miscoded.
- **Olan Voss** - Regulator with limited patience and broad subpoena power.
- **Leona Marr** - Statistician helping Iris test the model.
- **Judge Elian Arda** - Administrative law judge.

## 50 Chapter Plan

1. **Loss Ratio** - Iris sees a burial society's claims exceed every table.
2. **Sable Mutual** - Calder offers demographic drift as explanation.
3. **Address Cluster** - Leona maps losses by street and policy price.
4. **Old Manual** - Jonas shows pre-model underwriting was safer and fairer.
5. **Clinic Codes** - Nadia finds medical codes changed after intake.
6. **Reinsurance Layer** - Bram's side layer profits from concentrated deaths.
7. **Validation Memo** - Talia mentions a memo that warned of proxy bias.
8. **First Notice** - Olan opens a model conduct review.
9. **Cheap Premiums** - Mara explains why residents trusted the policies.
10. **Model Room** - Iris audits features masked as neutral variables.
11. **Postal Proxy** - Leona proves zip codes acted as risk selection.
12. **Missing Attachments** - Talia's validation attachments are gone.
13. **Bram's Fee** - Broker fees rise when losses rise.
14. **Calder's Defense** - Calder calls the model objective.
15. **Policy Lapse** - Lapse rules hide claim exposure.
16. **Rate Filing** - Olan finds the model filed under an older name.
17. **Clinic Ledger** - Nadia's ledger contradicts insurer health data.
18. **Street Table** - Iris builds a clean mortality table.
19. **Side Letter** - Jonas finds a reinsurance side letter.
20. **Hearing Set** - The judge orders model preservation.
21. **Feature Importance** - Leona shows address rank drives pricing.
22. **Red Team** - Talia's red-team tests predicted discrimination.
23. **Burial Meeting** - Policyholders testify to misleading sales.
24. **Reserve Gap** - Iris finds reserves were set too low.
25. **Calder Threatens** - Sable threatens trade-secret claims.
26. **Data Freeze** - Olan freezes model changes.
27. **Bram Cornered** - Bram admits the side layer targeted the cluster.
28. **Memo Restored** - Talia recovers the validation memo.
29. **Equation Board** - Iris explains the scheme in simple equations.
30. **Regulator's Table** - Olan issues preliminary findings.
31. **Underwriting Ghost** - Old manual rules were used secretly to screen elsewhere.
32. **Sales Script** - Mara produces the cheap-policy sales script.
33. **Proxy Trial** - The judge tests whether neutral variables can discriminate.
34. **Reserve Hearing** - Iris proves Sable booked profit before expected claims.
35. **Calder's Email** - An email calls the neighborhood "reinsurable heat."
36. **Model Witness** - Talia testifies against her former team.
37. **Settlement Offer** - Sable offers refunds without admission.
38. **Community Refusal** - The society rejects silence.
39. **Order to Correct** - The judge orders rates, reserves, and claims review.
40. **Broker Sanction** - Bram loses his license.
41. **Sable Board** - Calder is removed from model governance.
42. **Clean Filing** - Iris supervises a transparent model filing.
43. **Clinic Repair** - Nadia corrects medical codes.
44. **Burial Fund** - The society receives restitution.
45. **Leona's Paper** - Leona writes the bias analysis for public use.
46. **Jonas Returns** - Jonas trains underwriters on lawful review.
47. **Olan's Rule** - The regulator writes a proxy-audit rule.
48. **Mara's List** - Mara closes the list of unpaid claims.
49. **Quiet Table** - Iris checks the corrected loss ratio.
50. **The Sable Equation** - The final equation accounts for people instead of hiding them.
