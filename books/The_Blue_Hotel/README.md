# The Blue Hotel

**A Suspense Western**

## About

During the worst blizzard to hit the Nevada territory in thirty years, four strangers are stranded at the Blue Hotel, a faded way station on the old stage road. As the snow piles against the doors and the fire burns low, each guest begins to suspect the others. One of them is the outlaw Cyrus Vane. By morning, not everyone will survive.

## Characters

- **Elias Cole** — A drifter running from a past he cannot outride
- **Margaret "Maggie" Caldwell** — A woman traveling under a false name
- **Marshal Duncan Reed** — A lawman with a story that does not hold
- **Owen Pavey** — The brooding hotelier with locked doors in his cellar
- **Marta Pavey** — Owen's wife, who has secrets of her own
