## Chapter Structure

The book is divided into five parts:

1. **Part I: First Doors (Chapters 1-10)** — Early explorations, establishing the rules of the street
2. **Part II: Love and Its Shadows (Chapters 11-20)** — Relationships, marriage, children, loss
3. **Part III: Ambition and Art (Chapters 21-30)** — Career paths, creative lives, purpose
4. **Part IV: The Price of Choice (Chapters 31-40)** — Darker outcomes, consequences, resilience
5. **Part V: The Street Itself (Chapters 41-50)** — Return to the present, integration, final choosing

## Key Characters

- **Elena** — A translator in her early thirties who walks through doors into alternate lives
- **The Map Seller** — An old man who built the street and sells maps of impossible places
- **The Man at the Cafe** — A quiet witness who appears in every life; the last door
