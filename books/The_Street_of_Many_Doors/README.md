# The Street of Many Doors

**Author:** Barış Kısır
**Genre:** Sliding Doors
**Language:** English
**Status:** Complete

## Overview

Elena walks the same cobblestone street in Prague every day for a year. Each day she pauses at a different door — a blue door, a green door, a door with a brass lion's head — and when she opens it she steps into a different version of her life. She is a pianist in one, a widow in another, a mother of three in a third. Some lives last minutes. Some last decades. All feel as real as the one she left behind.

Through this labyrinth of possibility, guided by a mysterious Map Seller and watched by a silent Man at a Cafe, Elena must discover if there is a true self beneath the choices, or if every self is just a door we walked through.
