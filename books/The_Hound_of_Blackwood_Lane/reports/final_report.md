# The Hound of Blackwood Lane — Final Report

## Summary

The Hound of Blackwood Lane is a cozy mystery novel by Barış Kısır. The story follows Mina Hart, a retired librarian turned bookshop owner, as she investigates the disappearance of a rare sixteenth-century Portolan chart from the village's annual fete. The investigation uncovers a web of rival collectors, old grudges, and a decades-old fire at Hound Manor that some villagers would rather forget.
