# The Astronomer's Daughter

A Victorian Science novel by Barış Kısır

## Premise

In 1889 London, Catherine Whitfield secretly completes her dead father's astronomical calculations and discovers a comet on a collision course with Earth. Dismissed by the Royal Society, she must navigate the rigid hierarchies of Victorian science to deliver her warning before time runs out.

## Characters

- **Catherine Whitfield** — A 24-year-old self-taught astronomer who inherits her father's unfinished work
- **Professor Ambrose Huxley** — The Astronomer Royal, her father's old rival who suppressed the warning
- **Thomas Greaves** — A young fellow of the Royal Society who becomes Catherine's ally
- **Margaret Heston** — Catherine's loyal cousin
- **Lord Balfour** — Newspaper magnate who publishes Catherine's findings

## Structure

- 50 chapters across four acts
- Act I: The Inheritance (Chapters 1–12)
- Act II: The Discovery (Chapters 13–30)
- Act III: The Warning (Chapters 31–42)
- Act IV: The Reckoning (Chapters 43–50)
