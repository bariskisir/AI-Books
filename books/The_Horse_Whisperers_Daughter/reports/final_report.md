## Story

Ellie McAllister must train a damaged thoroughbred colt named Midnight to win the Kentucky Derby and save her family's farm, Shadow Hill. With her father's health failing, a rival trainer circling, and a hidden injury threatening the horse's career, Ellie must learn to trust herself as much as she trusts the horses she trains.
