## Summary

A complete novel of 50 chapters and approximately 12,300 words. The story follows Nova, a teenage maintenance technician aboard the generation ship *Promise of Morning*, who discovers the mission to Proxima Centauri is a lie and Earth is still alive. With the help of the ship's AI (The Archivist), she exposes Captain Ishida's conspiracy, wakes the sleeping passengers, and guides the ship toward a rendezvous with Earth's rescue vessel.
