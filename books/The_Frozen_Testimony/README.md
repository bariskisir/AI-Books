# The Frozen Testimony

## About

**Genre:** Detective Noir / Cold Case Mystery

**Model:** deepseek-v4-flash

When construction workers unearth a skeleton beneath a condemned warehouse on Portland's frozen waterfront, cold case detective Sarah Kincaid identifies the remains as Caleb Foster, a teenager who vanished on New Year's Eve 1999. The original case was closed as a runaway, but the condition of the bones tells a different story. As Sarah reopens the investigation, she discovers the warehouse was owned by a shell company linked to the victim's family real estate firm, and the original investigation was systematically compromised. The deeper she digs, the more she uncovers a twenty-five-year conspiracy involving insurance arson, corrupt city officials, and a killer who has lived openly in the victim's shadow.
