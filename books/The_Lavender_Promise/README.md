## Synopsis

After a decade climbing the corporate ladder as a high-end event planner in London, Nora Bellamy is burned out and unmoored when her beloved grandmother passes away, leaving her Ivy Cottage in the picture-perfect Cotswold village of Thornwick. Reluctantly at first, Nora arrives to sort through the estate and instead finds herself falling in love with the overgrown herb garden, her grandmother's handwritten journal of remedies, and a community that wraps around her like a warm blanket. But when a property developer threatens to bulldoze the historic market square, Nora must decide whether she has the courage to stay and fight for her new home.
